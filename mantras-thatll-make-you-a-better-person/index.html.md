# 17 Priceless Mantras Thatll Make You a Better Person ...

The right words can change your life. That's why you should set out to read as many different quotes as possible. You never know when one of them will make you feel differently about the world or about yourself. Here are a few mantras that will make you a better person:

## 1. You Become What You Believe

![You Become What You Believe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r2/ns/nok7z8pn5617c3c3ec9c5614066039_600x600.jpg)

There's no one wiser than Oprah, so make sure you take these words to heart. Stop setting little "realistic" goals and start creating big ones.

## 2. Live a Life without Escape

![Live a Life without Escape](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z0/os/q7aex17j5617c3c4c523c812237291_600x600.jpg)

You shouldn't always be looking forward to summer vacation. Then you're only enjoying a few months out of twelve. You should set up your life in a way that allows you to enjoy every season.

## 3. Don't Let Your Emotions Control You

![Don't Let Your Emotions Control You](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yy/wy/e9unh67v5617c3c406484360401531_500x671.jpg)

Your emotions can impact your words. That's why you should stop yourself from making promises when you're in a good mood and making decisions when you're in a bad mood.

## 4. Don't Be like the Rest

![Don't Be like the Rest](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ke/cj/j73tcfo15617c3c4529f6284099123_500x568.jpg)

You're the only "you" that exists, so don't try to be like the rest of the world.

## 5. Wake up and Kick Ass

![Wake up and Kick Ass](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ll/vw/gbb9tyxd5617c3c4e3867912987451_500x497.jpg)

Every single day is filled with opportunities. Never let them go to waste.

## 6. Imagine, Believe, and Achieve

![Imagine, Believe, and Achieve](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i9/x3/jozfl2m85617c3c4bc128885556802_428x579.jpg)

Use all of the power you have to imagine, believe, and achieve. You're more capable than you give yourself credit for.

## 7. Go Someplace You've Never Been

![Go Someplace You've Never Been](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xs/m1/sfa5hrd75617c3c55891a231357696_450x584.jpg)

Life is meant to be lived. That's why you should take time out every year to go on a new adventure.

## 8. Don't Love What Can't Love You

![Don't Love What Can't Love You](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j8/mt/k3g2h8ps5617c3c5e6428320018326_500x600.jpg)

You deserve to be loved. That's why you should only give your love to the people who will return it.

## 9. Take Every Risk You Can

![Take Every Risk You Can](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fv/w3/u42c0gmd5617c3c6bccdd350700458_600x600.jpg)

You'll never live your dream life without taking risks, so be brave.

## 10. Only Three Things Matter in Life

![Only Three Things Matter in Life](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nd/ke/g2j6pgdp5617c43fb8e1f898574640_600x434.jpg)

Stop focusing on material items and wealth, because those things aren't important.

## 11. You Are the Sky

![You Are the Sky](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e5/ba/kmv2tqfg5617c3c6b5be4528554266_600x600.jpg)

Don't let your cloudy days upset you, because you're as beautiful and as meaningful as the sky.

## 12. Do What's Good for the Soul

![Do What's Good for the Soul](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ic/vx/img3o2mg5617c3c6cf088034370814_550x571.jpg)

It's not that hard to find happiness. Just do whatever is good for your soul.

## 13. Live in the Moment

![Live in the Moment](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eb/n9/p2u7jrkd5617c3c742b58594749767_500x571.jpg)

Stop worrying about your past and prepping for your future. Just live in the moment and you'll have an easier time finding true happiness.

## 14. Put on Lipgloss

![Put on Lipgloss](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gq/um/xhjoba1g5617c3c90ff48479765134_580x538.jpg)

There are plenty of things we have to do that we don't want to do. That's when you need to dress yourself up and pretend you're happy to be doing whatever it is you're doing.

## 15. Keep Making Progress

![Keep Making Progress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sx/qd/xdsxd17b5617c3c87ae99212014105_600x600.jpg)

It's impossible to reach perfection. That's why you should always strive for progress.

## 16. You Are Enough

![You Are Enough](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mv/bd/puuj2hgl5617c3c82bf1a771894883_600x600.jpg)

Promise yourself that you'll stop tearing yourself down. Why? Because you are good enough, just the way you are.

## 17. Life is a Balance

![Life is a Balance](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ij/fs/oj91x7dt5617c3c8e2ea9320258468_600x600.jpg)

You need to decide when to hold on to certain people, places, and things, and when to let go of them. After all, some things aren't meant to last forever.

These words should inspire you to be the best person you can be! What other mantras have you heard that have helped you become a better person?
