# 7 Challenging Scorpio Traits and How to Overcome Them ...

The horoscope sign of Scorpio is a very interesting one. As a Scorpio, you have many wonderful traits in your personality. However, you also probably deal with a few challenging ones. While you probably won’t have all of them, these’re some common challenging Scorpio traits that you may have in your personality and some helpful ways to deal with them.

## 1. Jealousy

Jealousy is something that Scorpios struggle with. This can show up in a couple different ways. It could be that you feel jealous in a romantic relationship. In that situation, you can evaluate your relationship and decide if there’s a valid reason to feel that way. If you know that your relationship is strong then reassure yourself there’s no need for jealousy. You may also feel jealous of things that others have in their lives. Working at being happy for them is the key to overcoming this sort of jealousy.

## 2. Being Suspicious of Others

Scorpios aren’t naturally trusting creatures. That’s not a completely bad thing. Being too trusting can also earn you a lot of heartache. The important thing here is to be careful to not become too skeptical. You do need to trust others but do so cautiously.

## 3. Holding a Grudge

Holding a grudge doesn’t hurt the other person as much as you wish it would. It hurts you. Scorpios can be very determined to hold grudges but it’s worth it to reevaluate the decision to do so. Learn your lessons from bad experiences but don’t let them scar you. Forgiveness is the path to living your best life.

## 4. Being a Bit Pushy

As a Scorpio you know what you want and that’s a good thing. Where it runs into trouble is if you become a bit pushy in getting your way. You want to work on being assertive versus aggressive. Being assertive says that you know what you want and you aren’t going to back down. Being aggressive says that you’re determined to have your way regardless of the feelings of others.

## 5. Being Stubborn

You know, I’ve always believed that being stubborn isn’t a totally bad thing. What’s bad is if you’re stubborn for the wrong reasons. The key here is to think over your decisions and determine if you’re digging your heels in because you want your way or because you truly have a deep belief you’re making the right decision. Staying in tune with your heart is important here. It can give you the best guidance.

## 6. Tending toward Revenge

Revenge is a temptation to all of us at times but Scorpios struggle with it a little more. If you know this’s a trait that’s a challenge for you then remember this. Karma will win in the end. It always does. Trust in the fact that the universe knows what it’s doing and can handle the bad things that’ve happened to you.

## 7. Keeping Too Many Secrets

Everyone is entitled to their privacy. But if you’re too secretive then you can end up pushing others away. Consider letting those that’re closest to you in on the things you’re keeping quiet. This can help strengthen your relationships. This may take some time for you to work on but that’s okay.

These’re 7 challenging traits that Scorpios may struggle with. Which of these are true for you? I’d love to read your comments!
