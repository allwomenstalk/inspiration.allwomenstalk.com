# 17 Quotes to Help Stressed Girls Stop Caring so Much ...

You can't let stress take over your life. You have to take a deep breath, sit back, and relax. Of course, that can be hard, so here are a few quotes that'll help stressed out girls stop caring:

## 1. Stressed is Desserts Spelled Backwards

Chocolate is the answer to all of life's problems. As long as you don't go overboard, there's nothing wrong with having a little dessert after a long hard day.

## 2. Don't Take Away Today's Peace

You shouldn't freak yourself out by thinking of all the things that could go wrong tomorrow. Just enjoy today while you can.

## 3. Get a Good Night's Sleep

If you want to reduce your stress, then set aside enough time for you to sleep. Eight hours is recommended, you know.

## 4. Manage Stress to Achieve Success

Unfortunately, stress is a part of life. You need to learn how to deal with it if you want to make something of yourself.

## 5. You're Not Being Dramatic

Don't feel bad if you freak out when you're under a lot of stress. Your miniature meltdowns aren't as bad as an octopus' are.

## 6. Stress Doesn't Go with Your Outfit

Don't be stressed. It doesn't look good with what you're wearing, darling.

## 7. Ask for a Hug

Sometimes, the stress will disappear after some alcohol. But most of the time, all you need is a good long hug from someone you love.

## 8. Sex is a Stress Reliever

If you have a boyfriend, he certainly won't mind helping you reduce your stress. All he has to do is make you orgasm.

## 9. Stress is Caused by Being "here"

If you don't like where you are in life, then work to change your position. It'll be hard, but it's definitely possible.

## 10. Work Hard for Something You Love

You've heard that if you do something you love, you'll never work a day in your life, right? Well, that's true. The more you love your career, the less stressed you'll be.

## 11. Do Your Best

As long as you do your best, then you shouldn't be ashamed. It's all you can do.

## 12. Don't Give a F

Stop caring about what you look like, how much money you make, and what other people think of you. The less you care, the happier you'll be.

## 13. Don't Stress over Stuff You Can't Control

If you can't do anything about a certain situation, then you shouldn't stress over it. It's pointless.

## 14. Don't Think Too Much

You don't want to think too much. Overanalyzing could be the death of you.

## 15. Don't Stress Ahead of Time

Don't get so used to stress that it becomes a part of you. At least wait until there's actually something to stress about before you start stressing.

## 16. You're Blessed

Don't focus on the things you want, but aren't sure you'll ever have. Focus on what you already have.

## 17. Relax

Never forget this. Relaxing is always an option.

The next time you're super stressed, read through these quotes to calm down. Do you stress out easily?
