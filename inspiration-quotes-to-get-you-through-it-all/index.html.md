# 10 Inspiration Quotes to Get You through It All ...

Sometimes we just need that pick-me-up inspiration to tell us we're BA and we can kick today! Here's yours below.

## #1

![text, advertising, poster, font, flyer,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/up/p3/h993nltw594a8d5f8ce3f242132770_556x825.jpg)

This quote reminds us to be grateful no matter what you're going through because that's what makes you brave and strong . Through strive is how you find strength and courage.

## #2

![flora, branch, botany, flower, art,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vh/xk/v19obl71594a8d99480d6036155202_450x675.jpg)

Remember that confidence is key! How you handle yourself is how others will perceive you, so you decide your worth not others.

## #3

![footwear, shoe, leg,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pw/nf/l14xb0hf594a8da886bde321797442_425x480.jpg)

You're capable of anything, sometimes it just takes time for you to realize it. Don't be afraid, explore the unknown.

## #4

![blue, poster, art, writing, illustration,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jd/ai/kzfka73z594a8db5077fe474148382_500x500.jpg)

One of my favorite sayings is to find faith in life, whatever that may mean to you. Learn to let go and trust, but above all always keep that child-like spirit! Trust the unknown and believe in the unseen.

## #5

![text, font, biology, love, emotion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vy/id/ii2ika3z594a8dc39f936962716962_504x504.jpg)

In a world full of corruption and hate, be the light, be the reason someone smiles today. Remember that love wins and kindness will always prevail. So be sweet and soft, don't let anyone deprive you of that vulnerability because that is true strength.

## #6

![text, font, line, product, brand,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dy/e3/km52empj594a8dd2eff97471908073_600x468.jpg)

You are beautiful, smart and important. There will never be anything impossible if you believe in yourself, like Audrey Hepburn once said, even the word itself says I'm possible. Remember that we can do all things through Christ who gives us strength.

## #7

![sky, text, atmosphere, font, morning,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p0/is/gc2epiiq594a8df51d97d237543638_600x516.jpg)

If you live your life comparing yourself to celebrities, friends etc. you are setting yourself up for extreme feelings of envy and self loathing. We are all created unique individuals, with our own talents, quirks, personality, and that's what makes you special, so there's no need to be like anyone else in order to shine bright.

## #8

![text, font, biology, line, brand,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ds/h2/lqmlbkix594a8e04ddb29718188373_468x464.jpg)

Don't place your life in the hands of others. You do what makes you happy because happiness is the most important thing in life in my opinion. Don't let anyone decide what they think will be best for you because that is a path to destruction and unhappiness. Follow your calling, don't let anyone stop you.

## #9

![text, font, biology, presentation,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zo/c1/udt3dukh594a8e3eafb9e718288558_480x350.jpg)

This quote is so powerful that it really doesn't need much explanation, all I have to say is don't be afraid to love but guard your heart because not everyone has good intentions. Continue to be kind, giving, trustworthy, but don't let anyone use you or take you for granted. You have a voice, so speak up for those who can't!

## #10

![pink, flower, flora, branch, season,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uj/yg/kp55ka4z594a8ea5c10e8853691043_523x523.jpg)

Sometimes we're so disconnected from each other that we forget that one small act of kindness can make someone's day better. Just by smiling at someone you can change their life. You never know what storms that person is facing, and a smile might be just what they needed to keep them going. To know that someone cares about you can be a life saver, literally. So smile at everyone, you might get a few strange stares, but at the end of the day you have nothing to lose.
