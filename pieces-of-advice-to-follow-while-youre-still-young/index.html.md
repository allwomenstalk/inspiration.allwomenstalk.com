# 18 Pieces of Advice All Young People Should Follow ...

When you are young, most of the time you are just trying to push forward, to get more mature, and you forget to live. Really, really live. If you are anything like me, you want time to slow down when you get older.

## 1. Invest in Quality Clothing

![Invest in Quality Clothing](https://img.allw.mn/content/mc/cv/u5x40rg55737c014a6681084794922_500x281.gif)

Trust me, they never, ever go out of style.

## 2. Fall in Love at Least Once

![Fall in Love at Least Once](https://img.allw.mn/content/ol/wt/tzqsm7h95737c025b1fbf989916821_500x281.gif)

Don't close yourself off! Love is a beautiful thing.

## 3. Know That Nobody else Controls You

![Know That Nobody else Controls You](https://img.allw.mn/content/xo/ri/im8jp3h75737c03a0a0a3718742191_500x281.gif)

And if they try, you need a new friend, boyfriend, whoever. Nobody should ever control you.

## 4. If You Feel Hatred, Keep It to Yourself

![If You Feel Hatred, Keep It to Yourself](https://img.allw.mn/content/jl/i7/exq5kr985737c0575c599527300630_500x320.gif)

You should never actively try to hurt anybody else.

## 5. Forgive as Much as You Can

![Forgive as Much as You Can](https://img.allw.mn/content/o1/om/hwtmk2le5737c06b75052230981845_500x281.gif)

Don't forget, just forgive.

## 6. Keep Going and Going, Never Give up

![Keep Going and Going, Never Give up](https://img.allw.mn/content/wn/i7/co1pmo285737c07ae3892754518567_500x255.gif)

It will help you in all walks of life.

## 7. Embrace High School

![Embrace High School](https://img.allw.mn/content/z7/o1/v1ej2sn25737c08cb106e438359907_500x281.gif)

Sure, you aren't going to peak there, but it does have its place in your life cycle.

## 8. Be Nice to Humans

![Be Nice to Humans](https://img.allw.mn/content/ar/cs/hwl918ip5737c0ae57f3c285883067_500x266.gif)

Seriously. Don't make jokes behind people's back, give back to the community, be nice.

## 9. Help Animals

![Help Animals](https://img.allw.mn/content/td/kx/ud6bbkyv5737c0d755360569116980_500x286.gif)

In every form. Whether it is volunteering at a shelter or just adopting a pup from a shelter. Be nice to animals, always.

## 10. Find out What Commands You

![Find out What Commands You](https://img.allw.mn/content/pa/ft/ueuq0pqj5737c11a844da213198356_498x280.gif)

What is at the root of your entire being? Find it and indulge yourself.

## 11. Be Active

![Be Active](https://img.allw.mn/content/yt/jp/thn41ne35737c1421c1e0076701894_440x330.gif)

Always move. Exercise – whatever form it takes - and keep moving.

## 12. Find a Role Model

![Find a Role Model](https://img.allw.mn/content/a3/la/pqu6gt0g5737c156112e0946049895_500x248.gif)

Or a mentor and aim to achieve even more than they could ever, ever do. It'll push you forward.

## 13. Don't Go Crazy about Material Items

![Don't Go Crazy about Material Items](https://img.allw.mn/content/q7/ru/kge6d67o5737c181e5c0e393439845_500x252.gif)

Sure, when you are young you want to buy the newest of everything but calm down. Eventually, you want something deeper.

## 14. Have Trust and Confidence in Yourself

![Have Trust and Confidence in Yourself](https://img.allw.mn/content/we/xv/nostkeq65737c194635b7243856350_500x270.gif)

Always. This is huge and will carry you throughout your life.

## 15. Don't Worry, Just Try to Live

![Don't Worry, Just Try to Live](https://img.allw.mn/content/ot/he/urx3q6xw5737c1a804b02702926989_500x333.gif)

I love this. I need this more in my life.

## 16. Do You

![Do You](https://img.allw.mn/content/c7/rj/m6mpezto5737c1be46145235166557_500x278.gif)

Despite the judgment of others, just do you.

## 17. Hang onto Deep Relationships

![Hang onto Deep Relationships](https://img.allw.mn/content/yj/wa/pt0zb3qp5737c1deb1422822629185_500x283.gif)

Always, always hang onto them.

## 18. Remember, Age is Not a Disease

![Remember, Age is Not a Disease](https://img.allw.mn/content/jy/hn/av77op3k5737c21826d64399402702_500x376.gif)

We spend so much time trying to stay young. Keep in mind, age is not a disease.
