# 17 Things You Should Make More Time for in Life ...

I have two jobs, a wife, five animals and no time for anything it seems like. Are you like me? It gets old REALLY quick and I promise that you'll be burnt out in no time. So girls, take some time for yourself! Take some time for some of the things below – TAKE IT, your brain will need it.

## 1. Read a Lot

![Read a Lot](https://img.allw.mn/content/wa/km/of7y7wrc56dc8b73ce800391883139_499x344.gif)

Seriously, this is the best investment of time for you!

## 2. Pay Attention to Your Body

![Pay Attention to Your Body](https://img.allw.mn/content/e2/vw/u3u2irue56dc8b7426208630175226_500x250.gif)

Pay attention to your mental health, your body and how you feel!

## 3. Witness a Sunset over the Water

![Witness a Sunset over the Water](https://img.allw.mn/content/dq/ax/a9lzv8j756dc8b758c96f352276094_500x317.gif)

It's a miracle.

## 4. Witness a Sunrise over the Water

![Witness a Sunrise over the Water](https://img.allw.mn/content/m6/sq/oueluyzs56dc8b7682d42717172639_500x269.gif)

So is this one.

## 5. Indulge in a Passion You Didn't Know about

![Indulge in a Passion You Didn't Know about](https://img.allw.mn/content/g3/t8/gltd6wsu56dc8b77bdac6212563278_500x336.gif)

Mine is photography, what's yours? Do you know? Find out!

## 6. Spread Some Joy

![Spread Some Joy](https://img.allw.mn/content/tf/b0/a38fwf5r56dc8b78c60bb632194128_500x281.gif)

Spread some joy to anyone; even a simple smile can brighten up a day.

## 7. Travel a Lot

![Travel a Lot](https://img.allw.mn/content/i7/bu/q4d7mfsh56dc8b798a21f592788845_500x280.gif)

You should try to see as many places as possible.

## 8. Make a Wish List

![Make a Wish List](https://img.allw.mn/content/eu/s0/tr93gh6j56dc8b7b4d11e716142520_500x269.gif)

It can include anything!

## 9. Enjoy Nature

![Enjoy Nature](https://img.allw.mn/content/e4/ee/t1tcr8uj56dc8b7b778f4447127292_488x275.gif)

Get out and get some fresh air.

## 10. Spend Some Time at a Shelter

![Spend Some Time at a Shelter](https://img.allw.mn/content/vx/w2/zipf3gi856dc8b7c51b6e992876525_500x255.gif)

Spend some time with the animals; they'll appreciate it!

## 11. Examine What You Really Want in Life

![Examine What You Really Want in Life](https://img.allw.mn/content/dk/uj/qudypgnx56dc8b7d7ff8e955289052_500x311.gif)

And try to figure out exactly what you want.

## 12. The Oh-so-long Conversations

![The Oh-so-long Conversations](https://img.allw.mn/content/zq/s2/gz2j5g3a56dc8b7e5959c020179374_500x272.gif)

With your mom, your BFF, whoever ... have some deep conversations!

## 13. Interact with People – Face-to-face

![Interact with People – Face-to-face](https://img.allw.mn/content/e6/fx/i5g4mufp56dc8b7fa8733864187956_500x251.gif)

PUT YOUR PHONE DOWN.

## 14. Cook

![Cook](https://img.allw.mn/content/o4/jv/a4hxeszb56dc8b8068f86066585902_500x281.gif)

It can be so soothing and relaxing.

## 15. … if You Don't Cook, Eat

![… if You Don't Cook, Eat](https://img.allw.mn/content/ux/v8/hzb3foyf56dc8b814a337477736999_475x352.gif)
