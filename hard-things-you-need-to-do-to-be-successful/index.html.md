# 19 Hard Things You Need to do to Be Successful ...

You have to make the call you're afraid to make.

You have to get up earlier than you want to get up.

You have to give more than you get in return right away.

You have to care more about others than they care about you.

You have to fight when you are already injured, bloody and sore.

You have to feel unsure and insecure when playing it safe seems smarter.

You have to lead when no one else is following you yet.

You have to invest in yourself even though no one else is.

You have to look like a fool while you're looking for answers you don't have.

You have to grind out the details when it's easier to shrug them off.

You have to deliver results when making excuses is an option.

You have to search for your explanations even when you're told to accept the "facts."

You have to make mistakes and look like an idiot.
You have to try and fail and try again.

You have to run faster even though you're out of breath.

You have to kind to people who have been cruel to you.

You have to meet deadlines that are unreasonable and deliver results that are unparalleled.

You have to be accountable for your actions even when things go wrong.

You have to keep moving forward where you want to be no matter what's in front of you.

Not many people would like to hear the truth because it hurts. Not many people are willing to go an extra mile to make things happen. It comes from the person's will to make things happen. When you want something so bad, you're going to do anything and everything to have it. It is that drive of wanting to make it, to have it, or whatever it is, that you would fight with everything you've got and failure is not an option.

Success feels and looks different in every person, everyone has their own definition to it. It takes mental physical, emotional and everything. Life happens when you get out of your comfort zone. Just like most people say, notice your pain it teach you something.

Life doesn't come with instructions, but quotes and positive words help aid a little. These hard facts are quite hard. I, myself even get defensive. And these things come from your inner will to do it, regardless.
