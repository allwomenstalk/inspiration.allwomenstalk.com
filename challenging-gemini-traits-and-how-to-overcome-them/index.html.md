# 7 Challenging Gemini Traits and How to Overcome Them ...

The Gemini horoscope sign has it’s share of unique traits. Some of them are positive and some of them are challenging. It’s always good to be aware of what the challenging traits of your horoscope sign are so that you can work on overcoming them. As with the positive traits of your horoscope sign, you’ll probably have some but not all of those listed.

## 1. Indecisiveness

Geminis can struggle with being indecisive. You have an idea of what they want but making a final decision can be difficult for you. If it’s a major decision that will involve a life change such as a job or getting married then it makes sense to take your time and decide very carefully. But with smaller decisions, try going with your first instinct. If you’re stuck deciding between two dresses, the one that appealed to you first is probably the one to go with.

## 2. Taking on More than You Can Handle

A Gemini will often take on more than they can handle. You don’t mean to; you simply try to be as helpful and productive as you can. To prevent overextending yourself, take an honest look at your schedule before you make any new commitments. Remind yourself that you can’t do as good of a job on things when you’re spread thin. Saying no sometimes is okay.

## 3. Anxious

Everyone struggles with feeling anxious sometimes, regardless of their horoscope sign. Geminis just feel this a little more easily than others. Stress can get to you quicker. Geminis keep a lot of their anxiety and turmoil inside which isn’t always a good thing. It’s good to find some coping strategies to help you deal with these anxious and stressed feelings such as journaling or yoga.

## 4. Impulsive

Geminis can be very impulsive. You may speak or act before thinking about the ramifications. This can end up in hurting the feelings of those you love the most. When you’re feeling that impulsiveness, remind yourself to slow down. You won’t regret taking time to think before you act but you may regret your impulsive actions. You can overcome this challenging trait with some practice.

## 5. Judgmental

You may find that you’re judgmental as a Gemini. You sometimes see someone and make a snap judgment about them before you know their whole story. Remind yourself that you don’t fully know someone’s story. Resolve not to make judgments so hastily. Look beyond what you see to the circumstances that may be behind them.

## 6. Being Nosy

This one isn’t fun to recognize about yourself but if you’re a Gemini then it may be true. Geminis are sometimes very nosy. They want to know the ins and outs of other people’s lives. While you can’t help having some natural curiosity, make sure you keep it in check. If not, it could lead to trouble for you.

## 7. Easily Bored

Geminis generally become bored very easily. They do well in careers that involve a lot of action such as a paramedic or law enforcement officer. Sometimes boredom will tempt a Gemini to quit something that’s really important such as college. If this sort of thing happens to you, don’t give in to that temptation. Remind yourself that sticking it out can help you reach your goals.

These’re 7 challenging traits a Gemini may have. Do you see any of them in yourself? Share your answers here!
