# 7 Inspirational Quotes from the Walking Dead ...

There are inspirational quotes from The Walking Dead, a show about survival. Television shows aren’t only meant for entertainment purposes. They can teach you a lot about life. Here are some inspirational quotes from The Walking Dead that will help you get through your bad days.

## 1. Pain Management

Whether or not you like Andrea, you have to admit that she has some great lines. One of the inspiration quotes from The Walking Dead was when she said, “The pain doesn't go away. You just make room for it." It’s guaranteed that you’re going to get hurt in life. There’s nothing you can do to avoid the pain, but you can live with it. It’s always going to be there, but you’ll eventually learn how to manage it.

## 2. Right or Wrong

Lori said, “It's so easy to do the wrong thing in this world. So, if it feels wrong don't do it.” She acknowledges the fact that people are capable of evil deeds. They can easily do the wrong thing, because it is commonly the easier road. However, you need to be strong, and do what you know is right.

## 3. Think It through

Rick once told Carl, “Don’t talk, think. That’s a good rule of thumb for life.” Many people blurt out words without thinking them through. Instead, you should make sure what you’re saying needs to be said. You wouldn’t make any important decisions without thinking them through, would you? You should use the same thought process before you speak. Words are more valuable than people treat them.

## 4. Always Angry

Michonne said, “Anger makes you stupid. Stupid gets you killed.” While it might not get you killed, it will certainly cause issues for you. You need to learn to let your anger go, or you’ll live a miserable life. You don’t want your actions to be based on your feelings of rage. In order to think rationally, and to be at peace with your life, you have to let your anger free.

## 5. Hurt and Heal

Beth had a line that was similar to Andrea‘s saying about pain. She said, “When you care about someone, hurt is kinda part of the package.” You can’t expect everything in life to be perfect. When you care about someone, they can hurt you or die. It doesn’t mean that you should isolate yourself from other humans. You just have to remember that they’ll hurt you one day, in one way or another. Of course, the pain is worth it.

## 6. Mysterious Movements

Merle’s not the best role model, but he had a line that everyone can relate to. “I don’t know why I do the things I do. Never did. I’m a damn mystery to me.” Sometimes we do things without knowing why we’re doing them. It doesn’t make the action wrong. The human mind is a complex thing that we’ll never truly understand. You don’t need to be able to justify every single thing you do.

## 7. Worthwhile Risks

Hershel said, “You step outside, you risk your life [...] Every moment now you don’t have a choice. The only thing you can choose is what you’re risking it for." We’re not in as much danger as the characters on the show, but the message about risks rings true for us. If quitting your job or moving across the country can help you achieve your dreams, the risk is worth it. You need to decide what risks you’re willing to take in order to live a life you’ll love.

If you’ve never watched The Walking Dead or read the comics, you need to start! Are you a fan of the series? Who’s your favorite character from the show?
