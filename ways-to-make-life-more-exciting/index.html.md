# 10 Completely do\-Able Ways to Make Life More Exciting ...

Do you think your life has become boring and predictable? Then it’s time to make some changes! Get off your butt and start living a more exciting upbeat life. These **ways to make life more exciting** are easy enough to do.

## 1. GET YOURSELF out THERE

It can be very easy to slip in to a comfort zone of Netflix evenings on the couch every night, but even though this can be fun once in a while, you need to balance it out with some fun out on the town! Get your best girlfriends together and hit a club! On a weeknight!

## 2. MAKE BOLD CHOICES

Don’t be shy about your everyday look. If you want to more of an instant impact on people, then a good place to start is with some more dramatic color choices for things like lipstick and eye makeup. Mix it up a little bit! Maybe you should wear red to work today?

## 3. GET STARTED on YOUR BUCKET LIST

We all have one and instead of relegating what's on yours to "someday," get started on it today. Even if that means just making plans on meeting those goals, you'll be one step closer to an exciting life.

## 4. SAY YES MORE OFTEN

Saying ‘no’ to new ideas can become almost an instant, automatic reaction when your life has reached a boring point. Inject some life in to it by forcing yourself to say yes more often and open yourself up to new experiences.

## 5. DOCUMENT YOUR LIFE

Start an Instagram page where you can post photos of all of the fun things that you get up to, in a strange way, it will spur you on to do more fun things to keep the quality of your profile high!

## 6. TAP into YOUR CHILDHOOD SELF

The 10-year old you is still in there somewhere, and every now and then it’s good to let her out and indulge in some good old childish fun to help your forget about some of the more boring parts of your life!

## 7. SIGN up for a CLASS

There are classes for every kind of leisure activity and hobby these days! Whether it’s dancing or pottery or cooking, you can find a local class and make some new friends too.

## 8. GET in FRONT of a MIC!

Unleash your inner Whitney Houston at a karaoke night or your inner Chelsea Handler at a local open mic comedy night. There is nothing like the thrill of performing in front of a crowd!

## 9. WEAR YOUR SEXIEST LINGERIE

Just because your love life might not be going exactly to plan, it doesn’t mean that you can’t wear sexy lingerie under your work clothes. You’ll be super empowered the whole day!

## 10. SWAP DEVICES for PEOPLE

Stop walking around with your eyes glued to a screen. Don’t sit on the couch texting or browsing. Get out and reconnect with the real world and real people. Meet up with friends and all turn off your phones, or go to somewhere phones aren’t allowed to be on – movies, the theater or a concert.
