# 7 Compelling Quotes by Elizabeth Taylor ...

These motivational quotes by Elizabeth Taylor have led to her being hailed as one of the greatest actresses. During her lifetime, Taylor was known for her many marriages, as well as her love affair. When solely focusing on the artistic aspect of her career, her reputation as a film star certainly exceeded her. As a humanitarian and champion for those who didn't always have a voice in the media, she was widely celebrated because of her immense talent and her unwavering advocacy for people living with HIV/AIDS. That being said, these compelling quotes by Elizabeth Taylor expose a more honest side of the beautiful actress.

## 1. I'm Me

![I'm Me](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bk/jb/o5/hmyf1ugm_600x600.jpg)

Because of the fact that she blatantly admits her wrongdoings, this is one of my favorite quotes by Elizabeth Taylor. The flaw of being human is making mistakes; I don't think that there is one person who hasn't been plagued by something that he or she is not proud of. The message that I take from this quote is that the only way to be honest with yourself is by first accepting your mistakes and then learning from them.

## 2. Mood

![Mood](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ot/e6/rf/z8v7zoxn_600x425.jpg)

If you want to become more confident, you have to change the way you think about yourself. Self-esteem can only grow if you resist allowing your mood to define you; you should be able to look in the mirror and see yourself as you truly are - A beautiful woman. Don't let your mood cloud your vision; know your worth.

## 3. Patience

![Patience](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w2/or/vx/u4q8ez76_600x480.jpg)

I wholeheartedly agree with the concept that the older you get, the more patient you become. I think that it has something to do with the wisdom gained with age; ever since I was a kid, my mother would always remind me that patience is a virtue.

## 4. Yearning for Tomorrow

![Yearning for Tomorrow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xu/og/es/k3dqct9z_600x450.jpg)

I find this quote to be extremely inspirational. Believing that the sky is the limit is such a great mindset to have for copious reasons. First, you should always broaden your horizons; forcing yourself to stay between the lines only makes you become accustomed to your comfort zone. The best way to break out of those lines is by working hard to achieve your dreams.

## 5. A Sense of Adventure

![A Sense of Adventure](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cv/nd/cj/bzuc5fud_600x389.jpg)

Don't ever let your fear of the unknown stop you from going out and exploring what the world has to offer. Having an adventurous streak can be a good thing; not only will your experiences become more diverse and cultivated, you will find out a lot more about yourself along the way.

## 6. Success

![Success](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d1/bz/vo/n6pbjgio_600x462.jpg)

The harder you try, the closer you will be to success. Once you accomplishment your goals, you'll be able to leave your thoughts of any failures behind. Don't let yourself be weighed down by any past mistakes; it's all about the journey that you allow yourself to take.

## 7. Survive

![Survive](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ew/ta/ep/tmvtc6m3_600x565.jpg)

Lastly, Elizabeth Taylor's career had been plagued with illnesses before her passing. Whether it was through sheer force or determination, she was able to beat the odds. I feel that this quote can be applied to any situation; regardless of how gloomy the outcome looks, never stop fighting for your dreams.

I do believe that there's more to her character than simply being known as a seductress. I've always been in awe of the fact that the lovely Debbie Reynolds eventually forgave Elizabeth for her huge mistake and made peace with her, making Elizabeth's final act of leaving Ms. Reynolds her beloved jewels in her will that much more heartfelt. What are some of your favorite quotes by Elizabeth Taylor?
