# 7 Ways to Be Beautiful on the inside ...

I believe we would all like to know some ways to be beautiful on the inside. Inner beauty is every bit as important as outer beauty. This is something I believe we learn more and more as we grow and mature. These are some things you can do when you are searching for ways to be beautiful on the inside.

## 1. Accept Who You Are

You are who you are. Do not waste valuable time trying to change that. So many times we get wrapped up in admiring others' personalities and lives that we forget how valuable we are in our own right. Learning to accept who you are takes maturity. It is one of the best ways to be beautiful on the inside.

## 2. Learn about Yourself

Sometimes we need to learn more about ourselves. We may be surprised when we learn some new facets of our personalities. Take some personality tests to discover parts of you that you didn’t realize were there. It can really help the pieces fit together for you in areas you may not have understood about yourself. This goes right along with accepting who you are and what your virtues are.

## 3. Practice Kindness

Being kind shows inner beauty in one of its loveliest forms. All of us should do our best to be kind to those we know and those we don’t know. It makes the world a better place to live in for everyone. You will be beautiful on the inside when you practice kindness. A person even looks lovelier when they are kind, wouldn’t you agree? On the flip side, ugly behavior can make anyone look unattractive.

## 4. Learn Gratitude

I believe that gratitude is something we have to learn and work at. At least this has been my experience with gratitude. It comes to some people more naturally than it does to others but we can all work on being grateful for our blessings. One way to make sure you are consciously working toward this each day is to write down three things about your day you are thankful for before you retire at night. It is also a wonderful record to look back over when you are feeling down.

## 5. Show That You Care about Others

We know that we care about others, but do we express that? Letting others know what they mean to you is a wonderful way to show your inner beauty. More importantly, you will feel beautiful on the inside. It is really ironic, isn’t it? Outer beauty often involves what can be done for or to us while inner beauty often involves what we can do for and feel toward others.

## 6. Do Good Deeds

I think it is amazing how good you feel about yourself when you choose to do a good deed for another. Not only did you make them happy and add a blessing in their day but you feel happy and like you have received a blessing as well. There is something else to be said about doing good deeds: it is a way of sowing karma. It will come back to you. Try this for a while and see if it doesn’t prove itself to be true.

## 7. Love Yourself

Do you truly want to be beautiful on the inside? Love yourself. When you appreciate who you are and what your values are, your inner beauty will show on the outside too. Being comfortable and happy in your own skin can’t help but show and really is quite beautiful. Think about people you know who know and love who they are, and you will realize what I am talking about.

Being beautiful on the inside is a different kind of beauty that is just as important as the beauty that can be visibly seen. What makes you feel beautiful on the inside? I would love for you to share your thoughts on this subject.
