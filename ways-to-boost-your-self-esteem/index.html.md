# Fab Ways to Boost Your Self\-Esteem for Girls Feeling down ...

Life is difficult and that's why every girl needs **ways to boost your self esteem**. It may be school. It may be your parents. It may be a guy or maybe your friends. But things seem to always have a way of not just tiring us out, but making us question ourselves.

Protect the way you feel about yourself with these easy **ways to boost your self esteem**.

## 1. Find the Issue(s)

It’s entirely possible that more than one thing in your life is getting you down and making you second guess yourself or your worth. Analyze what’s wrong in your life right now and determine causes of stress. That's truly one of the best **ways to boost your self esteem**.

## 2. Figure out Ways to Fix It

A great way to boost your self esteem is by knowing you are capable enough to solve your own problems.

## 3. List Your Good Traits

Whip out some paper and a pen and put them all down. No, I’m serious. Listing your good traits and seeing them in front of you can help you visualize positive things and make you happier in the long run.

## 4. Seek Internal Approval First

In the end, nothing matters but the approval we give ourselves. Our friends will come and go. Even our family members might not be the best [people to look up to](https://inspiration.allwomenstalk.com/inspirational-people-to-look-up-to/) all the time. But you can never go wrong trusting yourself. Because even if things go wrong, at least you can say you did it your way.

## 5. Appreciate Everything

So much of what we feel in our internal worlds has to do with how we take in our environments. You’ll feel better about yourself if you decide to take the time to acknowledge the simple things in life. You’ll see yourself as a grateful person, which will make you feel great!

## 6. Know Your Uniqueness and Flaunt It

Yup, you read it right. For once, you’re being told to flaunt something. And it’s the greatest thing of all - your uniqueness. There are intangible realities that make you who you are. Acknowledge them, respect them, love them, and yes, flaunt them.
