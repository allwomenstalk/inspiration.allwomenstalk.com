# 7 Habits of Successful Women That You Should Develop ...

If you want to live a prosperous and positive life, there are 7 habits of successful women that you should develop. Whether you strive to be the next Oprah Winfrey or the First Female President of the United States (watch out, Hillary!), these habits of successful women are vital to develop. Forget all that unhelpful, cliché advice you’ve received - these are the only habits of successful women you need to achieve your goals.

## 1. Being a Morning Person

Many opportunities in life occur on a first-come, first-served basis, so it’s important to be the early bird who gets the worm. Waking up early rather than sleeping in is one of the habits of successful women and is a great way to make sure that you’re ready to pounce on opportunities that arise. Becoming a morning person isn’t easy for everyone, but once your body adjusts to your new sleeping pattern, you’ll see that it will also make you a more productive person. Early rising is one of the habits of successful women because success comes with hard work. There are only 24 hours in a day and once you get your recommended 8 hours of sleep, the best thing to do is take advantage of the remaining 16.

## 2. Organization

Organization is key and since you don’t have an assistant to keep everything in order, it’s important that you do it yourself. Keeping an agenda, creating and using to-do lists, color-coordinating, putting things back in their place - the list goes on - are forms of organization that can be found among the habits of successful women. So if you don’t have one already, go buy a planner, organize your wardrobe, pick up the knick-knacks and de-clutter your living space. It will make your life so much easier. Instead of running around and rummaging through messes, being organized saves you from that waste of time so that you can work hard and pursue your goals.

## 3. Using Social Media to Your Advantage

Nowadays, everyone’s on the Internet and chances are, you too. The way you present yourself online is important and something your reputation depends on. Making a good first, second and third impression on your social networks is crucial to the relationships and connections you create with the people you meet on your way to success. Don’t post incriminating, unprofessional or indecent pictures of yourself. Maintaining a presentable online profile is one of the habits of successful women that everyone should develop because you never know who may stumble upon your Facebook, Twitter or Instagram pages - he or she may be someone who can help you reach your dreams. Don’t turn them off with vulgar language or risqué selfies.

## 4. Self-Promotion

Prior to your success, chances are no-one’s going to promote you. It’s up to you to get your name out there. In addition to maintaining a presentable social profile, use your social networks to sell your skills and self. Self-promotion is one of the habits of successful women because if you want something, you have to go get it. Don’t expect everything to be handed to you on a silver platter - success doesn’t come on one of those. You need to earn success, and knowing how to sell yourself and publicizing what you have to offer is a necessity.

## 5. Taking & Using Criticism

The journey to success isn’t always a pleasurable one. You’re going to endure criticism. One of the habits of successful women is being able to receive and use feedback to improve themselves. Being in denial of your faults won’t get you far - take the negative and make it positive. Remember, the first step is admitting you have a problem. The next step is working to fix it.

## 6. Life-Long Learning

The world has so much to offer, and one of the habits of successful women is being receptive to new things. Similar to taking and using criticism, growth and improvement cannot be achieved by blocking out change. Being stuck in your ways won’t get you far on your way to success. Be open to new ideas, cultures and trends, but do so without losing your integrity or sense of self.

## 7. Sincerity

Be true to who you are. Striving for success can cause some people to lose themselves on the way, but one of the habits of successful women is introspecting - examining one’s own thoughts or feelings. If you find yourself behaving in a way that you never imagined yourself behaving or becoming a person you no longer recognize - stop. Think about what’s going on around you and how it’s affecting you. If you come to the conclusion that you don’t like the person you’re becoming, find a way back to your true self. As Eleanor Roosevelt once said, "People grow through experience if they meet life honestly and courageously."

We all want to be successful in one way or another. By developing these 7 habits of successful women, the road to success will be much brighter. What other habits do you believe successful women have in common?
