# 9 Quotes Thatll Make You See Your Strength ...

We underestimate how strong we really are. However, if you stop to think about your life and all of the dark days you've made it through, you'll realize how much you're capable of. You wouldn't be at the point you're at now without strength. If you can't see that, here are a few quotes that'll make you see your strength:

## 1. Letting Go Takes Strength

![Letting Go Takes Strength](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pi/mz/ryd2ry2q558177043d998041969201_550x400.jpg)

Don't consider yourself weak for ending a relationship or quitting a job. Sometimes, it actually takes strength to walk away from something. The fact that you're following through on a decision that's right for you is strong, not weak.

## 2. Art Encourages Strength

![Art Encourages Strength](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v9/rn/qvl7nd3c558177045aa47721259904_600x600.jpg)

If you don't feel like you're strong, there are ways to change your perception of yourself. Art is one of the best methods to use. If you write, draw, or sing, the power of your creations could finally make you feel strong.

## 3. Crying Doesn't Show Weakness

![Crying Doesn't Show Weakness](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kz/yp/gxmrca60558177046420f422182054_403x403.jpg)

Don't feel like you're a failure for crying. Everyone gets emotional over certain things, and crying is a great way to release all of your sadness and frustration. The next time you cry, don't be embarrassed about it. Be proud of yourself for being strong for so long.

## 4. You'll See Your Strength Tomorrow

![You'll See Your Strength Tomorrow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s8/x0/ee276v1l55817704bce00521675619_500x344.jpg)

It hurts when you exercise for the first time in a long time, but it'll make you stronger in the long run. That's not only the case when it comes to working out. It also applies to doing your homework and dealing with a breakup. Although the things that you're doing today might be difficult or even painful, it'll make you a stronger person in the long run.

## 5. Life Tests Us All

![Life Tests Us All](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s4/a8/zrv04w3k558177055fc1c910238057_500x500.jpg)

Don't become overwhelmed when a bunch of horrible things happen at once. Situations like that occur over the course of everyone's life. It's just a test to see how strong you can really be.

## 6. No One's Life is Easy

![No One's Life is Easy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l5/pt/lhamdyrn5581770569415655124547_600x597.jpg)

There's no one better to take advice from than Bruce Lee. He knows that no one's life is going to be easy. That means you can't ask for things to always go smoothly for you, because that's not the way the world works. You can only ask for the strength to survive the difficult times.

## 7. Strength Requires Struggle

![Strength Requires Struggle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vp/eq/f98fjtdo5581770870f94401976041_600x600.jpg)

You grow stronger with each hurdle you overcome. So the next time your life doesn't go the way you want it to, don't get upset about it. Be proud that it's making you a better, stronger person than you were before.

## 8. You Can Handle What's Throw at You

![You Can Handle What's Throw at You](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cz/ct/ucxa0lsr55817705b915d488886754_500x500.jpg)

It doesn't matter whether you believe in God or not, because this quote can still be meaningful to you. The universe wouldn't put you through something you couldn't handle, would it? If you're being faced with a problem, you must have the strength to deal with it.

## 9. Persistance is Powerful

![Persistance is Powerful](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yx/x4/hfann8me55817706170ab921310033_500x450.jpg)

Following your dreams and never giving up takes strength. Even if you don't succeed, your persistence is something to be proud of. It takes a lot of guts to keep going, even when you have every reason to give up.

You're stronger than you give yourself credit for. Remind yourself of your strength by jotting down some of these quotes to hang around your room. What's your favorite quote that makes you feel powerful?
