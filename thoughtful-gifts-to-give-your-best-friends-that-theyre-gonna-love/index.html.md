# 10 Thoughtful Christmas Gifts to Give Your Best Friends That Theyre Gonna Love ...

Need some ideas for thoughtful gifts for best friends? You've come to the right place. There is something incredibly satisfying about searching for the perfect gifts to give your best friends. Whether it is a gag gift or a token of appreciation, thoughtful gifts for best friends should always leave them laughing or feeling sentimental. The best way to make a gift look thoughtful is to take your friends' interests or any inside jokes you may have with them into consideration. Here are some thoughtful gifts for your best friends!

## 1. Jewelry

![jewellery, fashion accessory, necklace, bracelet, chain,](https://img.allw.mn/content/q6/hq/ol8ubuvj5a2c5a5215dee204494320_323x200.gif)

What woman doesn't love jewelry, especially when it's the thoughtful kind? You can never go wrong with beautiful engraved or personalized jewelry. Charm bracelets are also adorable and sure to bring a smile to her face!

## 2. Plants

![plant, flora, leaf, flower, bud,](https://img.allw.mn/content/tu/ez/huctsrf25a2c5a708c696247972863_480x324.gif)

Plants are such delightful and thoughtful gifts. They're guaranteed to add joy to your friends' lives and brighten up their homes. If they're amateurs when it comes to gardening, give them something simple like a cactus or succulent. They're easy to care for, hard to kill, and they look super cute! Perfect!

## 3. For the Love of Books

![book, publication,](https://img.allw.mn/content/so/tn/nc3gjzn45a2c5a8dd4c37832208271_500x266.gif)

Want to gift your best friend a little bit of magic? You can't go wrong with a book. To make it extra special, get her a copy of her favorite book or childhood bedtime story signed by the author.

## 4. Relaxing Gifts

![purple, television program, fun, product, event,](https://img.allw.mn/content/r1/zp/v87lt4qj5a2c5aff5dcb5750050726_480x270.gif)

God knows everyone needs a little "me time" during the stressful Holiday season. Why not help your best friend de-stress with some relaxing gifts like these?

## 5. Beauty Goals

![lip, skin, cheek, human hair color, eyebrow,](https://img.allw.mn/content/bj/cl/qqseufxa5a2c5b495d1b0100084390_500x281.gif)

If your BFF is someone who loves to keep up with the latest beauty trends and is addicted to makeup, gift her some of the hottest beauty products this Holiday season!

## 6. Room Decor

![event, girl, product, fun, socialite,](https://img.allw.mn/content/jg/hy/rvfwm0eu5a2c5ce70f02f841102861_500x288.gif)

Why not gift your best friend something that will remind her of you every time she looks at it? Room decor like wall art, cute Polaroid photos, inspirational framed quotes, etc. are incredibly thoughtful gifts no matter what the occasion.

## 7. You Can't Fail with Accessories

![finger, black and white, nail, hand, monochrome photography,](https://img.allw.mn/content/e7/e8/gvp6wlfd5a2c5c0149b7a306770911_500x261.gif)

Shopping for clothes for other people can be quite a daunting task, but thankfully shopping for accessories is much less intimidating! If your friend loves wearing jewelry, hats, or scarves, buy her something you know she'll love! She'll be grateful, and you won't have to sneak around her closet looking for what size jeans she wears!

![christmas decoration, christmas, christmas tree, tradition, event,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ux/b0/yktimklj5a2c5d023190a407844385_500x281.gif)

If your bestie is blessed with a creative side, help her hone her skills by giving her something related to what she loves doing. For example, if she loves drawing and painting, you could give her an adult coloring book. Is she obsessed with photography? Why not get her some accessories for her camera or maybe even enroll her in a class to help her get better? Take a moment to think about her hobbies and interests and the ideas will come to you.

## 9. Fitness Goodies

![physical fitness, muscle, joint, performance art, arm,](https://img.allw.mn/content/u2/sm/wudmzfau5a2c5c9462180446051360_500x281.gif)

Does the phrase "fitness fanatic" remind you of your best friend? If the answer is yes, don't think twice about gifting her some fitness related goodies. She will definitely be grateful for the thought you put into it.

![girl, fun,](https://img.allw.mn/content/pm/ql/x2freu0x5a2c5c6b38867814330454_500x280.gif)
