# 17 New Year's Resolutions That'll Make You Happier ...

Can't come up with a new New Year's resolution? There's no need to worry, because there are plenty you can pick from. You can even choose a few, so that you have multiple goals to achieve. Here are a few New Year's resolutions that'll make next year the best year of your life:

## 1. Take a Yoga Class

![fireworks, night, darkness, evening, event,](https://img.allw.mn/content/aq/e7/jw5hoy845863a5b896236442758889_500x300.gif)

Yoga benefits both your mind and body. It'll help you relax while helping you get fit. That's why you should sign up for a class. That way, you'll be forced to go to the gym every week.

## 2. Quit Your Bad Habit

![crowd, Happy, New, Year, Lieutenant,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ug/ti/rgsemx605863a5b8c92e6575461521_500x170.gif)

If you bite your nails, smoke, or eat with your mouth open, it's time to drop the habit. You don't want to continue doing something unhealthy, do you?

## 3. Compliment Yourself Every Day

![album cover, music, stage, HAPPY, The,](https://img.allw.mn/content/id/ju/cvk245i25863a5b912cd9245998088_500x272.gif)

Stop criticizing yourself. Instead, you should aim to compliment yourself every single day when you look in the mirror. It'll boost your self-esteem.

## 4. Get in Touch with Your Emotions

![Get in Touch with Your Emotions](https://img.allw.mn/content/rn/o5/qg8uae5656685bf543d73922447783_500x281.gif)

Don't hold back your feelings. Tell your loved ones how much you care about them as often as you can.

## 5. Stop Drinking Soda

![Stop Drinking Soda](https://img.allw.mn/content/ly/un/n6y53t2o56685bf5b27a0749008520_500x372.gif)

Stop drinking soda, coffee, or alcohol. It sounds impossible, but you can do it.

## 6. Learn a New Language

![person, cartoon, screenshot, comics, illustration,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ms/yt/mlw2fjke5863a5b92c277652567506_500x380.gif)

It's always helpful to know more than one language. It'll look great on a resume.

## 7. Talk to New People

![performance, singing, singer, guitarist, HAPPY,](https://img.allw.mn/content/o5/r7/vyknfggw5863a5bac0abf583174853_500x357.gif)

You don't have to become friends with everyone you meet. However, you should try to talk to new people whenever you can. You never know what you'll learn.

## 8. Drink More Water

![Drink More Water](https://img.allw.mn/content/h0/xj/di0ca6qv56685bf506c8d466152807_500x215.gif)

Water is essential to your survival. Make it your mission to carry around a few water bottles, so that you actually end up drinking as much as your body needs.

## 9. Sleep More

![Sleep More](https://img.allw.mn/content/a2/yn/f4gkh5qv56685ca6ebd5c767410176_500x200.gif)

Most of us don't actually need less sleep. We need more sleep. Make sure you're getting at least eight hours per night.

## 10. Watch Less Netflix

![Watch Less Netflix](https://img.allw.mn/content/gi/zs/ejvy1xuf56685ca61b4d4917824712_500x281.gif)

Netflix is amazing, but you shouldn't be watching it for ten hours a day. Try to cut down on how many shows you binge watch per year.

## 11. Go on More Dates

![Go on More Dates](https://img.allw.mn/content/l6/ta/porodlo756685bf68075a309540855_500x250.gif)

If you're in a relationship, make sure to take your man out on the town as often as you can. If you're single, make sure you ask out a few men to see your options.

## 12. Take Daily Walks

![cartoon, play, comics, illustration,](https://img.allw.mn/content/l6/ih/x3jwvc1b5863a5bb1ac6f995089335_500x375.gif)

You don't have to go crazy when exercising. You should simply take a few walks every week.

## 13. Recycle

![Recycle](https://img.allw.mn/content/z2/mn/rn87l76356685bf6cebfd423056688_245x184.gif)

You want to help the environment, don't you? Then you should start recycling, and keep to it!

## 14. Learn New Recipes

![Learn New Recipes](https://img.allw.mn/content/r4/v5/p6x4j9ew56685bf6a3c80326261328_500x375.gif)

Instead of aiming to read a new book every month, you should learn a new recipe every month. By the end of the year, you'll know how to cook a dozen new things.

## 15. Write Everyday

![Dick Clark's New Year's Rockin' Eve, rock concert, performance, disco, music,](https://img.allw.mn/content/sg/xd/pfwy18pq5863a5bb6db79826010085_480x270.gif)

It doesn't matter if you want to write fiction or want to write about your day in a journal. Either way, writing will keep your mind sharp.

## 16. Delete Negative People from Your Life

![cartoon, games, screenshot, HERE'S, ANOTHER,](https://img.allw.mn/content/aw/xm/fckqlh9t5863a5bc78d27826217874_577x351.gif)

If a friend or family member has been making your life a living hell, it's time to cut them out of your life. Delete them from social media and stop accepting their calls.

## 17. Cut down on Technology

![face, white, black and white, photograph, person,](https://img.allw.mn/content/uf/tp/puo4p6z75863a5bbb0c54957591613_500x350.gif)

Stop bringing your phone, iPad, and Kindle with you everywhere you go. Bring a paper book instead.

If you want next year to be better than this one, you need to pick a perfect resolution. What is your New Year's resolution going to be this year?
