# 15 Brilliant Ways to Be Happier 🤗 ...

Happiness is a sought-after goal in life for everybody, yet many find it hard to find it. A lot of people will delude their self by thinking “What I have (insert something here), then I will be happy.” In reality, happiness shouldn’t be dependent on having something or being a certain weight. Here are 15 **ways to be happier. **

## 1. Do the Things You Love

No matter what you love doing, do it. It’s not fun forcing yourself to do things you don’t like.

## 2. Help Others around You

Some people achieve their goals, but still feel empty. Contributing to another person’s life through volunteering can help.

## 3. Be Thankful for What You Have

Sometimes all you need to realize is how much you do have in life.

## 4. Share with Other People

When you share things with others, it will make you feel better. Life can become lonely when you don’t share it with others.

## 5. Smile More

Smiling can help change the way you feel internally. Smiling will make you happier.

## 6. Break a Sweat

Exercise helps to reduce stress and it releases endorphins.

## 7. Find a Life Coach

These coaches can help to evaluate your life and offer you advice on what you can do to make it better.

## 8. Learn How to Manage Your Stress

You deserve to be happy, so find ways to reduce your stress to feel better.

## 9. Eat Healthily

It’s hard to feel happy when you sick, but a healthy diet can help you feel better.

## 10. Spend Time with Those You Love

There isn’t a replacement for spending time with people you love to be around. This is true for introverts and loners as well.

## 11. Get Rid of Those Negative Thoughts

Negative thoughts make you unhappy. Work to become more aware of those negative thoughts and try to replace them with positive ones.

## 12. Give Gifts

It doesn’t have to be expensive. It can be as simple as an email, poem, or note.

## 13. Forgive and Forget

It’s not going to serve anyone to hold onto that long-term grudge. Let it go, and it will make you happier.

## 14. Walk through Nature

Spending some time in nature will help to renew and refresh yourself.

## 15. Just Be You

In the wise words of Steve Jobs, “You time is limited, so don’t waste it living someone else’s life.”
