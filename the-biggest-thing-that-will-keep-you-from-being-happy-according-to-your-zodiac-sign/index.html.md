# The Biggest Thing That Will Keep You from Being Happy According to Your Zodiac Sign ...

One of the main things that we all strive for in life is the simple notion of being happy ... being happy in your career, being happy in your relationships, being happy in your friendships, just generally being happy in as many aspects of your life as possible. Unfortunately, the nature of being human beings means that achieving all of these things at once is incredibly rare, but if you believe in the powers of astrology, then you might be able to find a way. Here is **the biggest thing that will keep you from being happy according to your zodiac sign**.

## 1. Aries

You are independent, which is great, but sometimes you can be independent to a fault. People around you feel like you aren’t going to let them in and make time for them, which stops them from trying to make meaningful connections.

## 2. Taurus

You have a great fear of the unknown, which has the power to stop you from trying new things and making a better life for yourself.

## 3. Gemini

You are a natural multi-tasker, but this often means that you have your fingers in too many pies and never end up actually seeing anything through to completion.

## 4. Cancer

You spend way too much time hanging onto the past, which means that you never have your eyes set on what the future might be able to do for you.

## 5. Leo

Your unhappiness tends to stem from your insecurities. On the surface, you are the life of the party, but deep down, the attention that you get feels hollow.

## 6. Virgo

You are a natural worrier. This in itself isn’t a bad thing, but your level of worrying starts to create problems that weren’t even there to begin with.

## 7. Libra

You are so indecisive that by the time you have come down on one side of something, the opportunity has been and gone. You are always left weighing things up when everyone else moved forward.

## 8. Scorpio

You have a tendency to get jealous of other people’s successes, and rather than let them inspire you to do better yourself, you retreat into yourself and just get a bit bitter about it.

## 9. Sagittarius

You are always on the hunt for adventure, but there comes a point where all of your companions and partners in crime are tired out and want to settle down, and you are left alone.

## 10. Capricorn

Your unhappiness will always stem from your own negativity. You have the tendency to put yourself down so much that your brain tricks you into thinking that that is what other people think of you too.

## 11. Aquarius

You are extremely sensitive, to the point where you sometimes let the tragedies and sadness of others take up too much space in your own life. Your empathy for others can drag you down.

## 12. Pisces

You don’t have a lot of patience, which means that if you can’t achieve a goal straight away, you tend to give up on it even if it was the thing that could have unlocked a lot of happiness in your life.
