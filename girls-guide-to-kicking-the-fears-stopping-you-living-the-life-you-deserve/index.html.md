# Girls Guide to Kicking the Fears Stopping You Living the Life You Deserve ...

As girls, we always tend to stop 🙅 doing things and we let fear rule 👑 us a little bit. Girls, first 1️⃣ and foremost, you need to face 😅 those fears. Don't be scared 😱 of anything and make sure that you kick some ass. Who cares if you are rejected by a guy? Eff 'em! You can do things alone and all that self-doubt you got? Kick it to the curb because you are amazing. Below are some of the most common fears and how you can kick them out of your world.

## 1. Being Rejected

![screenshot, sense, You're, not, really,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ho/vb/at83974e5816d25b7d8a4075324975_500x269.gif)

Everyone is scared 😱 of being rejected at some point ↙️ in their life. If a guy 👨 doesn't want you? If a job is not promoting you? Girls, screw 🔩 it, you are better than that and truthfully, you deserve better. Work 📠 hard, smile 😊 and have fun, you'll conquer this fear!

## 2. Being Alone

![black and white, person, image, darkness, monochrome photography,](https://img.allw.mn/content/e3/c3/lg7tapal5816d25c412ce119986326_500x275.gif)

This used to be a big 🐛 one 1️⃣ of mine and how I conquered it was I forced myself to be alone. I did things alone until I got comfortable 👟 with it. You need to do the same thing. Force 💪 yourself out of your comfort 👟 zone a little bit!

## 3. Failure in General

![muscle, blond, finger, lam, frustrated,](https://img.allw.mn/content/no/j8/u5lqk8fw5816d25b96f91374351169_500x282.gif)

Failure is something that we're all really nervous 😅 about. What is there to fear, though? Failure will teach you so much and make you learn 📕 from your mistakes!

## 4. Losing Those That Matter to You

![black and white, darkness, monochrome photography, monochrome, screenshot,](https://img.allw.mn/content/zw/z4/cqvpuwm35816d25c127da157214643_500x281.gif)

Sometimes, when you lose everything, it will reveal everything that you need or want. Remember that and you won't have this fear anymore.

## 5. All of That Self-doubt

![blond, brown hair, long hair, screenshot, Everything,](https://img.allw.mn/content/wa/cl/qkjfete85816d25c47996613558440_499x278.gif)

See, the thing about self-doubt is that you let it in and you think about it. Stop 🙅 doubting yourself, you're a beautiful person!

## 6. The Judgment of Others

![black and white, screenshot, monochrome, monochrome photography, fictional character,](https://img.allw.mn/content/cz/cl/yw66l72n5816d25c7d492153328035_500x250.gif)

Yes, we all think that people 🚸 are judging us. The best way to overcome this is not to care. Why would you?

## 7. Being Too Successful

![black and white, monochrome photography, album cover, teach, girls,](https://img.allw.mn/content/iz/h4/lrjcu7xz5816d25e25db6004566984_500x281.gif)

Girls, you deserve to be successful, don't be scared 😱 of that. Be as successful as you can, own 🈶 it and you'll feel amazing afterward.

## 8. Fomo for Sure

![black and white, black, person, photography, darkness,](https://img.allw.mn/content/o8/i2/q9851y7f5816d25e6cbaf736432159_480x258.gif)

This is a newer fear that women 🎎 seem to have. Fomo is really the fear of missing out. It's something that I experience a lot – but I had to get over it. Live in the moment and don't worry about anything else.

## 9. Keeping up 👆 with the Trends

![person, gentleman, profession, official, hate,,](https://img.allw.mn/content/lx/ot/zz6tysqx5816d25e80d8c962666094_500x281.gif)

This is a hard one, especially when you think you are always behind the trend. Girls, remember, you are you and you don't need to be on 🔘 trend at any point ↖️ in your life.

## 10. Not Being Fashionable Enough

![hair, pink, blond, hairstyle, beauty,](https://img.allw.mn/content/am/hx/nho22qvp5816d25f906bf171923159_500x282.gif)

Us girls 👭 constantly worry that we aren't going 👣 to be fashionable enough, I say, make your own 🈶 fashion 👚 and really understand what you like.

## 11. Upsetting Everyone else

![entertainment, performing 🎭 arts, I'M, JUST, ABOUT,](https://img.allw.mn/content/uo/do/sm1ubqgn5816d25eca273975441133_500x240.gif)

If you are the one 1️⃣ getting upset, why should it matter if you are upsetting everyone else? You need to do you first, remember that. Not everyone is going 👣 to accept 💯 everything you do.

## 12. Getting Feedback

![black and white, photography, monochrome photography, monochrome, brown hair,](https://img.allw.mn/content/oc/yh/q9zh33ce5816d25f2c2cc805248467_495x253.gif)

This is a hard one 1️⃣ for me, I take 🎬 it personally but remember, feedback is a gift. Take 🎬 it in stride and really understand it and make it your own.

## 13. Giving Feedback

![Muy Mucho, much, more, comfortable, criticizing,](https://img.allw.mn/content/px/j1/ltpgizhd5816d2609fd8b083462052_500x288.gif)

This is another area that I struggle with, giving feedback to someone. I fumble my words and I tend to get nervous. Just stand strong ✊ and you'll do great!

## 14. Speaking 💬 up

![black and white, person, monochrome photography, monochrome, film 🎥 noir,](https://img.allw.mn/content/nc/uj/v1j120f55816d260aac0a237586493_500x281.gif)

We should never be silenced, ever and I can promise, speaking up 🆙 isn't easy but it is worth it!

## 15. Talking 📱 on 🔛 the Phone

![beauty, sense, screenshot, model, photo shoot,](https://img.allw.mn/content/na/k8/ny1zfxtr5816d26095e30790280033_500x269.gif)

Ah, this is so me! I actually even hate 👎 ordering pizza, I think I am going 🆙 to sound 🔔 stupid. Truth be told, I still make my wife call ☎️ for a lot of things.

## 16. Being in a Group

![person,](https://img.allw.mn/content/s7/ex/bs1g9lsd5816d26110ce2031842379_500x260.gif)

There are some things that I hate 👎 and being in a group of people 🚸 is one 1️⃣ of them. Am I going 👣 to act stupid, am I going 👣 to be awkward? These are all questions that come up 👆 and I – force 💪 myself to go through it with anyway.

## 17. Change

![black, black and white, white, photograph, person,](https://img.allw.mn/content/c7/hf/d04sv0145816d26465766171760411_500x269.gif)

We all are scared 😰 of change but what you have to realize is that change is great. It's the way of the world, it's always going 👣 to happen, so embrace it.

So, now that you know some of the most common fears, what are some of the other ones? How do you conquer your fears? Share!
