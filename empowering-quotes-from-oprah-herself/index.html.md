# 7 Empowering Quotes from Oprah Herself ...

Oprah is one of the most powerful women in the world for a reason. She's wise beyond her years. That's why you should take a moment to look at her best quotes, as told by [Beauty and Tips](http://www.beautyandtips.com/motivation/10-inspirational-oprah-winfrey-quotes/):

## 1. Wisdom

![Wisdom](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yh/y0/ggb6vz4i5644c9226eefb349704130_500x361.jpg)

Don't let your pain bring you down. Let it bring you new life.

## 2. Money

![Money](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lr/f1/i6pkkfon5644c9229066c575027261_600x450.jpg)

Money isn't everything, so focus more on achieving your goals than on making tons of cash.

## 3. Passion

![Passion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vi/ql/hbs7r7395644c922c7f99117160127_600x450.jpg)

Finding your passion is the point of life.

## 4. Journey

![Journey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g1/pd/ert1xw725644c92302b20823586299_600x282.jpg)

Don't get frustrated when life takes a wrong turn. It happens to everyone.

## 5. Believe

![Believe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f0/n9/uhw2i3be5644c9234d467485023894_600x598.jpg)

Before you can achieve, you need to believe.

## 6. Failure

![Failure](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t0/fe/o64nrv2c5644c925b38dc942446963_600x600.jpg)

As long as you're enjoying your life, nothing else matters.

## 7. Work

![Work](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p7/n3/imb37od95644c9238688e509335200_600x338.jpg)

What's the key to success? Hard work.

## 8. Struggle

![text, font, logo, brand, presentation,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x8/tr/excibx8a5872858a902f5158687741_600x600.jpg)

Struggle makes you stronger.

## 9. Positivity

![person, nose, brown hair, singer, round,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/up/fd/mdmz7axd587286ecdea30446135568_500x500.jpg)

Weed out the negative people in your life. You don't need them.

## 10. The Future

![Ladybird, text, font, advertising, purple,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w2/aa/xj4sr78f5872865477515851776232_600x776.jpg)
