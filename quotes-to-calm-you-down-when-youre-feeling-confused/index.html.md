# 21 Quotes to Calm You down when Youre Feeling Confused ...

Life can get so confusing so quickly. No matter what's going on in your life, if you're currently trying to figure things out in your life, there are quotes on this list that will help you in even the most confusing, weird, and upsetting times. These quotes will give you hope in the darkest and strangest of times!

## 1. Sometimes You Have to Let People Go

![Sometimes You Have to Let People Go](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lz/k8/f3689aol567a0930c2933649044058_493x750.jpg)

If someone isn't adding something to your life and they're instead hurting you, it's time to remove them from your life, even if it seems hard.

## 2. Because when You Stop and Look around, Life is Pretty Amazing

![Because when You Stop and Look around, Life is Pretty Amazing](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l0/q8/jgf1pod1567a09312311d907804024_493x750.jpg)

Sometimes all it takes to clear your mind is taking the time to stop and look around.

## 3. Staying Strong

![Staying Strong](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q5/wm/p4lt8i2y567a0932d153a029577740_600x304.jpg)

If you have every reason to fall apart, stay strong and smile.

## 4. The Flower Blossoms

![The Flower Blossoms](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s5/d7/q65cmkvf567a09313d70e196113676_600x734.jpg)

Stop searching for people- they'll come to you when you're ready.

## 5. Daring Greatly Means the Courage to Be Vulnerable

![Daring Greatly Means the Courage to Be Vulnerable](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gl/l8/bhzirlkz567a0931a0d15986421264_600x600.jpg)

Sometimes you just need to show up and be seen.

## 6. Some Changes Look Negative

![Some Changes Look Negative](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q7/bo/ebspso7j567a0931e29c1377012992_481x720.jpg)

Everything in life is a process and constantly changing!

## 7. They're Just Mistakes

![They're Just Mistakes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/au/yp/hg9jhkuv567a093213087362342019_480x480.jpg)

Eventually you'll realize that it's time to clean up and move on.

## 8. Behavior Decides Who Stays in Your Life

![Behavior Decides Who Stays in Your Life](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u3/nv/kvb0g91o567a093250f29302475249_500x750.jpg)

So many different things determine the people in your life. Be conscious of that.

## 9. Keep Calm

![Keep Calm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lp/rd/kxl7yxln567a093297b90142699088_600x896.jpg)

Live, because you only get one life.

## 10. Fires Will Come

![Fires Will Come](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n9/mz/meiatsil567a0932b7d44253800514_400x400.jpg)

Learn to be fireproof.

## 11. Happy Endings

![Happy Endings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pl/qe/u48mmhq9567a0933abb85950513739_600x600.jpg)

Happy endings can mean so many different things. You'll learn what your happy ending is.

## 12. Ignore Uncertainty

![Ignore Uncertainty](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/np/pm/ightyinl567a093353efd107475420_500x500.jpg)

Uncertainty can be the cause of so much stress. Don't let it be.

## 13. You're Human

![You're Human](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pa/dx/eb1azh05567a0933799a9306939354_500x720.jpg)

You're human. Let yourself feel everything.

## 14. Before This and after This

![Before This and after This](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dl/s0/jkmm7m0o567a093445a56801474547_600x892.jpg)

We all have those moments in our life.

## 15. Don't Confuse Your Path with Your Destination

![Don't Confuse Your Path with Your Destination](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hw/yz/a31n3tcv567a093416c4b265827674_550x927.jpg)

Where you are right now is not your final destination.

## 16. Don't Care about Other People

![Don't Care about Other People](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d9/wu/tf2v2azf567a0933e3f9b690034935_400x534.jpg)

We all know people that are unhappy because they're so worried about what other people think.

## 17. Stop Pretending

![Stop Pretending](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kz/oj/es5xw8l5567a0934311c7528532912_500x750.jpg)

When you pretend you're someone else, you may forget who you actually are.

## 18. You're Developing Strength

![You're Developing Strength](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/of/us/b75nlho1567a0934824c9905254070_550x828.jpg)

You're going to need this strength more than you even realize right now. Keep pushing.

## 19. Stress Vs. Passion

![Stress Vs. Passion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jl/c6/rhsatoyl567a0934c24fd676513413_600x600.jpg)

This is the difference between stress and passion!

## 20. Life Changes Quickly

![Life Changes Quickly](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/px/pu/o8b6kwh3567a093539a83300845307_600x950.jpg)

Life can change in the blink of an eye so be prepared for the unknown!

## 21. It's Okay to Miss People

![It's Okay to Miss People](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ow/v0/sp7yj002567a09359e6d7429190720_500x700.jpg)

Even if you don't want that person in your life anymore, you can still miss the person they were!
