# How Your Zodiac Sign Makes the World a Better Place ...

I think it’s fair to say that all any of us can hope for in life is to be successful and happy in our individual pursuits, and hopefully find ways to make the world a better place while doing it. Whether it is through small actions or big ones, we can all find ways to make a positive difference, you might even already be doing things without necessarily realising it. Here is **how you make the world a better place according to your zodiac sign**!

## 1. Aries

You are a natural born leader, and you make the world a better place by taking charge and providing less confident people with a path and a journey to take with you.

## 2. Taurus

You are full of wisdom, and in your own small way you give out advice and counsel that can help those around to make better decisions in their lives.

## 3. Gemini

You are a real go-getter. When you want something done, you get out there and do it for yourself, which sets an amazing example for those near and dear to you.

## 4. Cancer

You are an expert peacekeeper. If there is tension in your inner circles, you are the person who can get in the middle of it and completely dissipate it. You keep relationships going strong.

## 5. Leo

Your power to help improve to the world comes in the form of your confident and moving ability of speech. You can articulate your thoughts so well that people have no choice but to sit up and listen.

## 6. Virgo

You have a really logical brain, which means that you can often see solutions to problems before anyone else. This kind of talent is vital for coming up with ideas that could one day change the world.

## 7. Libra

Your natural charisma means that people gravitate towards you and follow you, whether you want them to or not! This sort of magnetism makes you a perfect figure for spreading a positive message in the world.

## 8. Scorpio

You have an almost Sherlock Holmes level ability to uncover information and get to the bottom of a mystery. These abilities help you to be someone who people come to when they need answers and reassurance.

## 9. Sagittarius

You are a true initiator of change, somebody who has the power to inspire those around you to try new things and break out of their comfort zones.

## 10. Capricorn

You have the power to calm people down even in the most stressful of situations. You always know what to say and exactly how to say it.

## 11. Aquarius

Some people might label you as controversial and edgy, but what you really are is the kind of person who forces society to move forward and get with the times.

## 12. Pisces

You are a total dreamer and optimist, and when the world is in the kind of mess it seems to be in right now, dreamers are some of the most important people to have around.
