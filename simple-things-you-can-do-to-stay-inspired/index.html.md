# 9 Simple Things You Can do to Stay Inspired ...

Even though sometimes it can be pretty difficult to stay focused, there are still a few things you can do to stay inspired, so you can finish all your tasks and achieve all your goals. By being more motivated, you’ll feel happier, more enthusiastic, more ambitious and you will also be able to motivate the people around you. Just like Mario Fernandez said, “Rise above the storm and you will find the sunshine.” Just try to vanquish monotony from your life, since this is motivation’s number one enemy. Here are a few very simple things you can do to stay inspired that will help you feel like you can accomplish everything:

## 1. Stay Healthy

One of the simplest things you can do to stay inspired is to stay healthy every day. If you don’t take care of your health, you won’t be able to feel motivated to complete all your tasks. Try to eat healthy, do enough exercising and remember to drink enough water, so you can stay hydrated. Get at least 7 or 8 hours of sleep every night and make time for the things that make you feel happy. You need to relax every once in a while, so don’t forget about this important aspect of your life if you want to stay inspired!

## 2. Do Something for the First Time

Be courageous and do something for the first time! Whether it’s something you’ve been contemplating for quite a while or something you don’t think you’re capable of, just do it and see how good you will feel afterwards, when you realize that there’s nothing you can’t accomplish if you work hard enough. Just do whatever you want: bake a cake, learn a new language or join a dance class. I’m sure you have plenty to choose from, so what are you waiting for? Go and find your inspiration!

## 3. Plan Your Day

If you want to stay inspired, then you absolutely need to organize yourself and plan your day, so you’ll have enough time for work and also for the fun things you enjoy. Every morning, make a list of all the tasks you want to accomplish during that day and try to stick to your schedule. Work on one important task for 50 minutes, then make sure you have a 10 minute break, so you’ll be able to recharge your batteries.

## 4. Avoid Procrastination

In order to stay inspired all the time, you should do your best to avoid procrastination. This is actually productivity’s number one enemy, so you should do whatever you can to not become one of its victims. It’s so easy to find other fun things to do when you have a lot of tasks to take care of and a lot of work to do. Yet, you should always keep your goals in mind and you should try to avoid all distractions, so you can stick to your schedule and do your work according to your plan.

## 5. Keep Learning

If you want to learn how to stay inspired every day, you should make sure you never stop learning. Make enough time to read something every day, since reading increases knowledge, something that will help you stay inspired in order to reach all your goals. Watch different tutorials online and stay curious, because a lot of studies have shown that conducting research actually increases your capabilities.

## 6. Express Gratitude!

Be grateful for what you have, even if it’s not always what you wanted. You are so lucky just to be yourself! Every morning when you wake up, think about 10 things you are grateful for and you will see how happy and inspired you will feel during that day! Don’t forget, gratitude promotes happiness, so always be thankful for what you have!

## 7. Get Rid of the Clutter

In order to stay inspired, you’ll need to get rid of all the clutter from your life! Just organize your desk, clean your entire home and throw away all the things that you don’t use, that you don’t need or that you don’t like anymore. You could also try donating them to a charity, because I’m sure that there are a lot of people who could use them. Just get rid of all the clutter and you will see that a tidy house will give you the energy and the inspiration you need to complete all your tasks.

## 8. Do Something You Enjoy!

Do something you enjoy every day! Life isn’t all work and no play. You need to take a little break every once in a while, so you’ll be able to recharge your batteries. Just indulge in your favorite thing every now and then and you’ll see how inspired you’ll feel afterwards.

## 9. Keep a Journal

If you want to track your progress and see how far you’ve come since you first started to create something, just keep a journal and write down every little thing that troubles you or that you’ve found exciting. A journal is a wonderful source of inspiration, it will help you stay on track and it will help you find the motivation you need to reach all your goals.

There are a lot of simple, yet effective things you can do to stay motivated in order to follow your dreams. I just mentioned a few in this little article but I’m sure there are many more I haven’t talked about. Can you give me a hand and tell me what other simple things you can do to stay inspired? Please share your thoughts with us in the comments section!
