# Secrets of Girls Who Have It All Together for Girls Who Wish They Did ...

Do you look at the women around you and feel like you want all the **secrets of girls who have it all together**? Don't we all? Getting your life on track can be an overwhelming task, but it's not impossible. Women who have it together often have certain habits that contribute to their success. So, here are the best **secrets of girls who have it all together.** Use them to get your own life together. You won't be sorry.

## 1. She Exercises

![active undergarment, shoulder, joint, abdomen, thigh,](https://img.allw.mn/content/qp/lf/g93hb9gf59f0accf65281918666734_250x141.gif)

One of the best **secrets of girls who have it all together** is that they never skip a workout. Quit hitting the snooze button and get up for your run or weight lifting session. It gives you a feeling of accomplishment that transfers to other areas.

## 2. She Dances

![entertainment, event, fun, performance art, performance,](https://img.allw.mn/content/pt/zz/ielqtte859f0acf164ebf931781794_500x305.gif)

Stop being embarrassed and do what you love, whether it's dancing, singing or cooking. Girls who have their lives together don't care what others think. They do what they want, when they want.

## 3. She Gets Enough Sleep

![girl, romance, interaction, love,](https://img.allw.mn/content/t6/y9/ws713sfa59f0ad28f02c2382498367_500x200.gif)

Girls who have it together know that lack of sleep can really interfere with life. You should be getting at least 7 to 9 hours every night. If you're not, it's time to change your bedtime routine, starting tonight.

## 4. She Has a Signature Scent

![singer, blond, singing, performance, socialite,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ya/qn/o3pk6l1h59f0ad4eccf38508557658_500x267.gif)

A dab of perfume as you head out the door is a great way to appear polished and put together. If you don't have a signature scent, get one.

## 5. She Smiles

![facial expression, human hair color, beauty, black and white, smile,](https://img.allw.mn/content/wd/pa/enwkstce59f0ad8bb4eab500556155_500x273.gif)

Resting bitch face has its place, but girls who have it together will tell you that a simple smile will go a long way. Whether you smile at your boss, your kids or a total stranger, you'll both feel better and you'll feel like you have your life together.

## 6. She Downplays Her Flaws

![eyebrow, human hair color, nose, beauty, girl,](https://img.allw.mn/content/xy/oy/rf3i5u9b59f0add148d31282093836_500x270.gif)

Everyone has flaws, but a girl who has it together doesn't go around sharing what hers are. She quietly works on them, but doesn't make them the center of the show.

## 7. She Applies Herself

![hair, human hair color, blond, beauty, girl,](https://img.allw.mn/content/tt/mk/cp7p6cg659f0adf533c17910445453_500x248.gif)

Being lazy sure feels good, but it definitely doesn't make you appear put together if you do it all the time. Girls who have their life on track are those who apply themselves to their career, relationships and everything else life has to offer.

How do you keep yourself together?
