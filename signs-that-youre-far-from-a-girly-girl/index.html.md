# 17 Signs You Are Far from Being a Girly Girl ...

Being a tomboy is not at all a bad thing. In fact, I was a tomboy when I was a kid. Dressing up? Colors? Fashion? I knew none of it! What I did know was dirt, grit and grime. I knew playing in the woods and never dressing up. If you aren't a girly girl, you'll totally relate to this list!

![Sweatpants Are a Staple … Even outside of the Home](https://img.allw.mn/content/fw/id/nszvx69z56ad50eaadb7c771271645_500x262.gif)

… and inside of the home the second you get home from work.

## 2. You Really Don't Deal with Crying Well

![You Really Don't Deal with Crying Well](https://img.allw.mn/content/cm/oj/mgcvbae656ad50ead016d768864149_500x225.gif)

## 3. You Love the No-fuss Haircuts

![You Love the No-fuss Haircuts](https://img.allw.mn/content/xo/hz/xy8x03sm56ad50ebd5f62654452052_500x230.gif)

You know, the ones where you don't have to use a ton of products.

## 4. You're That Girl …

![You're That Girl …](https://img.allw.mn/content/uk/ce/zxrofy3b56ad50fcb58b7751885125_500x270.gif)

… that will play sports, that will get sweaty, that will roll with the boys.

## 5. Makeup is so Not Your Friend

![Makeup is so Not Your Friend](https://img.allw.mn/content/se/w6/hke6ikq656ad50f5efa4e988350135_500x281.gif)

## 6. You Get Ready so, so Much Faster than Girly Girls

![You Get Ready so, so Much Faster than Girly Girls](https://img.allw.mn/content/av/zh/mc81548256ad50f618f7f981846836_490x276.gif)

Tousled hair, sweat pants, splash of perfume – you're ready!

## 7. Heels and You Aren't Friends …

![Heels and You Aren't Friends …](https://img.allw.mn/content/xc/em/avzmw56o56ad50f54c568437356761_500x281.gif)

Never. Ever again. Pass the Toms over.

## 8. Cheerleading Doesn't Really Make Sense to You

![Cheerleading Doesn't Really Make Sense to You](https://img.allw.mn/content/cx/ns/op04s5iy56ad50f61463b280077224_500x238.gif)

Not that it is a bad … sport, it just didn't make sense to you.

## 9. You Eat a Decent Amount – Even if It's Messy

![You Eat a Decent Amount – Even if It's Messy](https://img.allw.mn/content/bh/y6/of3dtc4f56ad50f597d18850662796_500x250.gif)

## 10. You've Tried Hair Accessories … but Failed

![You've Tried Hair Accessories … but Failed](https://img.allw.mn/content/e5/cb/rvh9z07x56ad50f72f161594129298_500x260.gif)

Bows and things? Nope! Nope! Nope!

## 11. You've so, so Adventurous

![You've so, so Adventurous](https://img.allw.mn/content/pl/g4/uycz7mxn56ad50f736677133705451_500x332.gif)

Like, you love to go hiking and when you swim? You'll get your hair wet.

## 12. Besides Sweats, Jeans and a Tee Shirt Are a Staple

![Besides Sweats, Jeans and a Tee Shirt Are a Staple](https://img.allw.mn/content/gn/ni/fhx8sbwf56ad50fc6217b174548777_500x281.gif)

## 13. You're Not at All a Diva

![You're Not at All a Diva](https://img.allw.mn/content/cx/y3/ry2rqz4r56ad50fcacf5e370585799_500x281.gif)

## 14. Video Games Are Your Life Sometimes

![Video Games Are Your Life Sometimes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b3/vm/lajk0gzi56ad510223b42423710235_500x281.jpg)
