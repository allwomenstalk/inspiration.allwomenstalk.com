# 7 Self\-Development Videos Thatll Help You Love Yourself ...

Sometimes, we might need a little help from our self-development friends and there are some great self-development videos on YouTube to help you get through life and help you learn to love yourself. Wading through the morass of self-development videos online can be a challenge and knowing which ones to watch and which ones to ignore can be difficult when all you want is that motivational hit to get you through the day. Here are some of my favorite self-development videos that'll help you love yourself that little bit more, starting from today.

## 1. Self-Esteem

Low self-esteem can get in the way of you achieving your goals and ambitions so it's essential to find ways to build it. There are many ways in which our self-esteem can be damaged. Maybe a relationship has broken down or you didn't get that promotion you wanted. The way in which we look at these events can either make us or break us and our self-esteem has a lot to do with it.

## 2. The Skill of Self Confidence

Self confidence is key in all avenues of life. Without it we're depending on others to make decisions for us and thinking that other people are best qualified to make decisions for us. We need to take life into our own hands and this video on self confidence is sure to help.

## 3. Success

We all want to succeed in life, right? Whether it's in our work, study or relationships, we want the best for ourselves. Success means different things to different people and is personal to you. Whatever you want to succeed in, watch this fabulous self-development video on success to get you super motivated.

## 4. How to Love Yourself

If you don't love yourself then no one else will. Well, that's not strictly true but you do need to make peace with yourself in order to find fulfilment in life. Accepting who you are and all of your wonderful qualities is essential and this video will help you move towards loving yourself that little bit more.

## 5. Ego

You may have heard of people talking about the ego and about so and so having a massive ego but do you know what it means? We all have this so-called ego but it's what we do with it that counts. Watch this fabulous video about the ego to learn more about it and how to master your relationship with it for a more fulfilled life.

## 6. Spirituality and Happiness

Finding peace and harmony within ourselves in this crazy world can be hard. When we make peace with ourselves however, we can find the drive and motivation to glide through life without letting the little things bother us. Eckhart Tolle is the master of all things spiritual and I love watching him talk because it puts me in such a state of ease.

## 7. Motivation

If your get-up-and-go just got up and went, you're probably lacking motivation to do anything at the moment. If you're struggling to find the motivation in life then watch this video to put that motivational spring back in your step.

These are some of my favorite self-development videos and hopefully they can help you to love and appreciate yourself that little bit more. Do you know any other great self-development videos?
