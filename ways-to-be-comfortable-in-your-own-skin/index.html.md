# 5 Ways to Be Comfortable in the Skin You Are in ...

There are lots of **ways to be comfortable in your own skin** and they will make you feel better every single day.

Everyone has something about their body that she would do just about anything to change. But why? We are all uniquely designed in our own way to exhibit our own beauty. We really should not need any outside source to tell us what we should look like. Compliments can boost your ego and confidence, but if no one ever compliments you, does this mean you don’t look good? Most certainly not. No one can boost your self-esteem but you. No one walks in the skin you are in, but you, so make the best of it. Here are some **ways to be comfortable in your own skin.**

## 1. Don't Compare Yourself to What Others Look like

This is tough one because so many of us has allow different things to validate how we should look. You are made exactly right and you need to remind yourself of how great you look, instead of always comparing your appearance to other people's. Quitting with the constant comparisons is one of the easiest **ways to be comfortable in your own skin.**

## 2. Enhance Your Features

There are simple ways of enhancing your beauty, without having to spend a lot of money doing it. Get used to looking at yourself first so that can get comfortable with who you are. A little bit of makeup doesn’t hurt, but getting a new hairstyle or a fab new outfit that fits you perfectly also works.

## 3. Keep a Positive Attitude

There’s nothing more beautiful than having a captivating attitude that compliments the beautiful you. Honestly, it doesn’t matter how much one tries to conceal their natural self. If your attitude reeks, then you don't look that good. Wake up every morning with a great attitude, despite how you feel or what’s going on in your life. There’s nothing like waking up and saying “Today is going to better than yesterday." Proclaim it!

## 4. Feed Your Spirit

Pray and meditate. Go somewhere quiet where you can concentrate. There’s no better company than your own company. Finding time just to get away from the world is priceless. When you spend time in prayer or meditation, it makes you appreciate YOU. It gets you acclimated to YOU. Doing this will give you the strength you need to believe in and love your natural self.

## 5. Look in the Mirror

Admire yourself in the mirror to appreciate everything you see right before your eyes. Don’t be afraid to look at yourself and say you are just fine the way you are and if anyone objects, well then that their loss. This is your life and no one else’s. Be satisfied with everything you see. There's nothing wrong with loving yourself.

The skin you are in is yours until the end of time. Be grateful for it!
