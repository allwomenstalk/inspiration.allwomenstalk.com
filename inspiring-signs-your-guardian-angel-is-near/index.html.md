# Inspiring Signs Your Guardian Angel is near ...

Most everyone views guardian angels as messenger from God. They are usually sent to give a person guidance or for protection.

There are other thoughts that show angels to be spiritual beings. They just exist in a frequency that is different from humans. They are here to help us find our true callings and to put us on the right path. Angels will love and support the ones they were sent to care for. They do this while being unseen. They give us signs to keep us on the right path.

Here are some signs that might seem insignificant and small but when you realize who sent them, they will bring you great joy.

## 1. Feathers

This is the most common sign that an angel is near. This hold especially true if the feather is found where feathers are not normally seen. Angels leave feathers to remind us they are near and watching us. This is very true if the feather is white.

## 2. Coins or Pennies

Some people believe that angels will leave coins or pennies on the path of someone who needs a little pick me up. This is done to make you feel happy and to give some advice. Every coin has a meaning.

## 3. Rainbows

If you see a rainbow and it hasn’t rained recently, this is definitely a sign from your guardian angel. They send these to show us that we are never alone.

## 4. Clouds

Shapes or symbols in the clouds are another sign that angels like to give us. You might see the shape of an angel, flowers, hearts, etc. This will be your guardian angel checking in on you.

## 5. Seeing Random Beauty

If you have had a horrible day and out of the blue something beautiful catches your eye, your angel has just sent you a sign.

Angels are wonderful at bringing us out of a slump. They can bring us up to a higher vibration with just one sign. When this happens, show your beautiful sign to somebody else to raise their energy, too.

## 6. Random Feeling or Touch

There are times that we just feel a presence that isn’t there. This might be a presence, emotional feeling, or a touch.

Your angel might reach out a wrap their wings around you. They might place a hand on your shoulder or back or even brush your neck or arm. They can even show you they are near by giving you a sense of unconditional love. You might even have a tingling sensation on your back or face.

## 7. Animal and Baby Reactions

Animals and babies usually are more perceptive of angels than we adults are. Children will sometimes wave or smile at absolutely nothing. If this happens, it might mean your angel is close by.

## 8. Support Call

If you have ever received a call right when you needed it, it was a call from your angel. Your angel gives you the guidance you need and does whatever it needs to in order to make it happen.

## 9. Butterflies

These beautiful creatures are sent to us by either our angels or loved ones who have passed away. They are showing us that they are always watching us and will be close by. It could also be a way to tell us that they are fine.

## 10. Robins or Cardinals

This is a sign from a loved one who has passed away. They will be a welcome sight and will visit the same garden every year to reassure us that they are still close by.

## 11. Smells and Scents

At times, lost loved ones will remind us they are nearby surrounding us with a familiar scent or smell that will make us think of them. Notice when a familiar aroma comes your way. Who knows? They might be a sign from a dearly departed loved one. Angels can also show their presence by sending floral smells to places they have visited.

## 12. Dreams

Angels or deceased loved one will visit us in our sleep because we are more receptive and relaxed. These dreams will be more vivid than normal dreams and you stay in your mind a lot longer.

## 13. Sparkles

These types of messages are sent by a certain angel that knows you need its help. Each archangel has a specific color. If you have been having a hard time, you might keep seeing green sparkles, this is a sign from the Archangel Raphael. He brings healing and lets you know he is near.

## 14. A Voice

You will hear a voice out of nowhere. This is the best sign that an angel has visited you. It will either call out to you to let you know of its presence or you will get a reassuring feeling. If you are feeling scared or uncertain, you might hear a small whisper that everything will be okay.

## 15. Television and Music

Hearing a certain lyric or song that uses specific words was sent to reassure you that you are on the right path. These are never coincidences but angels working to clarify a situation. If you are just sitting and clicking through the channels and land on a show that is relative to what is bothering you, don’t keep going, because it might be a message from a higher power that is giving you the answer you need.

## 16. Magazines and Books

The next time you are reading and a certain word or phrase catches your attention, pay close attention to it. It might be a book that falls off the shelf as you walk by or a headline on a magazine that catches your eye. Take a minute to read it closely. You might be amazed that the words might just relate to what you are going through right this moment. You can be sure it is a message from your guardian angel.

## 17. Slogan and Buildings

This might be something simple as you asking for help from the Archangel Michael and then you see a building by the name of St. Michael’s soon after that. This is a sign that he heard you and is there for you. Angels can also send you a message in a number of ways. This is why you pay attention to any slogans, billboards, names of businesses, or roads that you keep seeing. This might be the message you are looking for.

## 18. Shapes of Angels

Angels can be very clever. They are everywhere and can appear to us. We might see them in just about anything from the bubbles in your bath, a shadow, a leaf, etc.
