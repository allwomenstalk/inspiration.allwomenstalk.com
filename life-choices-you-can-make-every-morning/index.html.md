# 17 Life Choices 🤔 You Can Make Every Morning when You Wake up ...

Every single day, you wake up with a million choices. If you make the right ones, then you'll feel more fulfilled and get a step closer to reaching your dreams. That's why you should remember that you have these choices every morning:

## 1. If You’ll Sleep in or Get Work Done

![If You’ll Sleep in or Get Work Done](https://img.allw.mn/content/l1/zt/jgklrqig5630e8af3088c914841127_500x278.gif)

You can either hit the snooze button on your alarm clock ten times, or you can actually wake up once you hear it ringing. Getting up an hour earlier doesn't seem like a big deal, but you can get a lot done during that time.

## 2. If You’ll Eat a Healthy Breakfast

![If You’ll Eat a Healthy Breakfast](https://img.allw.mn/content/ip/cg/lmex8t9e5630e8af69f18116751371_476x360.gif)

Don't start your day off with a bite of chocolate. Grab a banana and some high fiber cereal, so that you're healthy from the start.

## 3. If You’ll Exercise

![If You’ll Exercise](https://img.allw.mn/content/v5/rw/i0o4hye65630e8b471c98381186142_500x270.gif)

You don't have to spend your entire day lounging around. You can actually get up and move around. So make sure you fit some exercising into your day.

## 4. If You’ll Text Someone You’ve Been Missing

![If You’ll Text Someone You’ve Been Missing](https://img.allw.mn/content/sq/de/xao1kjzo5630e8b382512309870164_500x256.gif)

Even if you're planning on staying home all day, you don't have to be antisocial. You have a phone that's capable of reaching anyone in the world.

## 5. If You’ll Focus on the past or the Future

![If You’ll Focus on the past or the Future](https://img.allw.mn/content/ft/bn/my4hkdix5630e8b36f57b463080539_499x285.gif)

If you worry about how much you embarrassed yourself yesterday, you'll never be able to enjoy today. That's why you should focus on the present instead of the past.

## 6. If You’ll Be Appreciative of What You Have

![If You’ll Be Appreciative of What You Have](https://img.allw.mn/content/iq/aa/fpk2y6st5630e8b56fb00815899534_500x256.gif)

You shouldn't go through life, assuming you have it bad. Be thankful for the good things you've been given.

## 7. If You’ll Be Optimistic

![If You’ll Be Optimistic](https://img.allw.mn/content/le/rh/ukzye9u35630e8b3d7e59189970654_500x201.gif)

Don't assume that your day will go poorly. If you're optimistic, your positive attitude could make your day go better than ever.

## 8. If You’ll do a Good Deed

![If You’ll do a Good Deed](https://img.allw.mn/content/ud/so/r2jlmq2y5630e8b44d509315519478_500x212.gif)

You should care about more than just yourself. That's why you should promise yourself you'll do a good deed for someone else sometime during the day.

## 9. If You’ll Watch Tv or Be Productive

![If You’ll Watch Tv or Be Productive](https://img.allw.mn/content/l9/c5/n7sgbt015630e8b4d73d1552471691_500x270.gif)

Netflix is tempting, but it isn't going to help you reach your career goals. Do some work instead.

## 10. If You’ll Waste Money or Make Money

![If You’ll Waste Money or Make Money](https://img.allw.mn/content/ev/ep/hfkmuk7b5630e8b4d3114740214195_500x281.gif)

You could either fill out applications for a high paying job, or you could waste money shopping for shoes. The choice is up to you.

## 11. If You’ll do the Dishes or Procrastinate

![If You’ll do the Dishes or Procrastinate](https://img.allw.mn/content/zj/l9/aw3fd23x5630e8b5145b2838056276_500x281.gif)

You could either leave your dishes in the sink or actually wash them all. Then you won't have to worry about it later.

## 12. If You’ll Drink Water or Alcohol

![If You’ll Drink Water or Alcohol](https://img.allw.mn/content/d6/wx/vc28flss5630e8b5d00fb006628352_500x281.gif)

Alcohol is tempting, especially when you're in a bad mood, but you should swap it for water. It's ten times healthier.

## 13. If You’ll Make a Plan or Go with the Flow

![If You’ll Make a Plan or Go with the Flow](https://img.allw.mn/content/yh/n8/r5rk222p5630e8b5c066d914417749_245x195.gif)

When you wake up, you can either plan out your day or just go with the flow. Of course, it's always easier to accomplish goals when you have a set plan.

## 14. If You’ll Get Dressed or Stay in Pjs

![If You’ll Get Dressed or Stay in Pjs](https://img.allw.mn/content/fz/y7/hg0bv07y5630e8b66d56a174402473_400x226.gif)

You could stay in your pajamas all day long, or you could get dressed. The latter will help you be more productive later on in the day, so choose wisely.

## 15. If You’ll Read or Waste Time on Your Phone

![If You’ll Read or Waste Time on Your Phone](https://img.allw.mn/content/d4/tv/lpitbo945630e8b671059950939046_500x208.gif)

The television and the internet aren't the only forms of entertainment. You could try reading for a change.

## 16. If You’ll Be Mature or Act like a Child

![If You’ll Be Mature or Act like a Child](https://img.allw.mn/content/zs/lp/r07o7l635630e8b66c295647351980_433x239.gif)

Are you going to deal with your problems like an adult, or are you going to whine about your life all day without doing anything to change it?

## 17. If You’ll Put in Effort or Let the Day Go to Waste

![If You’ll Put in Effort or Let the Day Go to Waste](https://img.allw.mn/content/hg/eh/muuu54fm5630e8b6a2c07644634181_500x222.gif)

Every day is loaded with opportunities. It's up to you to take advantage of them.

Don't let your days go to waste! What big choice are you going to make today?
