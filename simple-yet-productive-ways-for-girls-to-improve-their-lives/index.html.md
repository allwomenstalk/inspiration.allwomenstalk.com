# 13 Simple Yet Productive Ways for Girls to Improve  Their Lives ...

Life can be hard at times. Daily stresses and worries
can all add up, and make you feel like you have no control. At times like these it's important to have a positive mindset, and believe that there are ways to improve your life. You are the one who determines your happiness and it’s your actions and thinking that will contribute to it. Here are 13 simple ways to improve your life, as advised by careergirldaily.com. These simple yet productive steps will help you along the way to self-improvement.

## 1. Slow down

![beauty, fashion, screenshot,](https://img.allw.mn/content/re/pe/gg4qmslk5943dcb45528c217252122_500x281.gif)

Life in the fast lane can be exhausting and drain you of all your energy. Even worse than that it makes you not appreciate what you have. Hold back, slow down and simply enjoy the people in your life.

## 2. Declutter Your Life

![human hair color, blond, human action, person, mouth,](https://img.allw.mn/content/x5/ey/l28xbz605944fd79a916c230383798_400x226.gif)

The art of decluttering your space and mind is a very fine one. Decluttering can help you achieving long-term goals while freeing up some space in your brain!

## 3. Step Away from Negative People

![screenshot,](https://img.allw.mn/content/vi/kt/q39k02t25944e83021ef5007842836_500x268.gif)

If you want to be happy you need to surround yourself with people that only want the best for you. Say bye to the people that let you down and only bring negativity in your life.

## 4. Start a Journal

![human action, person, beauty, long hair, romance,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ik/ie/y4w9c6l25944e8d3d5e78500411586_500x280.gif)

A journal can be a girl’s best friend. If you’re happy, upset, vulnerable just take it all out on your personal diary: it will always be there to “listen” to you.

## 5. Enjoy Food

![face, black and white, nose, photography, monochrome photography,](https://img.allw.mn/content/g2/a7/dzyuy4i55944fdf0dd386764911760_500x280.gif)

Even if it can be tempting when the workload is high, never skip meals! Enjoy healthy meals and cook foods that are good for your mind, body, and soul.

## 6. Show Gratitude

![,](https://img.allw.mn/content/e4/re/e2l9rhxw5944ea68b2434751212888_480x359.gif)

Appreciate what you have and show the world you're grateful for all your experiences. What might look like not a big deal to you might be someone else’s lifetime dream so make sure to appreciate all the little things.

## 7. Treat Your Body Right

![human action, person, dance, performing arts, sports,](https://img.allw.mn/content/xx/km/fra7o6na5944ea803fc65926225168_500x281.gif)

If feeling more confident about the way you look can make a big improvement in your life then be the change you want to see. Eat healthily and exercise you will soon see the outcome of all your sacrifices.

## 8. Smile More

![hair, human hair color, face, clothing, blond,](https://img.allw.mn/content/be/dm/ltx6tx915944eaa141825942537346_400x238.gif)

Positivity brings more positivity. See what a difference smiling at strangers can have, smile more and you’ll feel more positive!

## 9. Volunteer

![,](https://img.allw.mn/content/cp/bn/yl4t2pa55944fe3e7ef89819925284_500x280.gif)

Giving back is the most fulfilling experience. If you have a little bit of free time in your hands invest it in those people who are in need of it, the elderly, homeless, shelter dogs. It will bring more joy to your life than you ever thought.

## 10. Make Spontaneous Decisions

![screenshot, fictional character,](https://img.allw.mn/content/xh/ly/fj37nwlz5944ee202cddf224353825_500x176.gif)

Stop overthinking and trust your gut, it will help you live a lighter life with a lighter head.

## 11. Treat Yourself

![photography, beauty, emotion, photo shoot, model,](https://img.allw.mn/content/fu/lx/l9ng8q0q5944ee5328f24177448584_500x281.gif)

Not always, but every once in a while dedicate yourself some solo time and spoil yourself a bit!

## 12. Read More

![person,](https://img.allw.mn/content/n8/tv/wqt2vfw85944febf70408544266343_498x232.gif)

The best way to improve yourself is by learning from people that inspire us. Head over to your favorite bookstore and read the words of brilliant minds.

## 13. Listen to Podcasts

![color, hair, blue, singing, entertainment,](https://img.allw.mn/content/vt/s2/ulisgk545944f0411184a521394173_500x281.gif)

Podcasts are the best way to get a rush of motivation even when you have little time in your hands. Plus, they help you learn and discover new things every day!
