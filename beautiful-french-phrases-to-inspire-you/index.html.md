# 10 Beautiful French Phrases to Inspire You in Life ...

I've got some **beautiful French phrases to inspire you**. How do you plan on inspiring yourself using what they say is the most beautiful language in the world? Take a look at the following phrases as I hope that they will inspire you to reach your goals. Here are some **beautiful French phrases to inspire you** every day.

## 1. “Petit a Petit, L’oiseau Fait Son Nid.”

Little by little, the bird makes its nest.

## 2. “Qui N’avance Pas, Recule.”

Who does not move forward, recedes.

## 3. “Il Vaut Mieux Faire Que Dire.”

Doing is better than saying.

## 4. “La Vie Est Un Sommeil, L’amour En Est Rêve.”

Life is a long sleep and love is its dream.

## 5. “Imaginer C’est Choisir.”

To imagine is to choose.

## 6. “Vivre Sans Aimer N’est Pas Proprement Vivre.”

To live without loving is to not really live.

## 7. “Tous Est Possible.”

Anything is possible.

## 8. “Sois Belle À Ta Faron.”

Be beautiful in your own way.

## 9. “Les Chefs D’oeuvre Ne Sont Jamais Que Des Tentatives Heureuses.”

Masterpieces are never anything else but happy attempts.

## 10. “Qui Vivra Verra.”

He/She who lives shall see.

Practice these inspiring French phrases and who knows? You might be able to utilize these on your next European trip. Au revoir!♥
