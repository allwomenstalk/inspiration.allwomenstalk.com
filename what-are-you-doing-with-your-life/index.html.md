# What Are You Doing with Your Life ?

The question we all ask at the end of each day. **What are you doing with your life**? Now, this question is just a question. Nobody can tell you what you should be doing with your life. It is always your choice. However, we do get so caught up in impressing other people that we forget to impress ourselves.

Some would say it is selfish to impress yourself. You should do what the group wants you to do. Sometimes, the group is wrong and sometimes you have to walk a different path. The group does not know what you should or should not be doing. They might assume like they know. But, they really do not know what you should or should not be doing.

It is hard to go against the group because the group will attack you. Should I impress the group or myself. You can not do both. You don’t have enough time to do that. You have to chase the thing you want.

Everything else is just a waste of time. You know it even if you do not want to admit it. You know that chasing things you do not want is a waste of your time. You know you should try things you want and turn your back on everybody. It is not about pleasing the group. It is about doing what you feel you should do. Even if the group does not agree with the decision. You have to do what you want to do. It will never be easy. But, easy is boring. Do not forget that. So, **what are you doing with your life**?
