# 7 Happy Facts to Make Your Day Better ...

Sometimes when you’re having a bad day, you just need a list of happy facts of life to put a little smile on your face! There are so many amazing things out there in the world, and sometimes we forget about it. So here is a list of 7 happy facts to make your day better!

## 1. Mickey Mouse and Minnie Mouse Got Married in Real Life

The coolest of happy facts is that Mickey and Minnie are married in real life. Wayne Allwine and Russi Taylor are their names, and there is nothing more perfect in life than knowing that the 2 stars Disney was built on are married in real life. Disney World is the happiest place in the world, and the center of that world is married. I love it!

## 2. Cows Have BFFs

That’s right ladies, cows have best friends. Scientists have done research that proves that cows have best cow friends, and when their best cow friends aren’t around and they haven’t seen them for a while, the cow will get depressed. So the next time you see two cows eating from the same batch of hay together, smile! Because now that you know one of the cutest happy facts about cows, you’re bound to have a smile spread to your face.

## 3. Blind People Smile, despite Never Having Seen One

This is without a doubt one of the most beautiful happy facts of life. It’s so amazing to know that a smile for us is instinctual. If we feel happy, we smile. There’s nothing else to it!

## 4. A Group of Weasels is Called a Confusion

One of the happy facts of life is that you can call a group of weasels a confusion, a group of flamingos is called a flamboyance, and a group of jellyfish is called a smack. This is just that much cooler than a school of fish, but not quite as cool as a grumble of pugs. That’s right, a group of pugs is called a grumble!

## 5. Otters Hold Hands when Sleeping

This is so they don’t drift apart while sleeping. This is perhaps one of the cutest happy facts I’ve ever read! And all otters are born being able to float thanks to the oils in their fur! So while baby otters are practically helpless on their own, they will never sink or drown!

## 6. Spiders Can’t Fly

I don’t know about you, but I’m terrified of spiders, absolutely petrified. Then if you stop to think about the fact that those little guys can’t fly, it is without a doubt one of the best happy facts about life, ever. Spiders don’t fly. Thank goodness!

## 7. Puffins Mate for Life

In addition to puffins, there’s also all these animals that mate for life: penguins, albatrosses, wolves, swans (who also do that cool thing with their necks that make a heart together), beavers, bald eagles, barn owls, and French angelfish. There’s more too, just look it up! Knowing that these animals mate for life is one of the more romantic happy facts that makes life better!

Sometimes, all we need to know is that there are some undoubtedly adorable and sweet facts out there that are just downright happy! So next time you’re having a bad day, just remember these awesome happy facts! Tell me, what happy facts make you smile?
