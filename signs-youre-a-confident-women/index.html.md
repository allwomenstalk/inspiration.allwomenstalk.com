# 7 Signs Youre a Confident Woman ...

Confidence is the key to a happy life. That's why you should make it your mission to learn to love yourself. Don't rest until these seven things, brought to us by [Seventeen](https://goo.gl/seIFS5), become true for you:

## 1. You Can Pull off Bold Fashion and Beauty Looks

![You Can Pull off Bold Fashion and Beauty Looks](https://img.allw.mn/content/du/v5/j0y8a8pu562101a6b1393455318455_500x208.gif)

You're brave, because you know you can rock any look. Even if others stare, you just ignore them.

## 2. ​You Know You're Destined for Big Things

![screenshot, WINNER, BABY!,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ng/cb/whfkgszs5851bec15bcab748979117_400x168.gif)

You don't hope that you'll achieve your dreams. You know you will.

## 3. You Genuinely Love Giving Compliments to Other People

![You Genuinely Love Giving Compliments to Other People](https://img.allw.mn/content/w9/j3/vlpx5u4f562101da44b06562662402_500x244.gif)

You feel amazing about yourself, and you want others to feel the same. That's why you always compliment strangers on their outfits.

## 4. Some People Think You're Intimidating

![Some People Think You're Intimidating](https://img.allw.mn/content/rs/gb/ioqwrsne562102190e9df271816984_486x283.gif)

Unfortunately, not everyone is used to confident women. That's why some people are a little scared of you.

## 5. You Aren't Afraid to Raise Your Hand in Class

![You Aren't Afraid to Raise Your Hand in Class](https://img.allw.mn/content/ve/l9/jf4yfbtw56210218d3ca1713201673_500x281.gif)

When you have something to say, you say it. You don't care what others think.

## 6. Your Instagram Reflects What Your Life is Really like

![Your Instagram Reflects What Your Life is Really like](https://img.allw.mn/content/m9/cp/nvkw5msr5621019ede58d925643067_500x275.gif)

Who needs filters? Your face is perfect just the way it is.

## 7. You Love Catching Your Reflexion in the Mirror

![You Love Catching Your Reflexion in the Mirror](https://img.allw.mn/content/zm/js/du96wvh2562101a038e53077004493_500x281.gif)

You know you're sexy, so you don't cringe when you get a glimpse of yourself. You're actually happy with your appearance.

Do you consider yourself to be a confident woman?
