# Whats Complex about You Based on Your Zodiac Sign ...

One of the most interesting things about human beings is that even though we are technically the same species, we are all so completely and utterly different! Unlike animals, we are advanced and evolved enough to be possessive of some really deep and meaningful thoughts and traits, and it our amazing complexity that makes the human race so wonderful. After all, wouldn’t life be so boring if we were all exactly the same? Here is **what makes you complex according to your zodiac sign**.

## 1. Aries

You have a real fierceness about you, and that can often make you very unpredictable and hard to read. People can’t always figure out how you are going to react to any given situation!

## 2. Taurus

Above all else, you love the fact that you are the most responsible person you know. Your complexity is in the way you are able to manage all of the important areas of your life like an absolute pro.

## 3. Gemini

You are complex because you have such a short attention span that people realise that they have to get to the point with you quickly before you completely switch off! It makes you a really interesting challenge!

## 4. Cancer

Complex isn’t really the right word for you, because you like everything to be a predictable and plain sailing as possible. Although, in a way, the ability to always see a light at the end of a tunnel is complex in its own way.

## 5. Leo

You tell it like it is, and you aren’t afraid to hurt a few feelings in the process. This makes you complex because no matter what you say, you never seem to lose any friends or popularity!

## 6. Virgo

You are always trying to be as helpful as you can, and that is a very complex quality in a person because it shows that you care not only about yourself, but very deeply about everyone around you.

## 7. Libra

You are complex because the motives that you have for doing things are usually completely different to the motives that most other people would have. It’s not a bad thing, it’s just a different avenue!

## 8. Scorpio

To your best friends you aren’t complex, you just don’t take fools lightly, which can often come across to strangers as being somewhat distant and mysterious.

## 9. Sagittarius

You are complex because you are always striving to find out what your really want in life. You can never settle on one thing, and people see this as you being super diverse.

## 10. Capricorn

Honestly, you aren’t complex at all. You know what you want, you’ve known forever, and you aren’t prepared to change your point of view for anyone!

## 11. Aquarius

You are so complex and complicated that half the time, you don’t even know what your motivations are, let alone other people knowing!

## 12. Pisces

On the outside you look and seem as calm as a small stream, but inside the complexity and layers and racing inside you like a full river!
