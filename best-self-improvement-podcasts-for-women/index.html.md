# 11 Best Self Improvement Podcasts for Girls Who Want to Better Themselves ...

I know I’m late to the game, but recently, I’ve been listening to podcasts nonstop! Lately I've been looking for the **best self-improvement podcasts.** In the car, while I fall asleep, while I walk the dog, while I run, in the shower. I love listening to podcasts! I love that I can listen to podcasts for basically all of my interests, including Harry Potter, current news, feminism, and self-care and improvement. Here are some of the **best self-improvement podcasts.**

## 1. Earn Your Happy

![pink, text, purple, font, product,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fy/z8/hp1jupfd59d5dd75ec796263475116_600x600.jpg)

This awesome podcast, hosted by self-proclaimed “self love junkie” is a great listen for the girl who wants to take action and live her best life. It's definitely one of the **best self-improvement podcasts** I've ever found.
[loriharder.com](https://loriharder.com/podcast/)

## 2. The Chasing Joy Podcast

_ Hosted by Georgie Morley, this podcast seeks to encourage the modern woman to find that which brings her joy. Georgie uses wit, humor, and personal stories to inspire her listeners.
init4thelongrun.com_

## 3. Happiness Podcast

![yellow, leisure, water, summer, vacation,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qa/og/wp5jt14o59d5dd3e170b1696778054_600x600.jpg)

This podcast looks in to the psychology of being happy. Dr. Robert Puff, PhD, gives specific examples and actions we can take to live a happy life.
[happinesspodcast.org](https://www.happinesspodcast.org/)

## 4. Style Your Mind

![hair coloring, forehead, black hair, long hair, product,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lk/y9/nbxdi4of59d5dd3f12a34679048127_500x328.jpg)

Another awesome podcast hosted by a kickass lady. Cara Alwill Leyba speaks to the woman who wants to jumpstart her life and reinvent herself with candor and wit.
[caraalwill.com](https://caraalwill.com/podcast/)

## 5. The Daily Meditation Podcast

_ This is an awesome daily podcast for stepping back and taking a deep breath. There’s a different meditation every day based on different themes, and most are less than ten minutes!
sipandom.com_

## 6. The Mindful Kind

_ The Mindful Kind, hosted by Rachael Kable, seeks to help its listeners become more mindful in meaningful and fun ways.
rachaelkable.com_

## 7. Happier with Gretchen Rubin

![text, yellow, font, logo, product,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/if/ii/zmxhh33k59d5dd423a7ef564991687_600x600.jpg)

This is one of my personal favorites to listen to. Gretchen Rubin is the author of several New York Times bestselling books, and she hosts this podcast with her sister. Her podcast has been downloaded millions of times, and after giving it a listen, you’ll understand why!
[gretchenrubin.com](https://gretchenrubin.com/podcasts/)

## 8. Nutrition Diva

![face, facial expression, text, cartoon, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hp/tf/zqxjokud59d5dd4283c75685979539_600x600.jpg)

Hosted by Quick and Dirty tips, a network of podcasts that cover several topics, Nutrition Diva is perfect for the girl who is trying to live a healthy lifestyle. The Nutrition Diva seeks to make the intimidating subject of nutrition less scary and more manageable.
[quickanddirtytips.com](https://www.quickanddirtytips.com/nutrition-diva)

## 9. Beyond the to do List

![blue, text, product, font, logo,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u8/o4/bfbw5r7h59d5dd424c5c8292351758_600x600.jpg)

Are you a girl who, like me, wants to be more productive? This podcast explores strategies and ideas that help you do just that!
[beyondthetodolist.com](https://beyondthetodolist.com/)

## 10. Good Life Project

![text, yellow, font, line, logo,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hq/d5/ttefjny759d5dd4377c44373008572_500x500.jpg)

“Don’t try to be different. Accept the fact that you already are.” This is a line from the Good Life Creed. Good Life Project aims to lift you up and inspire you to live the very best and fulfilling life that you possibly can.
[goodlifeproject.com](https://www.goodlifeproject.com/)

## 11. Tiny Leaps, Big Changes

![text, orange, font, line, area,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sp/a2/k4td293459d5dd4537753994056596_600x600.jpg)

This podcast looks at how the seemingly small choices we make every day have a big impact on our entire life. It gives ideas about how you can make small changes in your day to day life, and watch them blossom into big changes in your life.
[greggclunis.com](https://greggclunis.com/)
