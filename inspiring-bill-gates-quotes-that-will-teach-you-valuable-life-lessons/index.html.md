# 7 Inspiring Bill Gates Quotes That Will Teach You Valuable Life Lessons ...

If you are an entrepreneur, you want to become an entrepreneur or you simply need some motivation, then you’ve come to the right place because here are some very inspiring Bill Gates quotes that will help you reach your goals and fulfill your dreams. Billionaire, philanthropist and entrepreneur, Bill Gates is one of the most inspiring people out there. He has worked tirelessly to improve other people’s lives through his generous charitable contributions. He and his wife Melinda have donated billions of dollars to different charities and also, to all kinds of pressing causes around the world, making this planet a much better and safer place. Here are 7 inspiring Bill Gates quotes that will teach you some valuable life lessons:

## 1. Success

![Success](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zn/xs/gdzz930i_600x337.jpg)

In my opinion, this is one of those famous Bill Gates quotes that you should keep in mind especially if you’re an entrepreneur. Yes, being successful can make you feel like you’re on top of the world, like you are invincible, but don’t ignore the fact that you may be wrong sometimes. We all make mistakes since we all have flaws, but you should be aware of yours so they don’t sabotage you and prevent you from reaching your goals.

## 2. Failure

![Failure](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yd/fc/m4oh5bwz_600x332.jpg)

Don’t be afraid to fail, because failure can be one of the best teachers you’ll ever have. Just learn from your mistakes and move on. Don’t let yourself get discouraged if you couldn’t reach one of your goals - see what kept you from being successful and fix it. Celebrate your successes but don’t ignore your failures because they can teach you some of the most valuable lessons.

## 3. Patience

![Patience](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fl/z3/ar43hg66_340x270.jpg)

Patience really is the key to success. If you’re the owner of a small business and you don’t succeed right away, you shouldn’t be discouraged. Keep following your dreams and do everything in your power to reach all your goals. Don’t let yourself be intimidated by all the obstacles that will appear in your path! Be brave and don’t give up because you will definitely be successful someday!

## 4. Comparison

![Comparison](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ki/og/yi8kors5_600x332.jpg)

Don’t compare yourself with the people around you. Instead, you could compare yourself with a younger version of you and you’ll realize how much progress you’ve made down the years. Just keep going and pursue your dreams no matter what!

## 5. Learning

![Learning](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u4/rb/jh5uhu0y_600x375.jpg)

You should always value a customer’s complaints because they are a rich source of learning. This way, you will know what you can do to improve service quality at your business, how to succeed in your field and most importantly, how to make your clients happy. A happy customer is a satisfied customer and you should always take into account their opinions since they will give you some pretty valuable feedback.

## 6. Be Nice

![Be Nice](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qw/ng/r3ynzdjg_600x419.jpg)

You should be nice to everyone you meet, not only to nerds. Even though many nerds are described as being shy, unattractive and quirky, you should know that they are dedicated and intelligent people who work really hard and who will do everything in their power to succeed in their field. So, don’t be a bully, be a nerd!

## 7. Feedback

_ If you want to improve your skills and become better at what you do, then you need to ask for feedback. Don’t take it personally though, Try to understand if that person is right and then follow their advice.

Bill Gates is one of the most inspiring people four our generation and he is actually a role-model for a lot of people. Do you know any other inspiring Bill Gates quotes? Please share them with us in the comments section!
