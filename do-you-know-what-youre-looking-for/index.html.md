# Do You Know What Youre Looking for ?

**Do you know what you're looking for?** I don’t know what I am looking for. Sometimes, we think we know, but we really don’t. What you are looking for depends on what you really want. It is going to change as the years go by. You might want to be somebody, but you don’t want to be anybody. You might want to work, but you don’t want to work anywhere.

You might want to be something great, but you don’t want to be great for people. You might want a reward, but you want a reward that matters. You might want to impress the whole world, but you really want to impress a small group of people.

I look for what I want. I do not know what that is. But, if you were to put enough thought into it, you will know what you want. You have to learn how to decide or you will be like the majority of people who just look for popular items that provide nothing. You are not looking for one thing. You are looking for the objects, people, or experiences that make you appreciate why you're alive. That contradicts a lot of things that you were told to be considered successful. Sometimes, you have to realize that you do not want to be a part of the group because you don’t like the group’s decision. Accept what you are looking for. **Do you know what you're looking for?**
