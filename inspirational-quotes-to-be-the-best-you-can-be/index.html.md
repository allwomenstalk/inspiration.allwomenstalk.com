# 7 Inspirational Quotes to Be the Best You Can Be ...

There are countless inspirational quotes to be the best you can be. They give you motivation to better yourself, and to make your life worth living. By reading them, you’re gaining small pieces of wisdom from bright minds. Here are some famous, inspirational quotes to be the best you can be.

## 1. Possibilities

![Possibilities](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/01184217_5855_599x448.jpg)

Audrey Hepburn gave one of the most inspirational quotes to be the best you can be. She said, “Nothing is impossible, the word itself says 'I'm possible'!” You have to believe that you’re capable of anything. If you let your pessimistic thoughts get to you, you’ll be negatively affected. Remember that you can do anything that you want to do. Never give up on something because you think that it’s impossible.

## 2. Society

![Society](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/01184309_9757_600x400.jpg)

Gandhi said, “You must be the change you wish to see in the world.” You can’t control how the rest of the world behaves, but you’re in charge of yourself. Don’t fight fire with fire. If you’re annoyed by how rude everyone around you is, keep being nice. Don’t give in to society, especially when you know that they’re behaving poorly.

## 3. Movement

![Movement](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/01184356_1883_600x450.jpg)

Will Rogers said, “Even if you’re on the right track, you’ll get run over if you just sit there.” You have to be on a constant mission to better your life. You can’t get to a good place in life, and then refuse to move forward. You have to keep moving, and keep trying to be the best person that you can be. Never sit still, or you’ll eventually get left behind.

## 4. Beginnings

![Beginnings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/01184432_4045_564x640.jpg)

Nido Qubein said, “Your present circumstances don't determine where you can go; they merely determine where you start.” Some people believe that they’re unable to do something because of their financial or educational situation. However, your current situation doesn’t determine what the rest of your life will be like. You’re always going to be moving forward, and the present is your starting point. Remember that the beginning is the hardest part, and things will only get better.

## 5. Suffering

![Suffering](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/01184537_7886_600x543.jpg)

Muhammad Ali said, “I hated every minute of training, but I said, 'Don't quit. Suffer now and live the rest of your life as a champion.'” Life contains plenty of difficult moments. You have to get through the tough times in order to make it to the wonderful times. Nothing worthwhile comes easily. You have to fight to get where you want in life.

## 6. Failure

![Failure](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/01184639_0003_600x400.jpg)

Thomas Edison said, “I have not failed. I’ve just found 10,000 ways that won’t work.” This quote is all about thinking positive. It would be easy for Edison to think of his 10,000 attempts as mistakes, but he views them as a learning experience. There’s no doubt that you’re going to mess up time and time again. However, it’s the way you look at your attempts that determines what you gain from them.

## 7. Life

![Life](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/01184710_0595_600x450.jpg)

Mahatma Gandhi also said, “Live as if you were to die tomorrow. Learn as if you were to live forever.” You should always strive to fill your brain with as much information as possible. Learning is a beautiful thing that the human mind is capable of doing. Likewise, life is a special thing that should be cherished. Live your life fully, so that the experience is worth it.

You’re in charge of how you behave, and how you react to certain situations. You have the power to make sure that you’re the best person that you can be. Do you know of any quotes that aren’t on the list?
