# Inspirational Quotes on Being Yourself ...

I think we all need some **inspirational quotes on being yourself**. In the modern world, there is always pressure to fit a certain mold and to be a cookie cutter version someone else. In this post, we’ve compiled our favourite quotes to inspire you to have confidence in who you are. So, here are the best **inspirational quotes on being yourself**.

## 1. Maya Angelou

“You may not control all the events that happen to you, but you can decide not to be reduced by them.”

## 2. Lady Gaga

“Don't you ever let a soul in the world tell you that you can't be exactly who you are.”

## 3. Rumi

“Let yourself be drawn by the stronger pull of that which you truly love.”

## 4. John Grisham

“Don't compromise yourself - you're all you have.”

## 5. Coco Chanel

“A girl should be two things: who and what she wants.”

## 6. Frank Zappa

“If you end up with a boring miserable life because you listened to your mom, your dad, your teacher, your priest, or some guy on television telling you how to do your shit, then you deserve it.”

## 7. Bernard M. Maruch

“Be who you are and say what you feel, because those who mind don’t matter, and those who matter don’t mind.”

## 8. Marilyn Monroe

"Imperfection is beauty, madness is genius and it’s better to be absolutely ridiculous than absolutely boring.”

## 9. Roy T. Bennett

“Follow your heart, listen to your inner voice, stop caring about what others think.”

## 10. Jaeda Dewalt

“Don’t let others box you into their idea of what they think you should be. A confined identity is a miserable way to exist. Be you and live free. Trust that in living true to yourself, you will attract people that support and love you, just as you are.”
