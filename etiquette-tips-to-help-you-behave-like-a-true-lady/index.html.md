# 7 Etiquette Tips to Help You Behave like a True Lady ...

If you’re headed to a fancy dinner party, there are etiquette tips that you should keep in mind. When you’re eating with your close friends, you can behave however you’d like, but when you’re invited to a nice dinner, you should try to behave properly. Wear your best clothes, never forget to smile, and follow these etiquette tips:

## 1. Follow Silverware Rules

If the table is set up with multiple forks and spoons, you can’t grab whichever one you want. You should always start with the piece of silverware that is farthest away from your plate, and work your way toward the inside. The first fork should be for salad, the next for dinner. When it comes to your drink, it should always be placed on the right side of your plate. As long as you follow these etiquette tips, you’ll have no problem when it comes to utensils.

## 2. Always Be Polite

You should never speak when your mouth is filled with food. If you have something to say, finish chewing before you start your story. You should also remember to cut your food one piece at a time, instead of cutting it all apart at once.

## 3. Turn Electronics off

If you’re at a fancy dinner party, you wouldn’t want your phone to buzz beneath the table. Answering it would be rude, so you might as well turn off your phone or keep it on silent. Keeping this in mind, you don’t want to text while the meal is being served. Focus on the other guests, your host, or your food. Do anything to keep away your temptation to text.

## 4. Exhibit Proper Body Language

Your body language is important if you want to look refined. You should keep your elbows off of the table and your back straight. No resting your feet up on your chair or the table. When you unfold your napkin, place it over your lap. When the meal is complete, place the napkin on the table, instead of on the plate or leaving it on your chair.

## 5. Pass Food Properly

You should pass your food from left to right. Never reach across the table to hand food to someone. You risk spilling whatever is placed between you, and it appears clumsy. To be safe, just pass the food to your left and let someone else do the work.

## 6. Bring a Gift

When you’re invited to a party, it’s polite to bring some [type of food](https://food.allwomenstalk.com/cuisines-of-the-world/) or drink. You don’t have to buy the most expensive wine on the shelf. Even if you bring something cheap or make it yourself, it at least shows that you’re making an effort, and that’s all that matters.

## 7. Send a Thank You Note

Once the party ends, you’ll want to send your hostess a thank you note. It doesn’t have to be long and detailed. You can simply thank her for inviting you over, and compliment the food that she served. As long as you show your appreciation, she’ll be thankful.

There’s no need to follow these rules when you’re lounging around with your buddies. However, at a fancy dinner party, you should remember the proper way to behave. Do you have any additional tips on what to do during an elaborate party?
