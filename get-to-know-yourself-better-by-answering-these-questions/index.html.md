# Get to Know Yourself Better by Answering These 17 Questions ...

It's hard to believe, but you probably don't know yourself as well as you should. Instead of running from the truth, you should try to figure out what makes you tick. It might be uncomfortable, but you should ask yourself these questions to get to know yourself better:

## 1. What Would You do if You Couldnt Fail?

![What Would You do if You Couldnt Fail?](https://img.allw.mn/content/os/qk/fgtus0sj56685229d76a8281389531_500x281.gif)

If you knew you wouldn't fail, would you apply for a certain job or ask out a certain man? Well, then you should do it anyway!

## 2. What Are You Passionate about?

![What Are You Passionate about?](https://img.allw.mn/content/hv/cf/y0mblrn7566852288b46e012677238_490x230.gif)

If you love to paint, then never stop. Your passion is important.

## 3. Who do You Want to Impress?

![person, child,](https://img.allw.mn/content/jg/g0/deby2yv558886a368d16b781553536_500x300.gif)

Everyone wants to impress someone. Who are you trying to impress? Is it your crush, your parents, or yourself?

## 4. Who do You Love the Most?

![Who do You Love the Most?](https://img.allw.mn/content/m2/t7/xxiq8g5f5668522a0d2df902870001_500x341.gif)

If you love your friends most of all, make sure you spend as much time with them as you can. It'll increase your happiness.

## 5. What Are Your Goals?

![What Are Your Goals?](https://img.allw.mn/content/d4/c7/q7tp4b28566852293f0eb429483364_500x281.gif)

Create a list of goals for next year, then for the next five years, then for life overall.

## 6. What Are You Most Proud of?

![What Are You Most Proud of?](https://img.allw.mn/content/a7/vk/nxclu1qs566852b02c03e266719258_500x294.gif)

If helping others makes you proud, then you should think about a career working with the underprivileged. Either that, or you should try volunteering.

## 7. What Are Your Priorities in Life?

![What Are Your Priorities in Life?](https://img.allw.mn/content/n2/k1/v2zyczui566852297c69f640724332_300x167.gif)

Is your job your top priority, or is your love life? Whatever you care about the most is the thing you should focus on.

## 8. What Would You Spend 1 Million Dollars on?

![hair, face, person, nose, hairstyle,](https://img.allw.mn/content/lz/kp/byip9is258886a36b0ba7973168403_500x250.gif)

If you won the lottery, what would you do with the money? It helps to visualize what your life would be like if you could do whatever you wanted.

## 9. What do You Regret?

![What do You Regret?](https://img.allw.mn/content/mn/u4/idrzpkex56685229cb94d081114128_500x275.gif)

You can't change your past. Of course, if you regret not taking a chance early on in life, it'll push you to take more chances in the future.

## 10. Do You like Your Family?

![Do You like Your Family?](https://img.allw.mn/content/e3/gd/cmfndsp35668522aac892363203670_500x257.gif)

You don't actually have to keep in touch with unhealthy people. If you're happier away from your parents, move out as soon as you can.

## 11. Do You like Your Job?

![person, staff, military officer, military person, official,](https://img.allw.mn/content/r7/ll/fquz2i7i58886a3808e7b104740156_400x215.gif)

If you hate it, then you need to work on finding a career that would suit you better.

## 12. What Would You Tell Your past Self?

![What Would You Tell Your past Self?](https://img.allw.mn/content/fc/dk/d55d9lag5668522a89641060106073_500x299.gif)

You can't tell your younger self anything, but if you could, what would you say? If you'd tell her to relax and enjoy life, then you should probably be doing the same now.

## 13. What Would You Want to Know from Future Self?

![What Would You Want to Know from Future Self?](https://img.allw.mn/content/so/ku/hhwt5bj65668522aad93c968903430_500x281.gif)

Would you want to know what job you'd have, if you'd get married, or where you'd live? The question you'd ask can tell you a lot about your priorities.

## 14. What Bad Habits do You Want to Break?

![What Bad Habits do You Want to Break?](https://img.allw.mn/content/db/o9/rzzxm69f5668522b4aa5a667971243_500x280.gif)

We all have unhealthy habits we need to break. If you put your mind to it, you can do it.

## 15. What Good Habits do You Want to Develop?

![What Good Habits do You Want to Develop?](https://img.allw.mn/content/o1/gn/do85scoz5668522b157ae620396988_500x356.gif)

It's never too late to alter your personality. Think about the qualities you admire in others and try to replicate them.

## 16. What’s Your Purpose?

It's impossible to know what your true purpose in life is. Of course, you can take a guess.

## 17. Do You like Yourself?

![Do You like Yourself?](https://img.allw.mn/content/ya/jv/ksi43k5k5668522b9f539140979933_500x215.gif)

This is the most important question of all. If your answer is "no," then figure out why you don't like yourself. That way, you can work on changing yourself.

It's important to know yourself. It's the only way you'll be able to figure out how to live your best life. Are you able to answer all of these questions?
