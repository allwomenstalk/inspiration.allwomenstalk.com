# 19 Everyday Things to Love about Being a Woman ...

Women are magical creatures, not because we are perfect but because we are who we are. Every woman in the world is uniquely special and stunningly beautiful. Sometimes it's easy to forget how awesome we are because we are constantly faced with critiques and judgmental opinions designed to steal that confidence from us. Don't let that happen. Every day, love one thing about yourself – just one. Soon, you'll find more and more things to adore about being a woman. Here's a start.

## 1. How Well You Listen to Other People

![How Well You Listen to Other People](https://img.allw.mn/content/no/i4/jhme6uie56e9c40a137e2426831234_500x274.gif)

No, but seriously. As women, we might miss something when we're busy, multitasking, or admittedly not that interested in the conversation, but when someone you love, respect, or care for needs you, you're right there with a willing ear and an open heart.

## 2. Even when You Have an Ego, You Know How to Check It

![Even when You Have an Ego, You Know How to Check It](https://img.allw.mn/content/wg/o6/kln29fqa56e9c40aefc64586957571_500x281.gif)

## 3. We Are Masters of Multitasking

![We Are Masters of Multitasking](https://img.allw.mn/content/vy/bb/qn8pzwg756e9c40b2fcc0097279253_500x281.gif)

My shower routine is proof of this all by itself.

## 4. Our Communication Skills Are Second to None

![Our Communication Skills Are Second to None](https://img.allw.mn/content/qs/fy/l25zxtvp56e9c40abbf0c731482551_500x285.gif)

Sometimes we are passive aggressive and sometimes we are direct, but one way or another, we will always communicate our feelings. Always.

## 5. Our Wardrobes Are Amazing

![Our Wardrobes Are Amazing](https://img.allw.mn/content/cn/tz/ah7wh4dd56e9c40b6d501409253506_500x260.gif)

Even if your closet consists of a pair of jeans, three tee shirts, and a denim jacket, it is awesome. Why? Because those are your clothes and you know how to rock them like nobody's business.
