# 7 Quotes from Ralph Waldo Emerson That Will Change the Way You Think ...

If you have read some of the quotes from Ralph Waldo Emerson, you know how eye-opening they can be. Emerson was a highly educated man who graduated from Harvard and later became a licensed minister. Due to his education, his beliefs, and his innate wisdom, Emerson left a great mark on society with his words. Reading quotes from Ralph Waldo Emerson can be both inspirational and life affirming, particularly if you really listen to his advice.

## 1. Life Experiments

![Life Experiments](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oh/nt/i7cx91f5_600x600.jpg)

One of the quotes from Ralph Waldo Emerson that will make you think differently about life is, “All life is an experiment. The more experiments you make the better.” Looking at life as an experiment puts you in a position to try many new things without fear. If what you tried didn’t work, you can simply tweak the “experiment” or move to the next one. The more you experiment, the more you will experience, which make your life much richer.

## 2. Anger

![Anger](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m7/j9/hgih5ov1_550x480.jpg)

Everyone gets angry at some point, and some people get angry more often than others. While anger can be justified, it is not healthy. Emerson pointed out that, “For every minute you remain angry, you give up sixty seconds of peace of mind.” Being angry does not make you feel better; in the end it only hurts you. It is best to let go of your anger; you will feel much more at peace.

## 3. Fear

![Fear](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fn/mc/w0d2guqf_600x600.jpg)

How often have you let fear stop you from doing something? Hopefully not very often, but because fear can be paralyzing, it may be more frequent than you would want. If fear has you stopped in your tracks, try following Emerson’s advice to “Always do what you are afraid to do.” While this advice can be difficult to put into practice, it can help you try new things and get over fears that are holding you back.

## 4. Too Late

![Too Late](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/05/uh/xyjbn0ky_480x500.jpg)

Unfortunately, life does not provide an infinite number of opportunities. This is why Emerson declared, “You cannot do a kindness too soon, for you never know how soon it will be too late.” As you go through life, try to remember that it is finite, and always take the opportunity to say thank you and tell someone you love them. You don’t want to have missed your chance.

## 5. Potential

![Potential](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yr/du/hqiitgs7_500x520.jpg)

Emerson emphasized, “Our chief want is someone who will inspire us to be what we know we could be.” Everyone wants to reach their full potential, but sometimes a little help is needed. Coaches, teachers, and mentors have the ability to make you reach your full potential by pushing you to do your best. Don’t shy away from them. Seek them out!

## 6. Individuality

![Individuality](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bz/ko/cuknimzm_500x490.jpg)

Peer pressure and conformity are two things that are very difficult to avoid, especially if you are in high school. However, Emerson stresses that, “To be yourself in a world that is constantly trying to make you something else is the greatest accomplishment.” Even though it can be very difficult, it is always best to stay true to who you are and stick to your beliefs.

## 7. Sunshine

![Sunshine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n6/rt/md4cwcgu_600x818.jpg)

Emerson was known for his belief in having a connection with nature. This belief is evident in his statement, “Live in the sunshine, swim in the sea, drink the wild air.” Daily life in the twenty-first century is largely spent indoors. However, getting outside in the fresh air is incredibly important. It can make you feel alive, which is why you should try and spend time outside every day.

Ralph Waldo Emerson was extremely insightful, and he left us with many wise words. His quotes can change the way you think, and they can help inspire you to live your best life. Which of Emerson’s quotes do you find to be the most inspirational?
