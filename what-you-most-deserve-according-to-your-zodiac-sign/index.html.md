# What You Most Deserve According to Your Zodiac Sign ...

Want to know **what you most deserve according to your zodiac sign**? No matter who you are and no matter what you do, everybody deserves something in life. The interesting thing, though, is that not all people are searching for the same things, and not all people are deserving of the same kindnesses or impacts in their life. If you are a believer in astrology, then you will know that the stars can have a lot more to say about the things you deserve in life than you might be aware of. To help you see what I’m talking about, here is a run down of **what you most deserve according to your zodiac sign**.

## 1. Gemini

You deserve true happiness. You are so good at putting on a false front that even you forget sometimes that you don’t feel all that fine.

## 2. Cancer

You do a lot of things for a lot of people, but your quiet nature means those things often go unacknowledged. What you deserve is true acknowledgment for your good deeds.

## 3. Leo

People don’t always take you seriously because you like to live a life of the party lifestyle, but deep down you deserve just as much respect as the most serious person in the room.

## 4. Virgo

You have an issue with questioning your self-worth a lot, but what you need to understand is that you are just as deserving of confidence as anyone else. You deserve to feel good enough.

## 5. Libra

You are very conscious of all of your responsibilities, but you also need to realise that you deserve to treat yourself once in a while!

## 6. Scorpio

You have no problem giving out compliments to others, but for some reason, you can’t accept them for yourself. You deserve self-love, you deserve to like the person that you see every time you look in the mirror.

## 7. Sagittarius

You are somebody who has the tendency to suffer from anxiety in a lot of different forms, so what you deserve the most is the ability to feel safe wherever you are and not always see the worst in things.

## 8. Capricorn

This is generally a stressful time of the year for a Capricorn, so more than anything right now you probably deserve a nice big soothing hug!

## 9. Aquarius

You are so focused on helping other people achieve their goals that you often leave yours behind. You deserve to reach your dreams just as much as them.

## 10. Pisces

Your life is pretty much nonstop and you are more than happy to live it that way, but sometimes you just need to sit back and take a few breaths. You deserve a nice drink and a moment of relaxation with your nearest and dearest!

## 11. Aries

You are constantly on the move and doing what you can to help others, so what you deserve most of all is good, uninterrupted rest!

## 12. Taurus

No matter how hard you try, you can’t seem to be able to leave the past in the past, so what you deserve most is the ability to achieve closure on sticky situations.
