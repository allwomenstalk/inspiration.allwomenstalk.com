# 7 Inspiring Quotes from New Girl about Life ...

As silly as the show is, there are quotes from New Girl that can make you feel better about life. The show is hysterical, but deals with realistic problems. If you love the show, you know that it’s capable of making you smile during a rough day. If you’ve never seen the show, you can still enjoy these quotes from New Girl.

## 1. Life

One of the quotes from New Girl that can help you get through the day was said by Nick. He said, “Life sucks! And then it gets better, and then it sucks again.” Life won’t let you be constantly happy, but it won’t let you be constantly sad either. There’s always going to be a fluctuation in your emotions. Whenever you’re upset, remember that something is going to happen to make your happiness return. It’s only a matter of time before things change from horrible to fantastic.

## 2. Pain

Cece once said, “You got hurt. That doesn’t mean you stop trying.” If your boyfriend breaks up with you or you get fired from your job, you can’t let it stop you from going after what you really want. You have to learn from your pain; you can’t let it destroy you. Everyone gets hurt in life, but the happiest people are the ones who don’t let it get them down forever.

## 3. Personality

“We’re weirdos. But that’s who we are. And that’s fine.” There’s nothing wrong with acting a little strange. No one thinks that they’re completely normal. Everyone has quirks that set them apart from others. You have to embrace your differences and be proud of who you are.

## 4. Love

“What if I have some idea of love in my head and it’s just totally wrong?” While this doesn’t seem like the happiest quote, it’s one that everyone can relate to. Love can be hard to find, and it’s easy to question whether you’re looking for the right thing. It’s natural to have expectations of what love will be like, but the real thing might be different than what you imagined. When you find true love, you’ll know, and it won’t matter if it’s exactly what you thought you were searching for.

## 5. Regrets

“Do I regret it? Yes. Would I do it again? Probably.” There’s nothing wrong with doing something that you know you’re going to regret. Sometimes, we have to do things that we know we’ll regard as a mistake in the future. Of course, you should decide how much you’re going to regret it before you do anything. If the consequences are large, like going to jail, you might want to reconsider your idea.

## 6. Issues

“You can run away from your problems, but you’re just going to find new ones that pop up.” As difficult as it is, you need to face the issues in your life. It’s impossible to survive without going through hardships. In order to grow, you have to deal with the problems you’re met with. You can try to avoid them, but you’ll never be able to get rid of them completely.

## 7. Friends

“When you care about somebody you do what's best for them even if it sucks for you.” Sometimes, you have to make sacrifices in life. If you truly love someone, you’ll do whatever it takes to make them happy. Even if it makes your life worse, it’s worth it. You might be upset about the outcome, but you’ll be happy that they’re happy.

If you’ve never watched New Girl, what are you waiting for? It’s a hysterical show with a great cast and dialogue. Do you watch the show? What’s your favorite funny quote from it?
