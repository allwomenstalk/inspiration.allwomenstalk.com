# 9 Fitness Quotes Thatll Make You Feel Strong ...

If you're not in the mood to work out, you need to put yourself in the mood. The best way to do that is by reading inspiring quotes about fitness. Don't have any pinned to your wall? Then take a look at the ones that [Pop Sugar](https://www.popsugar.com/fitness/Fitness-Motivational-Quotes-38401755) came up with:

## 1. Sore Today, Strong Tomorrow

![Sore Today, Strong Tomorrow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u4/y5/yjxqky9g5612732dad51e219206989_600x400.jpg)

You might as well do something today that your future self will thank you for.

## 2. Because at the End of the Day, I'm Doing It for Me

![Because at the End of the Day, I'm Doing It for Me](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eg/te/rxm5ce445612732e5d053243547301_600x400.jpg)

You can't lose weight for your boyfriend or for society. You need to do it for you.

## 3. On the Good Days, I Work out. on the Bad Days, I Work out Harder

![On the Good Days, I Work out. on the Bad Days, I Work out Harder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m0/we/q3w1e8nq5612732e97632627270330_600x400.jpg)

It doesn't matter what went wrong during your day, because it's not a good enough excuse to miss a work out.

## 4. I Am Incredible. I Am Fearless. I Am Strong

![I Am Incredible. I Am Fearless. I Am Strong](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tm/if/p3k3e24n5612732ec1b99380730137_600x400.jpg)

Don't forget to tell yourself how amazing you are.

## 5. I Feel Prettiest when I Sweat

![I Feel Prettiest when I Sweat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m3/kz/sbzvmz3m5612732f0b403463305663_600x400.jpg)

Sweat isn't disgusting. It's beautiful.

## 6. You Can do Anything for a Minute

![You Can do Anything for a Minute](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z5/z9/d1aa1dmi5612732f4fc9c135618249_600x400.jpg)

And if you can work out for one minute, you can work out for two.

## 7. It Always Hurts before It Gets Better

![It Always Hurts before It Gets Better](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lt/vm/sj0hwowm5612733011c3b433790394_600x400.jpg)

The pain just means you're doing it right.

## 8. Don't Wish for It. Work for It

![Don't Wish for It. Work for It](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cx/u7/gew5ckik5612733040081799681523_600x400.jpg)

Your wishes won't come true, unless you work to make them a reality.

## 9. Believe You Can and You're Halfway There

![Believe You Can and You're Halfway There](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jb/m3/ka2mykrd56127330ab171675757292_600x400.jpg)

Don't sell yourself short. You're capable of anything.
