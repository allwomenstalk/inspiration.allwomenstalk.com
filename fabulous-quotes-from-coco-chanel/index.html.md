# 7 Fabulous Quotes from Coco Chanel ...

Even though she passed away in 1971, Coco Chanel is still one of the most famous female designers, and the quotes from Coco Chanel illustrate what a strong icon she was. Coco Chanel had a very difficult childhood. Her mother passed away when she was still quite young, and her father placed her in an orphanage. Despite the tragedy of this, being placed in the orphanage may have been the best thing for Coco’s future, because it is where she learned to sew. Coco Chanel opened her first shop in 1910. She began by just selling hats, but she later expanded to perfume and clothes. The famous Chanel no. 5 was launched in the 1920s, and Chanel’s signature suit with a collarless jacket and fitted skirt was first made in 1925. Due to the depression and WWII, Coco did have to close her shop, but years later she was able re-launch her label. Although she never married, Coco is famous for having a relationship with a Nazi officer, for which she was suspected of being a spy. Obviously, she was an interesting, strong and smart woman, and these **quotes from Coco Chanel** reflect her wisdom.

## 1. Uniqueness

![Uniqueness](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ca/z7/lx90rniq_554x483.jpg)

Sometimes people can become preoccupied with trying to fit in; however fitting in with other people isn’t the most important thing. Being your own unique person is one of the most important things you can do for yourself, and one of the quotes from Coco Chanel expresses this sentiment. Coco Chanel wisely stated, “In order to be irreplaceable one must always be different.” It is true that no one would be special if everyone was the same. Thankfully, every person has their own unique characteristics, which makes every person irreplaceable and special. Embrace your differences, because they are what make you unique!

## 2. Walls and Doors

![Walls and Doors](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qk/v3/u9w0flq5_554x576.jpg)

Have you ever spent a great deal of time and energy trying to change something or someone without much success? If you have, then you know that you can’t change everything. Coco Chanel expressed this fact perfectly when she emphasized, “Don’t spend time beating on a wall hoping it will transform into a door.” You have to know when to stop trying to make things or people change. You have to know when to move on. Moving on isn’t failure, it can be the wisest decision. It allows you to invest your time and energy in something worthwhile that will help you meet a goal or make you happy. No one can be happy trying to change something that won’t change, but they can be happy working toward a goal that is achievable.

## 3. Courageousness

![Courageousness](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s4/rj/ii3ugdsk_550x600.jpg)

Coco Chanel astutely declared, “The most courageous act is till to think for yourself. Aloud.” Thinking for yourself is something that takes courage, but actually voicing your opinions can require a lot of bravery. It is especially difficult to state your mind when your opinion is different from everyone else’s. However, if you can remember that your differences are what make you irreplaceable, you might be able to muster the bravery to start speaking your opinions. Even if what you believe is different from everyone else, there is still great value in your beliefs. Differences of opinions often espouse the greatest ideas, so never be shy about speaking your mind.

## 4. Being Rich

![Being Rich](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vg/nl/fgvttfsh_600x450.jpg)

It is often thought that rich people have a lot of money; however, money and richness do not always go hand in hand. Although she did end up having a fair amount of money, Coco Chanel wisely pointed out, “There are people who have money and people who are rich.” Having a rich and full life is about surrounding yourself with people you care about and love, it is not about having a lot of money. In the end, your family and friends are what bring richness to your life. A life that only has money would be very lonely.

## 5. Success

![Success](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q4/my/y8poae1f_550x625.jpg)

Despite the fact that she had a rough start as a child, Coco Chanel became a very successful woman. When discussing success, Coco Chanel affirmed, “Success is often achieved by those who don’t know that failure is inevitable.” Often, when people believe they will fail, failure becomes a self-fulfilling prophecy. Even if you try very hard to achieve something, if you believe you will fail, you probably will. However, if you ignore that failure is a possibility and believe you will succeed, the chances of success are very great. It is amazing what believing in yourself can do! Always enter into your goals with the belief that you will succeed!

## 6. Elegance

![Elegance](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zv/si/on1d1f16_475x636.jpg)

Coco Chanel’s elegance and fashion were timeless, which is why her quotes about elegance and fashion are so noteworthy. Regarding elegance, she expressed, “Elegance does not consist in putting on a new dress.” It is a simple statement, but it says a lot. Elegance comes from within and from the way you carry yourself. An elegant woman is someone who is poised and stands tall. You can have on the most beautiful dress in the world, but you won’t look elegant unless it is coming from within. Sometimes the woman wearing the simplest dress is the most elegant woman in the room.

## 7. Difficulties

_ Coco Chanel was very familiar with difficult situations, and she had a lot of wisdom about facing difficulties. She stated, “Hard times arouse an instinctive desire for authenticity.” When people are facing difficult situations, they generally find a way to bring comfort to themselves. Comfort can be found in many different places, but often comfort is found by living an authentic, honest life. Living an authentic life means being true to yourself and others. If you live authentically, hard times become easier to face.

Coco Chanel lived a very full and exciting life. She was a successful woman, whose fashion label is still well known. Reading Coco Chanel’s quotes is very inspiring and enlightening. She was a woman who had a lot of common sense and wisdom, and, fortunately, she shared that wisdom with others. Which of Coco Chanel’s quotes speaks to you?
