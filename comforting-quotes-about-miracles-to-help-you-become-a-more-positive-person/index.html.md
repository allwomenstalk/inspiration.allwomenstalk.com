# 7 Comforting Quotes about Miracles to Help You Become a More Positive Person ...

Some of us have seen and experienced miracles in our lives, while others may be wondering when a miracle will appear to transform their lives. Miracles transcend science and nature and can be likened to events that are synchronistic, magical, and simply divine. I hope as you read the following positive quotes about miracles, it inspires you to know that you can create more miracles in your life and allow this magic in.

## 1. Wonder of Life

“The miracle is not that I finished. The miracle is that I had the courage to start.” ~John Bingham

I selected this quote first as it pushes me to think that if I have the courage and desire to begin something I truly want to begin, it might just lead me to that perfect place that I have always dreamed of. This quote can also give you that perspective of amazement when you take a step back and really look at your own life and what you have been through. Nothing is ever what we expect in life, but if we don’t participate, we will never get what we have always dreamed of.

## 2. Believe in Humanity

“Never give up on anybody. Miracles happen every day.” ~Unknown
Small miracles happen all around us daily without us even knowing. It’s usually when we get disappointed in humanity or when we hear something bad about somebody that we once held in high regard, that we start to lose faith in humanity (as well as in ourselves). It’s in these moments that we need to shift our thinking and open our hearts to this quote’s wisdom.

## 3. Clear Perspectives

“If we could see the miracle of a single flower clearly, our whole life would change.” ~Buddha

Miracles play an important role in the act of honoring Buddhist relics in Southern Asia (as well as in many other religions). When you know that nothing is by chance and everything in life is connected; when you can truly see the beauty in something as simple as a flower, then you know that the Laws of Nature are well-established and that miracles are simply events that validate religious claims.

## 4. Never Ever Give up

“The moment you’re ready to quit is usually the moment right before the miracle happens.” ~Unknown

Motivational expressions like ‘keep going,’ ‘never give up,’ and ‘hang in there’ help us maintain our high spirits when the going gets rough and we feel like running for the hills. It is when you’re at the end of your rope, when you don’t want to ‘hang on’ anymore, that something usually turns in your favor, something amazing happens, for only good can come out of a bad situation. Remember that amazing things often happen right when you’re about to give up. So keep going and never ever give up!

## 5. Openness

“Miracles come in moments. Be ready and willing.” ~Dr. Wayne Dyer
How many of us truly ‘expect a miracle’ to happen in our lives? Most of us are far too busy going from one experience to the next, without giving much thought to things such as prayers, dreams and miracles. As Dr. Wayne Dyer puts it, you have to be open and ready for all positive changes to occur in your life; so make sure you are there and you are ready with outstretched arms…for that amazing moment to come.

## 6. Law of Attraction

“A miracle is a shift in perception from fear to love.” ~Marianne Williamson

We are all worthy of receiving miracles and as many miracles as we can think of asking for. When you ask for something in your life and then start thinking downer thoughts like, “oh it’s never going to happen to me” or “I don’t deserve this,” then the downer experience is what you’re going to get. If you flip those thoughts around and always think positive, you will begin to feel joy and gratitude for all the small and tiny miracles that are currently in your life, helping to create and attract even more miracles in more abundance.

## 7. Believe in Miracles

“I believe in manicures. I believe in overdressing. I believe in primping at leisure and wearing lipstick. I believe in pink. I believe that laughter is the best calorie burner. I believe in kissing, kissing a lot. I believe in being strong when everything seems to be going wrong. I believe that happy girls are the prettiest girls. I believe that tomorrow is another day and I believe in miracles.” ~Audrey Hepburn

Reading the last sentence inspired me to write this article; Audrey grabbed my attention for a second! Not only do I think she’s beautiful and glam, but this quote has many words of wisdom in it too. I hope that you believe in miracles and can acknowledge the sometimes small, yet amazing and somewhat miraculous components of your life. Go ahead and believe in more miracles in your life.

‘Expect a miracle’ that’s what I say…allow yourself to be led into the life you envision full of endless miracles and joy. Prayer, opening up to love (no fear ladies), and copious thoughts of gratitude will help attract even more miracles into your life’s experiences. Read these uplifting quotes during those dark days of doubt, when nothing feels like it's going right for you.
