# The Evil Side of Your Zodiac Sign ...

You know how you mom or your grandma probably used to call you an angel? Well, she might have thought so, but deep down you know that there is just as much devil in you as there is angel! We are all human, and we all have our own individual personality flaws; it’s just a case of being aware of them and embracing them rather than letting them absolutely rule you! Let's discover **the evil side of your zodiac sign**.

## 1. Aries

You have a super short wick, which means that your tendency to go over into full-blown anger is much higher than any other sign. You can be a dangerous person to be around if you are annoying and on the edge!

## 2. Taurus

It doesn’t matter whether you are right or you are wrong, nobody can tell you a single thing! You are too stubborn for your own good, even when you know deep down inside that you are in the wrong!

## 3. Gemini

You try your best not to be, but you can’t help but be unreliable most of the time. Keeping promises and meeting deadlines just isn’t something that comes naturally to you at all.

## 4. Cancer

You have the ability to change your mood in the blink of an eye, and it is unbearable because nobody know where they stand with you, and they go from being in your good book to being in your bad books in the space of a conversation!

## 5. Leo

You are a natural born flirt, which you don’t see as a problem, but it can be a problem both for your partner when they have to watch you ‘do your thing’, and also a problem for the person whose partner is your chosen one!

## 6. Virgo

You are super judgemental. You conjure a first impression of someone instantly, and it takes a hell of a lot for them to be able to change your mind.

## 7. Libra

You can be extremely manipulative when you want to be. You can pretty persuade anyone to do anything if you get five minutes alone with them.

## 8. Scorpio

You are way too jealous for your own good. You blow up at the merest hint of your partner or even a friend doing something that you aren’t sure about, and you won’t stop being a bitch until you get your own way.

## 9. Sagittarius

You love to talk more than anyone in the world, but unfortunately, you have a much harder time when it comes to listening to what other people have to say!

## 10. Capricorn

You are a straight up workaholic, which can be a good thing, but not when you find yourself routinely loving your job more than you love your partner!

## 11. Aquarius

You love nothing more than to give someone a good old fashioned lecture about their behaviour, even though you can come across as hypocritical most of the time!

## 12. Pisces

You expect too much of people, to the extent that you brand them as lazy and uncaring even when they are going above and beyond what any other person would deem the normal levels of care.
