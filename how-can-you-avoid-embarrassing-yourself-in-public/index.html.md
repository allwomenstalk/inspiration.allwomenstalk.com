# How Can You Avoid Embarrassing Yourself in Public?

Everyone can probably think of a time that they embarrassed themselves in public. Maybe it was through something you said or did but the memory of it definitely sticks with you. There’re some ways to avoid embarrassing yourself in public. I hope these tips are helpful to you.

## 1. Relax

One of the biggest things you can do to avoid embarrassing yourself in public is to relax. Most embarrassing moments happen because you’re nervous or apprehensive about a situation you’re in. Remind yourself that there’s nothing to be nervous about. Even if you’re around a group of people you don’t know, remember that they’re just people that have flaws, too. Relaxing is going to give you a much better chance at avoiding embarrassing yourself.

## 2. Pretend You’re Not Nervous

Pretending you’re not nervous can be a helpful trick to play on yourself. It can help you to avoid embarrassing yourself. It’ll also help you to truly feel less nervous. Faking confidence has a way of fooling you into feeling confident. It’s one of those fake it till you make it moments in life.

## 3. Take the Temperature of a Room

There’re times you could embarrass yourself by not being observant of the circumstances around you. Taking the temperature of a room when you enter it is always a good idea. Observe it for a few moments to judge if it’s a somber mood, a casual mood or maybe even humorous. Knowing what the mood is before you join in a conversation is a good idea. It can prevent you from making a comment that would embarrass you.

## 4. Give Other People Space to Talk

Some people tend to talk a lot when they’re nervous. It’s their way of making a situation more comfortable. Other people just have more outgoing personalities and tend to talk a lot. It’s great to be able to converse freely. But on occasion, you may want to pause to make sure you’re not taking over the conversation. Give others the chance to add to the discussion, too.

## 5. Be Yourself

Things are always going to go better in any situation when you’ll be yourself. Being yourself is what’s most comfortable for you because it’s real. And you’ll find most people appreciate authenticity. Of course we all want to work to be the best version of ourselves. But trying to be someone you’re not can easily lead to embarrassing yourself.

## 6. Laugh at Yourself

You know, occasionally everyone says or does something embarrassing. When you find yourself in this situation, laugh about it. It’s better to laugh at yourself than to give into embarrassment. If you laugh at yourself, others will probably laugh with you and any embarrassment will be forgotten. Remember that everyone has a moment like this happen to them from time to time in their life.

## 7. Keep a Handle on Your Temper

One sure way to embarrass yourself in public is to lose your temper. When you get angry, you temporarily lose concern of who’s around. Your anger gets the best of you. That can cause you to embarrass yourself quickly. Try to remember to keep your cool when something angers you, at least until you can handle the situation in a less public way.

These’re 7 ways to avoid embarrassing yourself in public. Have you ever embarrassed yourself in public? You’re welcome to share your stories.
