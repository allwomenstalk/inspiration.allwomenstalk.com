Snape Quotes Thatll Make You Love Him ...

By Heather

Oh, Severus Snape – amazing, amazing character, and one I happen to consider a hero. I know that's a divisive point in the Harry Potter fandom, but no matter what you think of sly Professor Snape, you have to admit that he was unforgettable. I happen to think he was lovable – in a dark and twisty, sharp and pointy way, admittedly. His scenes in the books and the movies are some of my favorites, as are a lot of his quotes. After you read these Snape quotes, I bet you'll love him even more – or maybe you'll decide you love him a little bit for the first time!

Frequently Asked Questions

Why are Snape's quotes about love so impactful?
Oh, they're just so deep and heartfelt! His quotes reveal the complexity of his love, especially his unwavering love for Lily Potter. It's fascinating how a character who seems so harsh can express such tender emotions.
What's the big deal about 'After all this time? Always.'?
Ugh, this quote gets me every time! It's probably one of the most iconic lines. It perfectly captures Snape's eternal love for Lily. Even after all the years and everything that happened, he still loves her. Just timeless.
Which Snape quote is your favorite?
That's such a tough choice! If I have to pick, I'd go with 'Control your emotions, discipline your mind.' It really shows Snape's complexity and the duality of his character – he's always battling between his feelings and his duty.
Why do people love Snape, despite his flaws?
I mean, who isn't a bit flawed, right? Snape's character is so relatable because of his imperfections. His journey from antagonist to hero is one that resonates with a lot of people. Plus, there's that whole tragic love story of his!
Are there any funny Snape quotes?
Oh, for sure! Snape has this dry, sarcastic humor that can be hilarious. While some lines might not be 'ha-ha' funny, his wit and timing can make certain scenes memorable and bring a smile.
