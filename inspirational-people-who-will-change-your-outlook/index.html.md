# 7 Inspirational People Who Will Change Your Outlook ...

Are you looking for inspirational people who can change your outlook? Over the years, I’ve discovered teachers, motivational speakers and authors who have inspired me to grow in all sorts of ways. It wasn’t easy, but I managed to narrow them down for this list. These are the top 7 inspirational people who have had the greatest impact on my life.

## 1. Dr. Sean Stephenson

![Dr. Sean Stephenson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qd/04/hdkgs1qa_450x320.jpg)

Dr. Sean Stephenson was born with Osteogenesis Imperfecta, a rare bone disorder that stunted his growth and caused him to have very brittle bones. Sean relies on a wheelchair to get around, but his physical challenges have never held him back. In my opinion, Sean is one of the most inspirational people of his generation. He inspires us to break free of fears, excuses and insecurities. Check out his free internet radio show: SeanStephenson.com.

## 2. Andrea Albright

![Andrea Albright](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k5/re/pdz4xo6t_498x342.jpg)

Andrea Albright is a weight loss expert and motivational speaker who battled her own weight problem for years. Andrea’s not about “quick fixes” and believes that lasting weight loss is only possible if you heal yourself from within. Yes, there are countless weight loss gurus on the internet, but Andrea’s different. She teaches us how to actually enjoy the process of healing our bodies. Her site: AmazingBodyNow.com

## 3. Dr. Wayne Dyer

![Dr. Wayne Dyer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iw/zi/m5ijo14x_600x454.jpg)

Dr. Wayne Dyer believes in living every day with gratitude. What makes him different? Wayne grew up in orphanages and foster homes, beating the odds in order to achieve his goals. Wayne is an inspiring teacher who shows us how to overcome obstacles and live our lives with purpose. You can learn more about him on his site: DrWayneDyer.com

## 4. Jerrold Mundis

![Jerrold Mundis](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/io/vu/gvll47l8_600x429.jpg)

Jerrold Mundis has authored many different books, but his money books are what truly turned my financial situation around. If you are dealing with money problems, “How to Get Out of Debt, Stay Out of Debt & Live Prosperously” is an absolute must-read. Jerrold explains how to break free of our money-related stress and fear. Honestly, Jerrold’s book gave me a whole new outlook on my financial situation. Check him out at: JerroldMundis.com

## 5. Dr. Tara Brach

![Dr. Tara Brach](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zk/ep/sv972dhh_473x338.jpg)

Dr. Tara Brach is an author and meditation teacher who spent years living and studying in an ashram, or spiritual community. Her books and guided meditations teach us how to embrace our fears by radically accepting ourselves, flaws and all. For meditation newbies, Tara makes it easy to grasp the concepts of mindfulness meditation (vipassana). Even if you’re a skeptic or think that meditation isn’t for you, Tara will change your mind. Check out her free guided audio meditations: tarabrach.com

## 6. Anne Lamott

![Anne Lamott](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ud/i9/tb3fqc1p_600x427.jpg)

I first discovered Anne Lamott on an episode of Oprah’s Super Soul Sunday. Anne inspires us to accept ourselves, just as we are today. She’s battled addiction and her books teach us how to begin healing the wounded parts of ourselves. One of her messages I love the most is about the importance of appreciating the sacred parts of our everyday lives. Anne doesn’t have a website, but she’s on Facebook and Twitter.

## 7. Dr. Caldwell Esselstyn, Jr. & Dr. T. Colin Campbell

![Dr. Caldwell Esselstyn, Jr. & Dr. T. Colin Campbell](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j6/wd/nlaf1769_600x429.jpg)

I just love these guys. I’m giving them equal billing because they are the two geniuses behind the film, “Forks Over Knives.” If you haven’t yet seen this documentary, it’s time you did because it could literally save your life. Because of their research, thousands of people have healed their bodies with simple food. They have proven that diseases like heart disease and cancer can be completely avoided by eating a 100% plant-based diet. They’ve inspired my husband and me to change our diets, which I never thought would happen with my steak-loving husband. For information about the film, check out the official site: ForksOverKnives.com

So there you have it. These 7 (actually 8) people have changed my outlook in so many different ways. Who inspires you?
