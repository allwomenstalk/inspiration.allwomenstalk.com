# 17 Ways You Can Change Your Life in a Week ...

It only takes two seconds to change your entire life. You just need to find the bravery to go through with it. Of course, life changing decisions aren't always big. There are some small things you can do to make a change. Don't believe it? Here are a few different ways you can change your life this week:

## 1. Get a Haircut

![Get a Haircut](https://img.allw.mn/content/m4/nm/bca6ekh556279ab9760ec627843775_500x275.gif)

You can get your hair dyed, shave off the sides, or just get a trim. A new look is always a pick-me-up.

## 2. Sign up for a New Class

![Sign up for a New Class](https://img.allw.mn/content/vq/jy/emu3yonu56279ab92c2fe277775101_500x208.gif)

Your college and high school classes aren't the only types of classes that exist. You can sign up for some fun cooking, baking, or painting classes near your house.

## 3. Learn a New Recipe or Language

![Learn a New Recipe or Language](https://img.allw.mn/content/z9/kc/hfn09jkm56279ab959346560242717_500x209.gif)

The more knowledge you possess, the broader your horizons will be. So fill your brain with as much information as possible.

## 4. Ask out Your Crush

![Ask out Your Crush](https://img.allw.mn/content/jt/s8/ou5fvt7d56279aba35a48606632011_500x281.gif)

Send him a text or ask him out in person. Either way, you need to make a move.

## 5. Set Your Alarm for an Hour Earlier

![Set Your Alarm for an Hour Earlier](https://img.allw.mn/content/z5/hh/siou7syp56279aba7be60497441759_500x281.gif)

An hour doesn't seem like a very long time, but you can get a lot done in sixty minutes. See for yourself by waking up a little earlier than usual.

## 6. Apply for Jobs

![Apply for Jobs](https://img.allw.mn/content/xp/j5/l8lyb3gw56279abad5333472026731_500x238.gif)

Most people are either unemployed or hate their job. If you're one of them, then start filling out more job applications. You can even apply for some internships.

## 7. Call up an Old Friend

![Call up an Old Friend](https://img.allw.mn/content/hj/z7/ahbhyxmj56279abae4f8f196321491_400x226.gif)

You don't have to sit around missing your friends. They're only a phone call away.

## 8. Delete Your Social Media Accounts

![Delete Your Social Media Accounts](https://img.allw.mn/content/p8/xr/audojldi56279abb2f3e4004772665_500x205.gif)

Sometimes, Facebook and Instagram will only bring you down. If you can't stand your "friends" online, then delete your accounts. It'll make you feel free.

## 9. Quit Your Bad Habit

![Quit Your Bad Habit](https://img.allw.mn/content/gj/nc/unfl2t9w56279abc155aa249025556_500x217.gif)

Maybe you smoke. Maybe you bite your nails. Maybe you lie. It's time to break whatever your bad habit is.

## 10. Read an Inspirational Book

![Read an Inspirational Book](https://img.allw.mn/content/kn/kz/rg8yvxlc56279abc16d42235501509_500x281.gif)

Words can have a huge impact on us. Instead of reading short inspirational quotes, read an inspirational book.

## 11. Start Exercising

![Start Exercising](https://img.allw.mn/content/qh/ep/w380rmfq56279abbbe7db450281034_400x268.gif)

The more you exercise, the healthier you'll be.

## 12. Cut Junk Food out of Your Diet

![Cut Junk Food out of Your Diet](https://img.allw.mn/content/wc/yp/n6v0m6u356279abbcb210330393384_500x278.gif)

You'll feel more energized if you stop eating chocolate from the moment you wake up until the moment you fall asleep.

## 13. Compliment Yourself Instead of Insulting Yourself

![Compliment Yourself Instead of Insulting Yourself](https://img.allw.mn/content/j7/fw/qs2sjyib56279abd24775061456651_400x226.gif)

It's time to [stop being mean](https://inspiration.allwomenstalk.com/useful-tips-on-how-to-stop-being-mean-to-people/) to yourself. There's enough nastiness in the world.

## 14. Start Conversations with Strangers

![Start Conversations with Strangers](https://img.allw.mn/content/dg/vo/pe3dmtck56279abd10d7b259225618_500x202.gif)

It's important to socialize. Even if you're never going to see the lady at the supermarket again, you can still have an interesting conversation with her.

## 15. Do a Good Deed Everyday

![Do a Good Deed Everyday](https://img.allw.mn/content/he/bo/nejqvdx756279abce73c7932667547_500x185.gif)

When you help others, you're really helping yourself. It feels good to do good.

## 16. Start Saving for a Vacation

![Start Saving for a Vacation](https://img.allw.mn/content/xl/po/qrcra08a56279abd52642928475039_450x249.gif)

If you're bored with your life, save up your money. That way, you'll eventually get to go on a thrilling vacation.

## 17. Cut Harmful People out of Your Life

![Cut Harmful People out of Your Life](https://img.allw.mn/content/li/it/l2oqg88i56279abddd1a7017366761_450x253.gif)

Even if you've been friends with someone for a decade, it's okay to cut them out of your life. If they're unhealthy for you, then you need to do what's best for your own mental health.

Now you have plenty of little ways to change your life throughout the week! Which one of these things are you going to do this week?
