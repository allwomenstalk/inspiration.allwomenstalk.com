# What about Your Zodiac Sign is Holding You Back from Living the Life You Want ...

Don’t you always find that life is much easier and sometimes much more enjoyable when you are living it in your head? We all love to daydream, there is nothing wrong with a little escapism once in a while, but when your real life starts to stutter and slow down in favour of your daydreaming, then perhaps it’s time to make a change and be a bit more proactive. This is much easier to do if you can identify what exactly it is that is keeping you from living real life to the fullest. Let’s take a look at **what about your zodiac sign is holding you back from living the life you want**.

## 1. Aries

You are cursed with having a real lack of patience. If something doesn’t happen immediately, then you give up on it.

## 2. Taurus

You aren’t as flexible as you need to be, you aren’t able to roll with the punches that life throws you as well as others.

## 3. Gemini

You are a typical Gemini in the sense that you can’t seem to be able to make up your mind. Your personal failure is in the fact that you can never make a decision and stick with it.

## 4. Cancer

You never like to feel insecure or uncomfortable, and this attitude can often lead to you turning down risky opportunities that might make your life better in the long run.

## 5. Leo

You are the life of the party, but that also comes with a negative in the shape of getting too caught up with what everyone else thinks about you.

## 6. Virgo

You are never confident enough in your convictions to really make your mark. Your general insecurity is always the thing that holds you back.

## 7. Libra

You don’t like conflict and confrontation, and this often leads you to not getting what you want because you are afraid to call someone out and demand your fair share.

## 8. Scorpio

You spend so much time being jealous of what everyone else has that you never find the time or energy to work toward getting those things for yourself.

## 9. Sagittarius

You are way too laid back and passive in your own life. You expect everything to magically happen for you when real success actually takes a lot of hard work.

## 10. Capricorn

You are a natural born pessimist, which unfortunately means that you always expect the worst and therefore never go for it in the way that an optimist would.

## 11. Aquarius

You have been let down a few times in your life, which has lead to developing an inability to trust the people who really do want to help.

## 12. Pisces

You can’t help it, but you are something of an attention seeker, and this manifests often in making out that your life is worse than it actually is for sympathy. This attitude will never allow you to move forward and prosper.
