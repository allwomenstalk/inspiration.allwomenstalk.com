# 7 Important Things to Remember when You Feel Lost ...

There are quite a few things to remember when you feel lost. Everyone feels alone, isolated and misunderstood sometimes, even the most charismatic or entertaining people you know. We all have doubts and insecurities and we all experience days when we feel lost and lonely. Charles Bukowski has a very interesting viewpoint; he said that “Being alone never felt right. Sometimes it felt good, but it never felt right.”. Here are a few things to remember when you feel lost that will help you realize that in fact, you’re never alone:

## 1. Sometimes You Need to Be Alone

In my opinion, this is definitely one of the most important things to remember when you feel lost. Sometimes you just need to be alone, so you can clarify certain things and find your way. We all need solitude in order to detach ourselves from obligations and to simply enjoy our own company.

## 2. You Are Actually Not Alone

When you’re feeling lost or lonely, I must tell you that in fact, you’re not alone. A lot of people feel this way and many of us are fighting the same battle alongside you. Just reach out to your loved one, talk to them and you’ll see that you’re actually not alone. There’s no need to feel embarrassed or pathetic because you’re feeling this way; there are a lot of people who experience the same emotions as you on a daily basis.

## 3. You Have More Control than You Think

Even though you sometimes feel lost and lonely, don’t victimize yourself, because you actually have more control than you think. You can choose your attitude in every situation, so how you choose to react is up to you. You can face any challenge and overcome any obstacles that might appear in your path if you just believe in yourself and if you have faith in your abilities.

## 4. Always Try to do Your Best

Always try to do your best but keep in mind that the best you can do changes from day to day. Don’t develop a defeatist attitude and don’t let this feeling affect your self-esteem or take away your sense of worth! Stay determined in spite of any obstacles you might have to overcome and acknowledge the person you are now.

## 5. Ask for Help

If the problem persists and you’re being overwhelmed with feelings of loneliness and desperation, then you could try to ask for professional help to see why you are feeling this way and what you can do to improve your well-being. Don’t be afraid to reach out and ask for help from your loved ones, because they will offer you the support you need to overcome this problem.

## 6. It Can Help You Find What You Are Looking for

Even though you feel lost and alone, I must tell you that sometimes a little solitude can help you find what you are looking for. Just remember that everyone feels a little bit lost sometimes, but instead of being scared, try to see what you can learn from this experience, because it may help you become stronger and wiser in order to reach all your goals.

## 7. You Are Not Who You Used to Be

People change and so do you. You are not who you used to be, but that’s okay. Even though you might feel lost or misunderstood now, you shouldn’t let those feelings discourage you. Just try to discover who the new you is and see what lessons you can learn from this experience. Meditate on who you are, what you want and how you can fulfill all your dreams and turn this lonely stage of your life into a more productive one.

Feeling lonely or lost can be extremely painful sometimes. You shouldn’t let those feelings affect you, so try to see what you can learn from that experience. Have you ever felt this way? What did you do to overcome those feelings? Do you know any other important things someone should remember when they are feeling lost? Please share your thoughts with us in the comments section!
