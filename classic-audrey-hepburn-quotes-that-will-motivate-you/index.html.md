# 7 Classic Audrey Hepburn Quotes That Will Motivate You ...

As one of the most celebrated fashion and movie icons, it isn't difficult to fathom just how powerful some Audrey Hepburn quotes are. Whether credit is due to her intelligent nature or the sophisticated manner in which she carried herself, Ms. Hepburn captured the attention of her diverse audience. Reading a plethora of her interviews has never failed to leave me in awe of her insight. I can only hope that these classic Audrey Hepburn quotes will be just as motivating to you.

## 1. Elegance

![Elegance](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07225907_8511_500x498.jpg)

"Elegance is the only beauty that never fades."

When it comes to Audrey Hepburn quotes, these insightful words on the importance of elegance have been remarked as a fan favorite. Elegance is not concerned with the beauty standards perpetuated by the media; it's all about being happy with who you are and never giving in to what others deem as beautiful.

## 2. Laughter

![Laughter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07230000_9090_450x367.jpg)

“I love people who make me laugh. I honestly think it's the thing I like most, to laugh. It cures a multitude of ills. It's probably the most important thing in a person.”

I wholeheartedly agree with the sentiment that laughter is one of the greatest things people can do. At the end of the day, if you can't throw caution to the wind and just laugh with your arms raised and your smile wide, then what else do you have?

## 3. Miracles

![Miracles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07230038_3671_592x444.jpg)

“I believe in pink. I believe that laughing is the best calorie burner. I believe in kissing, kissing a lot. I believe in being strong when everything seems to be going wrong. I believe that happy girls are the prettiest girls. I believe that tomorrow is another day and I believe in miracles.”

I love the vast amount of inspirational messages embedded in this quote. In the face of adversity, there is no better way to rise against your emotional tormentors, whether physical or psychosomatic, than by holding your head high and remaining headstrong. Remembering that there is always another day to look forward to is such an incredibly powerful thought to instill.

## 4. Possible

![Possible](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07230107_8123_600x444.jpg)

"Nothing is impossible, the word itself says 'I'm possible'!"

I can honestly say that this is one of my top mantras to live by. Just because a situation seems difficult at the time does not mean that you can not handle it. Being able to believe in yourself and your ability to be victorious despite your initial fears speaks volumes about your true nature. Anything can be possible if you will it to be.

## 5. Living

![Living](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07230135_7047_592x444.jpg)

“Living is like tearing through a museum. Not until later do you really start absorbing what you saw, thinking about it, looking it up in a book, and remembering - because you can't take it in all at once.”

The idea of comparing life to an intricate museum renders me speechless; I'd never thought about life experiences in this manner until reading this. Everything happens so suddenly that it only makes sense to sit back and take everything in as it comes.

## 6. Days

![Days](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07230226_7210_600x456.jpg)

“Pick the day. Enjoy it - to the hilt. The day as it comes. People as they come... The past, I think, has helped me appreciate the present, and I don't want to spoil any of it by fretting about the future.”

Reflecting on the past in order to understand the present is a great way to reconcile yourself with events that occur (of which you are able to control). Instead of worrying about what is going to happen in the distant future, take a step back, breathe and focus on what's going on in your life today.

## 7. Human

![Human](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07230319_3746_500x375.jpg)

"The best thing to hold onto in life is each other."

This final quote is one that I truly feels applies to humanity as a whole. I also feel that it's important for people to rely on one another. Everyone is connected by the same thread of being flawed and human, fueling the thought that there is no greater act than random displays of kindness to strangers.

Audrey Hepburn is the true embodiment of a classy and elegant woman. Her words continue to inspire both women and men to fully embrace human nature and go out into the world with their hearts wide open. What are some of your favorite quotes by this iconic beauty?
