# Why You Need Each Zodiac Sign in Your Life ...

Life is like a box of chocolates, and the best part of that is that you get to experience and sample relationships and friendships with every kind of person! If you are someone who happens to be in to astrology, then you might have seen things in the past recommending which signs you are compatible with and which signs you aren’t, but I happen to believe that following these kinds of restricted rules isn’t the best or most fulfilling way to live. Here is why you need each zodiac sign in your life.

## 1. Aries

You need an Aries for motivation. They are always the ones who are there to pick you up and make sure that you reach your potential every single time!

## 2. Taurus

You need a Taurus because that are pretty much the most trustworthy people in the world. If you confide in a Taurus, you can be sure that that secret is never going to get out.

## 3. Gemini

You need a Gemini for the interesting and fun conversation that they can provide. You will never run out of things to talk about with a Gemini in the room!

## 4. Cancer

You need a Cancer for the massive emotional support that they can provide for you. It is second nature to them to be caring and motherly for their friends when they can see that they are struggling.

## 5. Leo

You need a Leo for their fierce loyalty. Once you have made friends with a Leo, you can rest assured that that person will have your back for life!

## 6. Virgo

You need a Virgo for their amazing advice. They just seem to have a talent for seeing situations in a different way, and because of that they can provide lots of insights that you hadn’t even considered.

## 7. Libra

You need a Libra because they will always be there to brighten up your day and make you life fun. They are among the most sociable creatures in the world, and you need that sometimes!

## 8. Scorpio

You need a Scorpio because they will make you feel alive! They are spontaneous and unpredictable, and they inject some much-needed excitement into your life.

## 9. Sagittarius

You need a Sagittarius because they are totally honest. You will always be able to get a truthful opinion from a Sagittarius; they literally don’t know how to lie!

## 10. Capricorn

You need a Capricorn because they will inspire you to be more creative. They have a natural charisma that is really infectious.

## 11. Aquarius

You need an Aquarius for their adventurous side. You have a tendency to shy away from the unknown, but an Aquarius will take your hand and go on the journey with you.

## 12. Pisces

You need a Pisces for their massive heart. Nothing is too much for a Pisces, they will always find time for you.
