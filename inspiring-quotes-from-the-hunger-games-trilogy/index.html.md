# 7 Inspiring Quotes from the Hunger Games Trilogy ...

Since it’s such a meaningful trilogy, there are inspiring quotes from The Hunger Games. The novels, written by Suzanne Collins, are definitely worth the read. If you haven’t read them or at least seen the movies, do so immediately. Here are some of the most inspiring quotes from The Hunger Games trilogy.

## 1. Don’t Collapse

![Don’t Collapse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/23233409_2294_600x450.jpg)

One of the most inspiring quotes from The Hunger Games trilogy was said by Finnick. He said, “It takes ten times as long to put yourself back together as it does to fall apart.” If you’re trying to make a better life for yourself, you have to remember that it takes time. You can’t magically make everything fall into place. However, all it takes is one moment for you to fall apart. This is why you have to be strong, and take whatever life throws at you.

## 2. Make It through

![Make It through](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/28122615_1926_600x496.jpg)

“You don't forget the face of the person who was your last hope.” When you’re placed in a bad situation, you have to remember that there's always hope. Of course, you have to find the strength to make it through and hold onto what inspires you. Don’t let the stress of life get to you. With a bit of willpower and hope, you can make it through the toughest days. When it’s all over, you’ll be proud of yourself for hanging in there.

## 3. Power of Hope

![Power of Hope](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/23233747_1380_600x450.jpg)

President Snow is a dangerous but intelligent man. He once said, “Hope. It is the only thing stronger than fear.” You might be terrified of what your future holds, but you have to have hope that you’ll accomplish your goals. When you believe that something is possible, your actions change. You can do anything, so long as you have hope in your heart.

## 4. Know the Odds

![Know the Odds](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/08091209_9259_600x375.jpg)

**It's the quintessential quote:** "May the odds be ever in your favor." You can't always stack the odds in your favor but you can make your own by always trying your hardest. Stay inspired, stay strong, and the odds will always be in your favor.

## 5. Always Remember

![Always Remember](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/23233922_6210_600x598.jpg)

It‘s dangerous to get your thoughts jumbled. You have to “remember who the real enemy is.” Don’t blame others for things that they cannot control. If you’re angry about something, don’t take it out on the wrong people. You have to remember the real reason behind why you’re upset.

## 6. Stay True to Yourself

![Stay True to Yourself](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/23234020_8184_600x530.jpg)

Before the first games, Peeta says, “If I'm going to die, I want to still be me. Does it make any sense?” He represents the desire to remain a good person even when the odds aren’t in his favor. He doesn’t want a dire situation to change his personality. No matter what, he wants to remain the man that he is.

## 7. Never Forget

![Never Forget](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/09021916_5966_403x392.jpg)

Katniss once told Peeta that they should try to forget what happened during the games, and he said, “But I don't wanna forget." Even when you’re subjected to torture, you don’t want to forget what you went through. Your past determines who you currently are. Even in your worst times, there is some beauty. You don’t want to forget the good things, or the bad.

If you haven’t read the books, purchase them as soon as possible. Are you already a fan of The Hunger Games? Who’s your favorite character?
