# How to Guide for Girls to Find Your Calling in Life  ...

If you are stuck in a job you don’t like or you simply don’t know what you want to do when you grow up, here are a few very helpful tips on how to find your calling in life. Sometimes, figuring out what to do with your life can be a bit scary but you will have to step out of your comfort zone if you want to find your true purpose in life. There are a few things you can do that will get you closer to your ultimate dream and that will help you make a positive difference in your life. Here are a few tips on how to find your calling in life that you should consider:

## 1. Use an Online Skills Profiler

If you want to find your calling in life, the best thing that could help you achieve this goal is an online skills profiler. This tool will only require you to list all the skills that you possess, the level you are at and it will generate multiple jobs that will suit your skills. Then you can click on each of those jobs, learn about the degrees that job requires, the salary you’ll get or any other things that will help you understand if that occupation will suit you.

## 2. List It out

In oder to find your true calling in life, you could start by listing all the things you like doing and all the things you are good at. Write down even the things that seem impossible to you. When you are done, just leave that list alone for a while and see if you can come up with any more ideas.

## 3. Notice What You like to Read

What do you usually enjoy reading? If you want to find your purpose in life, you could take measure of what kind of books you like reading since those books might give you a hint of what career path to head toward.

## 4. Look at Your Hobbies

There are a lot of people who've turned their hobbies into high-paying jobs, so why not do the same? What are your favourite hobbies or what do you like to do in your spare time? Just see if you can turn any of these activities into things that could generate a revenue. This way, you will manage to make money doing the things you love and it won’t even feel like working.

## 5. Read Autobiographies of People You Look up to

In order to find your true calling, try to be inspired by the lives of the people you look up to. Those books can motivate you to work hard to reach your goals and they can help you figure out what you want to do with your life.

## 6. Take a Class

If you are interested in a certain field, then take some classes to find out more about those domains. A lot of colleges offer a wide range of classes that can match your interests. Also, in your spare time, you could take some free online classes since they will help you find out more about the fields that interest you.

## 7. Interview People

If you want to understand better what a specific career entails, then try to listen to what someone who works in that field has to say. Interview people and ask them more about their jobs. Find out what they did that made them become so successful, what degrees they obtained, what skills they had to develop or even if they are satisfied with their salary.

If you want to find your true calling in life you should also follow your dreams, work hard to reach your goals, take up new hobbies and learn new skills. Do you know any other tips on how to find your calling in life? Please share your thoughts with us in the comments section!
