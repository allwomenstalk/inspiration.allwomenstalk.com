# 11 Most Inspiring Quotes from Albert Einstein to Help You Get Back on Your Feet ...

Every time I’m feeling a little bit down, I like to read a couple of motivational quotes from Albert Einstein because this always helps me get back on my feet in no time. A lot of people consider Albert Einstein to be the most influential and most successful scientist to ever live and also, the father of modern physics. In 1921 he received the Nobel Prize in Physics and he published more than 300 scientific works and over 150 non-scientific works. Although he is an incredible scientist, he is also a huge source of motivation. As a child, no one trusted his abilities, his teachers rejected him and his colleagues mocked him. Despite all that, he never gave up; instead he did everything in his power to follow his dreams and now “his discoveries are foundations of the teachings in our science curriculum.” Here are 11 of the most motivational quotes from Albert Einstein that are really inspiring and which will help you see things from a different perspective:

## 1. Know Your Self-Worth

![Know Your Self-Worth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c8/o7/i6/k4nl9731_600x450.jpg)

This is definitely one of the most inspiring quotes from Albert Einstein that will motivate you to reach all your goals. Know your self-worth because everyone is talented in one way or another. Don’t let people tell you what you can and cannot do! Follow your dreams and believe in yourself! You can achieve all your goals if you work hard enough and if you have enough patience. Never give up on your dreams and love yourself, because you are perfect just the way you are!

## 2. Never Give up

![Never Give up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ce/d6/j3/gf7wpcad_534x400.jpg)

You should never give up on your dreams, no matter how many obstacles may appear in your path. You will manage to overcome them if you keep your balance and if you keep on going. Things aren’t always comfortable or pleasant. You may have to do a lot of things you don’t like sometimes, but if your goals are really important to you, then you shouldn’t let yourself get discouraged so easily. Just keep moving and work on fulfilling all your dreams!

## 3. Don’t Be Afraid of Failure

![Don’t Be Afraid of Failure](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l7/e1/k5/ahb6a90r_600x450.jpg)

Don’t ever be afraid to fail, because failure can actually be one of the greatest teachers you’ll ever have. Learn from your mistakes and focus on reaching your goals! There will be all kinds of obstacles in your path; you’ll just have to overcome them, so don’t lose sight of your objectives and never ever give up! Follow your dreams and don’t let anything stand in the way of your happiness!

## 4. Experience

![Experience](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y9/vg/zd/wc3mv3pp_600x450.jpg)

Albert Einstein couldn’t have said it any better! The only way you’re ever going to learn anything is by experimenting with things, by making mistakes and by learning how to correct them. Don’t be afraid to fail and don’t be afraid to try new things! Live, experiment and enjoy yourself because this is all that matters after all.

## 5. Goals

![Goals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t8/cs/kc/jy0n2bmr_500x348.jpg)

Don’t be afraid to dream big! Having a goal and doing everything in your power to fulfill it is what makes you happy. So, focus on reaching your objectives and don’t get distracted by people or by different objects you might think you need. There’s no greater satisfaction than reaching a goal, no matter how small or insignificant it might seem to other people. If it matters to you and if it makes you happy, then this is all that counts.

## 6. Values

![Values](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tj/ts/g5/gy660yk0_560x359.jpg)

While pursuing your dreams, don’t forget who you are and also, don’t forget your values! Don’t let success change you for the worst and always remember that you’ll be able to fulfill all your dreams just by working hard and dedicating yourself 100% to reaching all your goals! Be a person of value and do what you think is right, not what others tell you that you should do if you want to be successful. You know what makes you happy, so remember who you are, work hard and I’m sure you’ll become very successful someday.

## 7. Imagination

![Imagination](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/il/qe/y0/a3xaiyhf_600x338.jpg)

Be creative, dream big and do everything in your power to fulfill all your dreams! Take initiative and don’t be afraid to try new things! While knowledge is limited, your imagination is endless. You can achieve everything you set your mind to do, no matter how crazy or impossible this may seem sometimes. Just believe in yourself and don’t let people tell you what you cannot do!

## 8. Be Flexible

![Be Flexible](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y4/oz/yo/q4r2mflz_600x338.jpg)

Learn how to be more flexible and just adapt to all the changes that may appear in your life. If you want to solve a problem and you tried something but it didn’t work out, then just try something else. Be open to new things, evolve and allow yourself to grow, to become better at what you do. Just overcome that fear of success and do everything in your power to reach all your goals!

## 9. Take Initiative

![Take Initiative](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zl/c5/kj/ffnprtly_600x396.jpg)

Well, he couldn’t have been more right when he said that! Just like Mahatma Gandhi said: “Be the change you wish to see in the world!” Take initiative and stand for what you believe in! Don’t let others tell you what you can and cannot do! Fight for what is right! Don’t be afraid to try new things and take action! Do everything in your power to help other people and persevere! You will make the world a better place for you and for your loved ones.

## 10. Don’t Let Others Tell You What You Can’t do

![Don’t Let Others Tell You What You Can’t do](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ys/gg/ym/e9hohk6j_600x452.jpg)

Don’t ever let other people tell you what you can’t do! Just use your intelligence and assume the consequences of your actions! Don’t be afraid to fail and always believe in yourself, no matter what other people tell you! Be courageous and always stand up for what you think is right!

## 11. Find the Opportunity

![Find the Opportunity](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bv/mz/ew/ddao44bp_599x518.jpg)

Find the opportunity you need to be successful! Never give up looking for it and try to simplify the things around you. Find harmony and find your strength so you’ll be able to overcome all the obstacles that might appear in your path. Don’t give up on your dreams and do everything in your power to fulfill them!

Albert Einstein is definitely one of the most inspirational and motivational people out there. Do you know any other famous quotes that belong to him that have really helped you see things from a different perspective? I would like to hear your suggestions, so please share your thoughts with us in the comments section!
