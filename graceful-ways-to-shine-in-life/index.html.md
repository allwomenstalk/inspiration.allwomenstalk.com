# Graceful Ways to Shine in Life ...

There are lots of **graceful ways to shine in life**. When you think about someone who shines brighter than everyone, in terms of talent and personality, the movies and TV shows have taught us that in order to get to that point and stature, you will have had to engage in some serious Showgirls style tactics, mowing down others in order to climb the ladder and establish yourself at the top! This might be the case for some, but backstabbing and playing mind games don’t always have to be the way to go. If you want to have success and become somebody who everybody knows, then why not do it in a more pleasant fashion? Here are some suggestions for **graceful ways to shine in life**.

## 1. Keep Going

This is one of the most important **graceful ways to shine in life**. No matter how many setbacks you experience, the important thing to do is to keep on going. There is nothing graceful about somebody who gives up at the first sign of struggle, the more you work hard to pursue your goal, the more people will respect you and the more opportunities you will end up having to choose from.

## 2. Do Your Best

At the end of the day, you can only ever do your best, and your best shouldn’t involve trying to sabotage others. If you are truly the best person for a particular job or the making the best case for a certain argument, then your talent will shine through without having to try and make the other people involved look or feel bad. Concentrate on yourself and you will do your very best work.

## 3. No Short Cuts

Make sure that you are not tempted to take any shortcuts in your journey because shortcuts are a sure fire way to slip up later on down the line. Half of the success that you get to enjoy is about the experience that it takes to get there, so if you know that you haven’t done all of the hard work that you were supposed to do, then you won’t be able to shine as bright, both on the inside and the outside.

## 4. Bounce Back

When you experience the inevitable low points and setbacks, make sure to bounce back with positivity rather than negativity. Nobody in the world has ever managed to shine gracefully when looking for revenge of coming at something from an angle of anger or cynicism. Take every set back as a learning experience and use that experience to make sure that you shine twice as bright when you reach the point on the ladder again.

## 5. Positive Team

Make sure that you pick your company wisely. Surround yourself with people who are going to lift you up rather than drag you down, because you will feed off of whatever energy is the closest to you. You can try as hard as you like, but if you don’t have the correct support systems in place, you won’t be able to shine as bright as those who do.
