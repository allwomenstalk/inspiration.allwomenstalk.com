# Little Ways to Make Time for Yourself ...

I am so bad about taking time to myself. I always feel like I have something to do and it makes me feel anxious, so this year, in 2016, I am making it my resolution to take more time for myself. Who's with me?

## 1. Get Enough Sleep

![Get Enough Sleep](https://img.allw.mn/content/ku/si/mh8g1fh5569855a7f39af970226364_500x280.gif)

Because truthfully, you'll be useless without sleep.

## 2. Tea, Drinking Tea is Great

![Tea, Drinking Tea is Great](https://img.allw.mn/content/ac/if/qzy0uu0y569855a814ec3841966033_500x300.gif)

It's especially good when you just need five minutes to yourself.

## 3. Evaluate How You Are Wasting Your Time Each Day

![Evaluate How You Are Wasting Your Time Each Day](https://img.allw.mn/content/fe/d4/r5ex3ty8569855a83060f516040811_500x240.gif)

Whether it be Facebook or Tumblr, you need to figure out how you are spending your time and focus that time more on you.

## 4. Pamper Yourself in the Shower

![Pamper Yourself in the Shower](https://img.allw.mn/content/yu/gy/isxtj7pv569855a936506298685464_500x280.gif)

One day, shave your legs, one day, put on a face mask. Showers shouldn't be all rush, rush, rush – take the extra five minutes.

## 5. Treat Yourself to Lunch

![Treat Yourself to Lunch](https://img.allw.mn/content/sn/aa/vr2zl0b7569855a9dfe96260454250_500x252.gif)

Yes, yes, it's great to save money and bring your lunch, but at work – have something to look forward to and buy lunch and then sit down and eat it with a few coworkers you like best.

## 6. Surround Yourself with Your Friends

![Surround Yourself with Your Friends](https://img.allw.mn/content/mh/bi/l33zfyhe569855aa07383060937704_500x283.gif)

They are absolutely going to make everything better.

## 7. Take a Long Walk

![Take a Long Walk](https://img.allw.mn/content/sy/kf/xk5n5oza569855aaba992964765927_500x281.gif)

This is my favorite and one way I use to get away.

## 8. Oh so Many Pretty Clothes

![Oh so Many Pretty Clothes](https://img.allw.mn/content/e1/y7/bomf9d6u569855aacb389922564284_500x280.gif)

Wear your best clothes and go out!

## 9. Journal? Write down Some Thoughts!

![Journal? Write down Some Thoughts!](https://img.allw.mn/content/dq/jr/z383pxta569855ab54a85292206286_500x250.gif)

It helps. Believe me, it really, really helps.

## 10. DIY Time!

![DIY Time!](https://img.allw.mn/content/a2/h0/amjo38tz569855ac10f2a097605706_500x281.gif)

Have a Pinterest board full of DIY projects? It's a great time to start on them now!

## 11. Clean up Your Space

![Clean up Your Space](https://img.allw.mn/content/cb/vl/sm6r738s589acc708fccc606546552_500x272.gif)

It calms the mind.

## 12. Pop a Bath Bomb and Chill out

![Pop a Bath Bomb and Chill out](https://img.allw.mn/content/ns/ex/ivp3yr3f569855ab6f564304921749_500x281.gif)

Trust me, a bath bomb makes a difference.

## 13. Meditate

![Meditate](https://img.allw.mn/content/em/v1/hvxh3x0n569855ad0d7f9392541046_500x281.gif)

It'll clear up your mind.

## 14. Yoga Helps Too

![Yoga Helps Too](https://img.allw.mn/content/i8/ma/etixm2x9569855accb8de854671709_498x254.gif)

Namaste y'all.

## 15. Color – Best Trend Ever!

![Color – Best Trend Ever!](https://img.allw.mn/content/a0/rk/g6pv7ao8569855af0834d431944028_500x281.gif)

They have a Harry Potter coloring book. Need I say more?

## 16. Hike It up

![Hike It up](https://img.allw.mn/content/o1/oc/l0zwjq8f569855ad5af68400233998_500x281.gif)

Hiking is good for the soul. If you are into that kind of thing!

## 17. Go for a Swim

![Go for a Swim](https://img.allw.mn/content/y8/is/xsmkost2569855ae10205606743961_500x250.gif)
