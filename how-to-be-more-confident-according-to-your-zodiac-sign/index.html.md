# How to Be More Confident According to Your Zodiac Sign ...

As normal human beings, confidence is something that a lot of us struggle with. For several different reasons, we always end up thinking things along the lines of “I’m not good enough”, “I’m not smart enough”, “I’m not pretty enough” and every other kind of negative comment that you can think of, but I’m here to tell you that those thoughts are just that, all in the mind! Thankfully, confidence is definitely something that can be worked on and improved upon, as long as you know which ways are going to be most beneficial for you personally. Here is **how to be more confident according to your own zodiac sign**.

## 1. Aries

The thing with an Aries is that you are often TOO confident, so most of your time is spend trying to temper that! There’s nothing wrong with confidence, but what you need to work on is making sure it doesn’t come across as arrogance.

## 2. Taurus

You are at your best when you are allowed to take your time, so to bring out your confidence, you should try to start your tasks and events earlier than others.

## 3. Gemini

You are most confident when you can dictate the direction and pace of a conversation, so it will help if you are the one who makes the first move to talk.

## 4. Cancer

You get your confidence from being around loved ones and feeling safe and secure, so make more of an effort to see your family and friends more often.

## 5. Leo

You are your most confident when you are looking fine, so make the effort to get dressed up to the nines more often!

## 6. Virgo

You are very detail oriented, so the more you know about a specific situation, the more comfortable and confident you will be around it.

## 7. Libra

You understand the importance of making a good first impression, so your confidence will be higher if you have a little time to prepare yourself for meeting new people.

## 8. Scorpio

You are more confident when given less time to think about things. Your instincts serve you well. It’s when you start having seconds thoughts that the nerves kick in.

## 9. Sagittarius

You get your confidence from gaining knowledge, so your best course of action is to educate yourself as much as you can. It will help you not feel out of place in general conversation.

## 10. Capricorn

Your natural instinct is to quit as soon as something gets hard, but you will find if you persevere through and out of your comfort zone, you will discover and new confidence and bravery that you didn’t know you had.

## 11. Aquarius

Your wheels are always turning, but they turn in a way that always anticipates the worst happening. Try practising some mindfulness so that you don’t catastrophize all the time.

## 12. Pisces

You are at your best when you have been given time to be by yourself and recharge your batteries. You will be more confident with people if you give yourself enough time to prepare alone beforehand.
