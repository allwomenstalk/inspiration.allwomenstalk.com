# Practical Tips to Help Boost  Your Popularity Level for Girls Feeling below Average ...

When they hear the word “popularity,” most people think about snobby girls with Louis Vuitton purses, but that’s not the kind of popularity I am going to talk about in this article. In my opinion, popularity means being noticed, being likeable, being social and getting involved because this is a very valuable skill that will serve you in every aspect of your life. It will also improve your chances of turning heads and getting smiles everywhere you go. Being popular will help you build confidence, build relationships and also, build a large social network that can support you. Here are 7 ways to boost your popularity that you should consider:

## 1. Get Noticed

![Get Noticed](https://img.allw.mn/content/ds/ik/rte6r3li55f87c42933ff828392062_500x274.gif)

One of the first and most important things you should do if you want to boost your popularity is to get noticed. Stop hiding in your corner and let people see what an amazing individual you are. If you spend all your time worrying about how other people think of you or the way you look, you won’t get very far. Instead, try to love who you are and what you do and be proud of yourself.

## 2. Be Confident

![Be Confident](https://img.allw.mn/content/qa/fx/lh1cl5aq55f87cf598dc6148313084_500x220.gif)

No one is perfect since we are all human and we all make mistakes. Don’t focus only on your flaws if you want to be more popular. Focus on your good qualities instead and believe in yourself. If you don’t feel confident, just act in a more confident manner, because this will improve your mood and it will help you develop your confidence.

## 3. Put Yourself out There

![Put Yourself out There](https://img.allw.mn/content/lf/gl/pjt6bxbz55f87d5374d8d714768401_500x190.gif)

This means that if you want to boost your popularity, you should be willing to step out of your comfort zone, meet new people and show them what an amazing individual you are. Just make conversation, crack jokes and engage people. Even if you are shy, quiet or introspective, you need to change how you interact with people if you want to get noticed.

## 4. Find Your Own Style

![Find Your Own Style](https://img.allw.mn/content/qy/sz/uro3keto55f87ff2d4108733398747_500x275.gif)

This doesn’t mean that you have to dye your hair pink or get a tattoo on your face. Just try to find your own style and show people that you feel comfortable being yourself. You don’t have to buy the trendiest clothes that everyone else is wearing if they don’t suit your personality. Just wear something that will fit you well and that will help you get noticed.

## 5. Take Risks

![Take Risks](https://img.allw.mn/content/dn/as/i29u70l655f8817e69944009045869_500x268.gif)

If you want to be popular then you need to take a few risks on a social level. Just be bold and do things that you never felt comfortable doing before. For example, go and introduce yourself to someone you’ve never met, make statements that will make you stand out in a crowd or just interact more with people.

## 6. Ask People about Their Lives

![Ask People about Their Lives](https://img.allw.mn/content/ko/o1/bph2t4u655f8824699b62107155746_500x281.gif)

By talking more about your life, you will manage to build closer friendships with your colleagues but learning more about other people’s lives will help you enter their inner circle. Listen to what other people have to say and pay attention to the little things like their birthday or their hobbies.

## 7. Help Others out

![Help Others out](https://img.allw.mn/content/k7/c9/zybr3htc55f8832a48644734399223_500x282.gif)

If you perform small and random acts of kindness every day, people will like you more, you will feel more positive emotions and it will increase your popularity. Helping people out will make you feel good about yourself since you will learn new skills, you will meet new people and it will boost your self-confidence and self-esteem.

If you want to be more popular, try to be friendlier and more approachable, be dependable and proactive. Do you know any other things people should do to boost their popularity? Please tell us about them in the comments section!
