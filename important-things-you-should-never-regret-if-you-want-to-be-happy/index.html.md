# 7 Important Things You Should Never Regret if You Want to Be Happy ...

There are a few things you should never regret if you truly want to live a happy and fulfilled life. I absolutely love this quote whose author is yet unknown: “The saddest summary of life contains three descriptions: could have, might have, and should have.” We all have regrets, we all wish we would have done some things differently, but you should never regret the lessons life has thought you, because going through those experiences has made you stronger and wiser. As we grow older, we learn more and we realize that we mostly regret the things we didn’t do than the ones we had the courage to try. Here are a few important things you should never regret if you want to be truly happy:

## 1. Saying I Love You

Among the things you should never regret if you want to live a more meaningful life is the fact that you showed your love for your dear ones. Never regret saying I love you, even if those feelings were not mutual! Be honest and cherish the people you love. You wouldn’t want to regret later that you had the chance to tell somebody how you really felt about them and you missed it because you were too afraid to acknowledge your feelings.

## 2. Living the Way You Want to Live

It’s your life and you choose what you want to do with it; after all, you are the only one responsible for your choices. Don’t make bad compromises that could make you feel miserable, just to please everyone around you! Live your life the way you want to live, without any regrets! You can accomplish all your dreams, if you are willing to work hard enough to reach all your goals.

## 3. Dreaming Too Big

Never regret having big dreams! Those big dreams, even if they seem impossible and crazy sometimes are the ones that will motivate you to reach your goals. Don’t let people tell you what you can’t do! Believe in yourself and dream big! Shooting for the stars and landing on the moon is way better than aiming for a mountain and landing on its top.

## 4. Believing in Yourself

In order to live a more happy and healthy life, you should never regret believing in yourself even if you failed sometimes. People make mistakes, we all fail from time to time, but a positive attitude will help you get back on your feet much faster. If you don’t believe in yourself, then no one will. Be your biggest supporter and work hard in order to make all your dreams come true!

## 5. Falling in Love

Never, ever regret that you have fallen in love, even if you did it for the wrong person. You have something to learn from every experience, even from a broken heart. We all want to love and be loved, and that will happen at the right time, but we may have to kiss a lot of frogs until we meet our prince. Don’t run away from love because you are too afraid of getting hurt. You might miss out on some wonderful things and on some of the most amazing experiences.

## 6. Quitting Your Job

Another thing you should never regret if you want to learn how to live a happier and fulfilled life is quitting your job, especially if that was the cause of your unhappiness. If your old job really made you feel miserable and it prevented you from reaching your full potential, then you’ve made the correct choice by quitting. You are not a coward for doing this. There are times when quitting is the best thing you can do in order to become a better person. Don’t be stuck in a dead-end job because you are too afraid to try new things! Be responsible for your actions and for your own happiness!

## 7. Giving It Your All

Never regret giving someone or something the best of you! This way, you won’t have anything to regret, especially the thought that you could have done better. Always go that extra mile if you feel like your goal is worth all that effort and never regret working so hard in order to fulfill your dreams, even if you don’t manage to do that right away. Some things just take time, so always work hard and be patient, because the good things will happen to you at the right time.

There are a lot of things you should never regret doing if you want to learn how to feel happier. I just mentioned a few, but I’m sure there are many more that I haven’t talked about. Could you give me a hand and tell us what other things someone should never regret? Please share your thoughts with us in the comments section!
