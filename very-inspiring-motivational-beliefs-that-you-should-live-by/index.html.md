# 7 Very Inspiring Motivational Beliefs That You Should Live by ...

If your most important goal is to live a happy and fulfilled life, you should try to develop a couple of motivational beliefs that will help you create the world you want to live in. A lot of people are inspired by all kinds of motivational quotes since those very clever words are helping them focus on what they want to obtain and they also guide their behavior in order to reach all their goals. These motivational beliefs can have a very powerful influence on your behavior if you allow them to do so and if you integrate them into your belief system. So, here are a couple of wise statements that I hope you will find inspiring and that will motivate you to fulfill all your dreams:

## 1. “Make It Happen.”

In my opinion, this is one of the most inspiring motivational beliefs that you should live by. You can obtain whatever you dream of if you are willing to work hard and if you have enough patience. Just like Michael Jordan said: “Some people want it to happen, some wish it would happen, others make it happen.” You will gain success in all the areas of your life if you let yourself be guided by these very wise words.

## 2. “Be Passionate about What You do.”

Try to be passionate about every little thing that you do, whether you are at work or just hanging out with your friends. By bringing passion into your life, you will overcome more easily all the difficulties you’ll have to face and you will live your life to the fullest. Leave all the worries behind and enjoy whatever it is that you are doing, no matter if you are taking out the trash or you are writing a novel.

## 3. “I Am the Product of My Choices.”

Keep these magical words in mind the next time you have to make a big decision, because we are indeed the product of our choices, whether we like it or not. Learn to take responsibility for your actions and even if you make some mistakes along the way (don’t worry, we all do, because we are human after all), learn from them, so you’ll avoid them in your future. Failure is actually the best teacher you’ll ever have so don’t be afraid to experience it and learn to assume the consequences of your actions.

## 4. “I Have so Much to Be Grateful for!”

It is true; you really have a lot to be grateful for! Just take a look around and you’ll realize in an instant how lucky you really are. You have a roof over your head, something to eat and people who love and care about yo. Don’t dwell on the things you don’t have or that you haven’t managed to obtain yet. Learn to recognize the things that make you so fortunate and simply be happy for being yourself!

## 5. “I Must Try.”

No matter how many times you’ve failed before, you shouldn’t give up when the road becomes a bit rocky. You never know what might happen if you persevere and if you don’t give up on your dreams. As I said, failure is the best teacher you’ll ever have, so accept the fact that things may not work out as planned sometimes. When it comes to this topic, I love Michael Jordan’s motivational advice: “I can accept failure, everyone fails at something. But I can’t accept not trying.”.

## 6. “Stay Committed.”

Whenever you start doing something, stay committed and don’t give up until you manage to reach your goal. Put in all the work that your dream requires from you and learn to be patient! Even if you have to make a lot of sacrifices to reach all your goals, you will be rewarded with the satisfaction and the peace you’ll experience once you’ll manage to fulfill all your dreams. I absolutely love this famous quote by Tony Robbins: “Stay committed to your decisions, but stay flexible in your approach.”.

## 7. “I Control My Attitude.”

In life there actually are a lot of things you can’t control, like your past or what other people think or say, yet, you can choose to control your attitude. Just like Michael Jordan said: “Always turn a negative situation into a positive situation.”. A good attitude and a positive outlook on life will help you overcome all kinds of obstacles you might encounter in your path, so you can really say that attitude is everything sometimes.

This is my list of motivational beliefs that you should live by in order to be successful in everything that you do. Do you know any other inspiring beliefs that we should add to this list? Do tell!
