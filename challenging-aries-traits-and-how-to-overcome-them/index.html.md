# 7 Challenging Aries Traits and How to Overcome Them ...

Being born under the sign of Aries means that you have a lot of great traits such as being fun, enthusiastic and energetic. But like all signs, being an Aries has its challenges. It’s good to be aware of what those challenges are so that you can work to overcome them. As with the positive characteristics of the Aries sign, you’ll probably have some but not all of these.

## 1. You May Be Impulsive

As an Aries, you may be impulsive. You might make decisions on the fly that you later realize weren’t the best choices. You act quickly and don’t always stop to consider the impact of your actions to yourself or others. You can overcome this by being deliberate about taking a pause to think before making decisions. Even a couple of minutes to think things over can make a difference.

## 2. You Might Be Moody

Aries individuals are known for being moody. If this is true of you, then you’re probably aware of it. Most Aries individuals will freely admit to being moody, as well. Moods come and go but there are things you can do to help yourself shake them off. Even just making a decision to try to shake off a bad mood can help.

## 3. You Could Be Bossy

Aries individuals make great leaders but they can go a bit too far. You may come across as bossy if you aren’t careful. You may have the best intentions in the world. However, people may not see that if you offend them. Framing things as suggestions instead of instructions can be very helpful here.

## 4. You May Seem like a Know-it-all

Going hand in hand with coming across bossy is the fact that you may come across as a know-it-all. You probably don’t mean to do this in any way. Most likely you just want to be helpful. But it can be taken wrong if you aren’t careful how you say things. It can hurt your relationships with others if you’re this way and don’t work to overcome it.

## 5. You May Not Finish What You Start

An Aries is a person that may not finish what they start. And that’s okay if what you started truly isn’t working out or you see that it’s the wrong choice. But don’t allow yourself to quit things because you feel overwhelmed in the moment. If you persevere, you’ll be so proud of your accomplishments. Sometimes it’s better to hang in there.

## 6. You May Have Childish Moments

Let’s be honest. We’re all given to childish moments at some point. An Aries is just a little more given to them than other horoscope signs. Not getting what you want could be a trigger to act childish. Thankfully, you always have a choice about how you act and react in situations. It’s something you can work on.

## 7. You May Have a Tendency to Look down on Others

An Aries may tend to look down on others at times. It can be helpful to remind yourself that you don’t know the whole story of a person’s situation. That can help you to keep a balanced view. Practicing empathy for others can help you to conquer this. Empathy will actually make this challenging trait fade away.

These are 7 challenging traits that an Aries could have. Do you see any of these in yourself? How spot-on was this for you?
