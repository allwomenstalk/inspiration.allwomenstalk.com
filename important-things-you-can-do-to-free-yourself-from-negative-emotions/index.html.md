# 7 Important Things You Can do to Free Yourself from Negative Emotions ...

In order to live a happy and fulfilled life, you need to free yourself from negative emotions. Sometimes you can feel a bit overwhelmed if you let all those negative emotions build up within you. You won’t manage to see to wonderful things in your life that you need to be grateful for and you won’t even realize how lucky you are to just be you. You can learn how to get rid of all those negative emotions and how to avoid getting stuck in their vicious circle. Just like Brian Tracy said: “The greatest enemies of success and happiness are negative emotions of all kinds. Negative emotions hold you down, tire you out, and take away all your joy in life. Negative emotions from the beginning of time, have done more harm to individuals and societies than all the plagues of history.”. Here are a few very effective things you can do to free yourself from negative emotions, so you won’t allow them to hold you back:

## 1. Don’t Make Excuses!

One of the most important things you should do if you want to learn how to free yourself from negative emotions is to avoid making excuses for your behavior and for other people as well. You are responsible for your own actions, so try to be more reasonable and don’t let your emotions influence your decisions. Don’t victimize yourself and take some action, so you can change the way you think and especially, the way you act on certain occasions.

## 2. Rise above Other People’s Opinions

I’m not saying other people’s opinions are not important, just don’t let them control you and don’t let them influence your decisions. Do what you think it’s right, control your emotions and take responsibility for your actions! A lot of people can be mean without a particular reason, so don’t let their poisonous words hurt you and do not give them so much power! Know your self-worth and don’t let anyone else but you define your self-image!

## 3. Quit Your Bad Habits

Quit your bad habits and free yourself from negative emotions! It may seem a bit difficult at first, but with a lot of work and with a bit of patience, I’m sure you’ll succeed to reach all your goals. Some bad habits will simply bring you down, so you need to get rid of them in order to live a happier and healthier life. Try to avoid engaging in all kinds of behaviors that will make you feel angry or depressed and focus on the wonderful things in your life instead.

## 4. Avoid Bad Influences

Avoid bad influences and people who say they are your friends but who constantly bring you down. Don’t let anything, and especially anyone, have a negative effect on your feelings, on your self-esteem or on your self-confidence! Surround yourself with positive, optimistic and happy people and you’ll realize shortly that their healthy attitude has rubbed off on you and that you now share the same views.

## 5. Be Grateful

Be grateful for everything you have and for what a wonderful person you are. If you really think about it, you’ll realize that you actually have a lot to be thankful for, so let go of all those negative feelings and just enjoy the numerous beauties the world has to offer. Make a habit out of thinking of at least one thing every day that you should be grateful for and you will free yourself from all those negative emotions in no time.

## 6. You Can do Whatever You Want

Just remove the words “I can’t” from your vocabulary and be aware of the fact that you can do whatever you want to do if you are willing to work hard enough to accomplish all your goals and fulfill all your dreams. Start believing in yourself and give yourself some credit! Free yourself from negative emotions and start enjoying your life!

## 7. Stop Justifying

In order to get rid of all your negative emotions, you’ll need to stop justifying them. Just like Brain Tracy says: “The first of the four root causes of negative emotions is justification. You can be negative only as long as you can justify to yourself and others that you are entitled to be angry or upset for some reason. This is why angry people are continually explaining and elaborating on the reasons for their negative feelings. However, if you cannot justify your negativity, you cannot be angry.”.

There are a lot of things you can do to free yourself from negative emotions. I just mentioned a few in this little article but I’m sure there are many more I haven’t talked about. Can you give me a hand and tell me what other important things you can do to free yourself from negative emotions? Please share your thoughts with us in the comments section!
