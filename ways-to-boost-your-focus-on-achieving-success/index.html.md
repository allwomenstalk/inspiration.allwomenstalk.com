# 9 Ways to Supercharge Your Focus and Achieve Success ...

**_Focus_** - Ah! The elusive superpower we all crave, especially in today's frenzy-filled 2024, where Instagram reels, Tik Tok dances, and the endless binge-watch lists on Netflix could easily take up a PhD in our lives. But have no fear! This article is here to help you **supercharge** your focus and achieve success in a world full of shiny distractions. Trust me, as someone who attempted to master the art of focusing while taking a Calculus midterm and scrolling through social media simultaneously, I've picked up a trick or two.

In our quest to **_boost productivity_**, many of us have tried elaborate time management systems, expensive courses, and a medley of mental exercises, only to end up where we started – with a wandering mind and a cluttered schedule. Spoiler alert: You don't need a magic potion or a secret code. What you need is a practical guide with actionable steps.

We're diving into the magical world of **_mindfulness_**, busting the myth that focusing is akin to enduring a dental appointment. With the right strategies, **_multitasking_** will be a thing of the past-instead, you'll discover the joy of deep, uninterrupted work. Think of it as breaking free from a lifelong diet of junk food and discovering the flavors of gourmet wellness.

Let's talk about our largest nemesis – procrastination. Yes, the monster under your bed that's fed by endless emails and random YouTube videos on 'how to train your hamster'. By incorporating the [first strategy](#1) like 'Setting Clear Goals', you can kickstart a journey of improved **objective clarity**. Remember the satisfaction of finally cleaning out that closet you've been avoiding? That's what hitting those focused milestones will feel like.

One revolutionary tip we're going to discuss is harnessing the power of **hydration**. Wait, what? Yes, staying hydrated can keep those brain cells in peak condition. And no, this doesn’t mean guzzling down gallons of coffee – water is your best friend here, as we’ll discuss in [point five](#5).

And who can forget about taking breaks? It's an art. You might have heard the phrase "work hard, play harder" - well, it turns out, your brain works the same way. We’re going to dive into strategic break-taking and how the art of doing nothing can actually **improve mental stamina** aka [point three](#3).

So buckle up, get ready for a fascinating journey to reclaim your inner focus ninja. Who knows? By the end, you might even turn into a focus superhero among your peers, making 2024 the year you conquer your goals while leaving your hamster training videos (for the most part) unwatched.

## 1. Trust Your Intuition

One of the best ways to focus on excellence is to train yourself to trust your intuition and go with your gut. Sometimes the logical choice isn’t always the choice that is going to bring you the most success, and our bodies are usually really good t letting us know what is and what isn’t right. Listen to what your intuition is telling you; it usually knows best!

## 2. Clarity

When trying to achieve a certain goal, you should do your best to block out all other external factors that are not strictly related to succeeding in your chosen task. Procrastination and distraction are the archenemies of achievement, so make sure that your mind has as much clarity as possible in order to smash your targets and reach your goals.

## 3. Have Conviction

A person’s personal success can so often be curtailed by their inability to put themselves first and say no to others who ask too much of them. To achieve your personal goals, you need to have courage in your convictions and courage to be able to put yourself first and let others down gently when their requests directly affect your own targets.

## 4. No Fear

Fear is the number one reason why some people do not go that extra mile to achieve their dreams and success. Whether it is fear of change, fear of failure or fear of putting yourself out there, you need to override this natural instinct to hide away and really push for your dreams. It is normal to be afraid of pushing yourself, but the important part is to cast this fear aside and jump in head first.

## 5. Be Happy

It’s no secret that a happier a person is, the more fulfilled their life will seem. True, it’s not as easy as just wishing for happiness and having it granted to you, but finding happiness and achieving your goals often go hand in hand. Whether than means quitting a job that you hate in order to try for the one that you dream about, or something on a smaller scale, the link between happiness and success stays the same.

## 6. Seek a Mentor

In order to really excel in your chosen field, it can be a really good idea to seek out a mentor who can give you good advice and guide you in the right direction when you need help. Having somebody to talk to is always a great form of therapy and can be so helpful.

## 7. Be Decisive

Sometimes, the longer it takes for you to mull over certain decisions, the further away achieving your dreams seems to be. I’m not saying act super impulsively, but make the effort not to let certain decisions drag on for longer than they have to. Be decisive and you will reap the rewards.

## 8. Don’t Settle

Life is way too short to be settling for second best, and this is even more so the case when it comes to reaching for your ultimate goals. Instead of taking the easy option, stick to your guns and demand the best. This is only way that you are going to end up with what you deserve.

## 9. Don’t Compare Yourself

We all have different and varying dreams and goals, so don’t measure your own success with the success of somebody who is aspiring to completely things than you. There is no direct correlation, and the exercise will only prove to be disheartening for you if you find that the other person is achieving more at this precise moment.

The only things really standing in the way of you achieving excellence and success is you. Be strong and focussed and you'll succeed.
