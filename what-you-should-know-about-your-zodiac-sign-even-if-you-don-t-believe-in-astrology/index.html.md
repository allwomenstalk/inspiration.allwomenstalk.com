# What You Should Know about Your Star Sign Even if You Dont Believe in Astrology ...

I'm here to tell you all about **what you should know about your zodiac sign even if you don’t believe in astrology**. No matter what kinds of personal beliefs to do with spirituality and the great unknown that we all hold as individuals, there is no getting away from the fact that astrology is something that has a following of millions and millions all over the world! Even if you think it is all rubbish, I bet you still know your own star sign, don’t you? Yep, I knew it! You can’t turn a page in a teen or celebrity magazine without some reference to astrology, so don’t you think you are better off knowing a little about it rather than being oblivious? Here is **what you should know about your zodiac sign even if you don’t believe in astrology**.

## 1. Gemini

You are a natural born detective, and your superhero like senses mean that you can spot a phony from ten miles away!

## 2. Cancer

You like to jump in head first in all situation, so when it comes to love, you have no other option than to love long and love hard.

## 3. Leo

You are as ferocious as the lion that symbolises your sign, and you will defend the people that you love no matter what the cost may be.

## 4. Virgo

You hate conflict, so you are known to be very flexible in terms of accepting and going with the plans of other people who aren’t so open.

## 5. Libra

I hate to break it to you, but you are probably the most indecisive person in the entire world! You couldn’t make a choice for a group even it a million dollars was on the line!

## 6. Scorpio

You have a natural air of mystery about you that comes off as very magnetic. You will find that people are drawn to you after only meeting you once.

## 7. Sagittarius

You don’t think about the past and you can’t be bothered to look into the future, you are all about the here and now and the impact that you are making at this current time.

## 8. Capricorn

You don’t always want to admit it, especially to new acquaintances, but you are pretty much the best at everything. Your talents are diverse and there isn’t a single thing you can’t master.

## 9. Aquarius

Some people call it a talent, some people call it a flaw, it’s the fact that you absolutely cannot lie. You are passionate about telling the truth, even if avoiding it might sometimes be the better option!

## 10. Pisces

You are emotional and intuitive, which makes you sensitive but also, unfortunately, makes you a person who tends to overthink every little thing.

## 11. Aries

You are someone who acts before you think a lot, so you often find yourself asking for forgiveness after the fact rather than permission beforehand.

## 12. Taurus

If there were ever a competition held to see who is the most loyal and lucky person in the world, you would be the winner of that strange but positive combo!
