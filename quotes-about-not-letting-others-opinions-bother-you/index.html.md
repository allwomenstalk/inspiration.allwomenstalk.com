# 7 Quotes about Not Letting Others Opinions Bother You ...

There are quotes about other people's opinions that will help you realize that you shouldn't care about the nasty things people say about you. Gossip can be disgusting, so it's best to remember that you're an amazing human being, no matter what others think. Here are a few quotes about other people's opinions to remind you that you're perfect, even though others think otherwise:

## 1. Reality's in the Reflection

![Les Furets,human action,text,product,font,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pv/id/umlbp_les-furets_human-action_text_product_font_600x411.jpg)

When someone spreads nasty rumors about you, it says more about them than you. It shows that they're the type of person who cannot be trusted and that they enjoy pointless drama. This is one of the most reassuring quotes about other people's opinions, because it lets you know that the person badmouthing you is the flawed one, not you.

## 2. They're Just Jealous

![text,font,handwriting,brand,writing,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tn/z2/d5n2p_text_font_handwriting_brand_writing_403x275.jpg)

Have you ever heard the phrase "bad publicity is better than no publicity?" If people are gossiping about you, it means that they care about you. There must be a reason that they're paying attention to you, because if they thought you were actually a horrible person, they wouldn't bother acknowledging you.

## 3. Importance of Situations

![text,font,brand,emotion,line,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yv/xa/r12h1_text_font_brand_emotion_line_483x306.jpg)

If someone is judging you based on the small amount of detail they know about your situation, their opinion is invalid. Unless you sat them down to explain exactly what's going on, then whatever they're saying isn't accurate. If they don't know the situation, their opinion shouldn't matter to you, because they don't know the full story.

## 4. Think Things through

![Pandora,Pride and Prejudice and Zombies (2013),text,font,black and white,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ul/p2/u80c9_pandora_pride-and-prejudice-and-zombies-2013_text_font_black-and-white_500x472.jpg)

If someone you don't care about is talking badly about you, why does it matter? If they aren't important enough to ever cross your mind, then you shouldn't be sad about the nasty things they say. They don't deserve to be a part of your life or your thoughts. Just ignore them.

## 5. Value Your Own Opinion

![Northern Writers Awards,text,font,brand,presentation,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ss/em/escom_northern-writers-awards_text_font_brand_presentation_550x600.jpg)

Sometimes, other's opinions will get you down, whether you like it or not. Even if the insults are true, you shouldn't let them make you miserable. You have to continue to have confidence. You're a beautiful person and have to learn to ignore others if you want to remember how much you're worth.

## 6. The Right to Be Wrong

![Juice Bar,Horsefeathers,text,font,poster,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ox/w2/he19q_juice-bar_horsefeathers_text_font_poster_500x521.jpg)

If someone tells you their opinion, even when you didn't ask for it, don't get angry. They have the right to judge you, just like you have the right to ignore them. You also have the right to tell them that their opinion completely differs from yours. That's the beauty of freedom of speech.

## 7. You Matter Most

![Handshake,text,font,product,line,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zt/zc/xw5wy_handshake_text_font_product_line_600x464.jpg)

At the end of the day, your opinion is the only one that matters. It's not selfish to care more about your own thoughts than other's thoughts. You have a responsibility to be your own person, and that means that you can have your own, unique thoughts. Don't let others impact you with their nasty words. Keep your head held high and remember that you're as beautiful as you were before the gossip started.

People like to talk, and it's hard to blame them for it. Even if you're anti-drama, you've gossiped about others in the same way that they've gossiped about you. Of course, that doesn't make it right. Do you let other people's opinions bother you or are you good at ignoring them?
