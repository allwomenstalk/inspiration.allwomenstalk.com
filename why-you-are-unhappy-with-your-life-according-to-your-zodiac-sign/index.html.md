# Why You Are Unhappy with Your Life According to Your Zodiac Sign ...

Ever wondered **why you are unhappy with your life according to your zodiac sign**? If you were to be completely honest with yourself, everybody can find something in their lives that makes them a little unhappy. For some people that might be a really obvious, overbearing thing, but for others, it could be just a small annoyance that only rears its ugly head once in a blue moon. Or perhaps, the case might be that you are feeling a little down in the dumps lately but you can’t quite put your finger on why that might be? Well, why not turn to astrology to see if it can reveal some answers? Here is **why you are unhappy with your life according to your zodiac sign**.

## 1. Gemini

You are someone who likes to feel tethered, so your unhappiness is probably coming from a place of not feeling like you have any real direction right now.

## 2. Cancer

You are very emotionally sensitive, so it could be the case that you haven't surrounded yourself with a group of people to make you truly happy.

## 3. Leo

You don’t feel happy and content because at this point in your life you have still to fully develop a complete sense of self. You don’t know who you are yet.

## 4. Virgo

You have always been sensitive, and your unhappiness is usually triggered by worrying and caring too much about what other people think of you.

## 5. Libra

You are all about balance and structure, so it is probably the case that you have yet to find a perfect balance between work and leisure.

## 6. Scorpio

You are the quintessential over thinker, and with that many thoughts flying around in your head, it is no surprise that you are unable to find peace and contentment.

## 7. Sagittarius

You are a person who needs to be active and busy to really thrive, so perhaps it is the case that you are simply not doing enough to satisfy yourself right now.

## 8. Capricorn

You are probably unhappy at the moment because for one reason or another you don’t feel particularly grounded. Are you between jobs? Between homes? Perhaps between relationships?

## 9. Aquarius

You are unhappy because you aren’t being as successful as you want to be, but this stems from not having ambitions that are grand enough for your talents in the first place.

## 10. Pisces

You are at your happiest when you have an easy and available outlet for your natural creativity, so it would make sense that that is the thing you are lacking in particular right now.

## 11. Aries

You feel like there is something missing and that you are not quite complete and content, and that is because you are not being challenged enough by those in your life.

## 12. Taurus

You are a very visual person, so the unhappiness in your life right now is stemming from the fact that you have been unable to find the right aesthetics both for yourself and your surroundings.
