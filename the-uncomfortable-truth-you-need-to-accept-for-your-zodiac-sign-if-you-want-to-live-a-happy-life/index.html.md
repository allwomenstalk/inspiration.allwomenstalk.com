# The Uncomfortable Truth You Need to Accept for Your Zodiac Sign if You Want to Live a Happy Life ...

I think we could all agree that above everything else, the main thing we want to be in life is happy. If you have happiness as your foundation, then it feels like everything else just slots into place, and you tend to worry much less about the smaller, more insignificant things because at the end of the day, you are happy and silly stuff isn’t going to dent that! However, to achieve true happiness, it is often the case that you need to overcome something personal before it is possible. Here is the **uncomfortable truth you need to accept for your zodiac sign if you want to live a happy life.**

## 1. Aries

Not everyone in the world is going to be as loyal as you are, so don’t put your trust in every single person you meet.

## 2. Taurus

Sometimes your generosity will go unappreciated, so don’t do good deeds with the sole hope of getting something back.

## 3. Gemini

Your natural charisma isn’t always going to be enough, so make sure that you do more work on yourself than just cultivating the surface personality.

## 4. Cancer

Nobody in the world can escape pain altogether, so don’t be naïve enough to think that you are never going to go through a bad patch.

## 5. Leo

No human being is immune to disappointment, so don’t expect to have your expectations met every single time something happens.

## 6. Virgo

Sometimes those kind gestures that you like to do for others don’t always come across as they were intended. Try not to come over as patronising or condescending.

## 7. Libra

It’s absolutely impossible to be liked by everybody, so don’t try to be. Focus on those in your life who you already know love you; give them more of your attention.

## 8. Scorpio

Not everyone recognises a good person when they see one, so don’t waste your time trying to convince someone of your character when they aren’t even worth it in the first place.

## 9. Sagittarius

Pessimism can be as infectious as optimism, so try not to let negative thoughts and cynicism creep in to your mind too much.

## 10. Capricorn

Trying hard isn’t always going to be the thing that gets you success. Sometimes luck has more to do with it, but don’t let that get you down. It shouldn’t stop you from always reaching to achieve your goals.

## 11. Aquarius

There really is no such thing as an original idea, so don’t get hung up on trying to be the person who comes up with something innovative and groundbreaking;just work with what you’ve got.

## 12. Pisces

Overanalysing all the time will hold you back more often than not. You don’t always have to understand and pick apart every detail of a situation in order to be able to go with it. Just go with the flow!
