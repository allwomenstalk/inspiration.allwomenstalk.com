# 7 Quotes to Help You Survive Your Quarter Life Crisis  ...

Forget midlife crises. Life is stressful for us young women, which is why we suffer from quarter life crises. Of course, you're capable of surviving them. Check out the quotes that _You Queen_ compiled to help you through your dark days:

## 1. Francis of Assisi

![Francis of Assisi](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ck/yv/iglktmdp563a72b525755471908387_585x585.jpg)

Nothing is impossible, so delete the word from your vocabulary.

## 2. Christopher Columbus

![Christopher Columbus](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rm/aj/k7sw91cs563a72b5e2e69018656934_585x846.jpg)

He might not be the best role model, but he spoke the truth.

## 3. Steve Jobs

![Steve Jobs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g0/dv/xuq20pl9563a72b38b4ec268036147_500x333.jpg)

Do what you want to do, not what others want you to do.

## 4. Jim Rohn

![Jim Rohn](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lq/mt/ud7jkgi2563a72b589fe0980876630_585x585.jpg)

Take charge of your own life, because you're meant to be in control.

## 5. Alice Walker

![Alice Walker](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gr/mm/io245ysn563a72b447a37270264923_520x347.jpg)

You're more powerful than you think.

## 6. Thomas Edison

![Thomas Edison](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x1/dh/yzg7yjlv563a72b4922f3807278094_600x338.jpg)

If you "fail" once, try again until you succeed.

## 7. Babe Ruth

![Babe Ruth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t3/zi/b46m0wti563a72b514cd2765091223_550x550.jpg)

Every single day, you're a day closer to success.

## 8. The Maine

![New Orleans, text, font, brand, THIS,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lq/hg/j4xyh31m587263e660ab4953056510_600x808.jpg)

You may feel lost right now, but this too shall pass.

## 9. Steve Jobs

![text, font, product, line, brand,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lg/o0/rzha8o2c587264328c623500369397_580x552.jpg)

So true!

## 10. D.H Sidebottom

![text, font, astronomy, night, astronomical object,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ff/um/yjbrppr75872662a22ae7391156651_500x697.jpg)
