# Your Secret Fear According to Your Zodiac Sign ...

Everyone loves a good horoscope, and I'm no different. Your horoscope can tell you all kinds of things, including **your secret fear according to your star sign.** Maybe you know you're afraid of this thing, but it's always great to be validated by the stars, right?

Ready to find out **your secret fear according to your star sign?** The answer may just surprise you.

## 1. Aries (March 21-April 19)

Aries babies tend to hide their innermost feelings. They love to act as if they are fine letting someone go, but in reality they are scared to lose the people they love. As soon as they walk out the door they regret fighting and want those people back. If you are an Aries, **your secret fear according to your star sign** is that you will love the ones you love.

## 2. Taurus (April 20-May 20)

​This may not be obvious to Taurus babies, but they are constantly wondering if they actually mean something to the people around them. They are afraid that they are not important and someday people will realize that they don’t mean anything to them and leave. They can get forgetful and lazy sometimes and that makes them think people will leave them for someone better.

## 3. Gemini (May 21-June 20)

​Gemini babies are terrified to be alone. They would rather change themselves just to fit in and be friends with everyone. Gemini people will adapt to any situation and try to be relatable to everyone around them because being alone scares the shit out of them.

## 4. Cancer (June 21-July 22)

​Cancer babies try to be happy all the time. They want people to see them happy and strong because they are afraid that if someone sees them upset, they will disappoint them. They will act as if everything is going the way they want it to because they don’t want people to worry about them.

## 5. Leo (July 23-August 22)

​As much as Leos love themselves, they are the most sensitive person you will ever meet. They are afraid that they are unlovable and everything that goes wrong is somehow their fault. They will act tough, but deep inside they are the most affectionate person you will ever meet.

## 6. Virgo (August 23-September 22)

Virgo babies are naturally the over thinker of all the signs and they have a trouble handling life. It may seem like they have everything in order, as if they have control of everything that is happening to them, but in reality they have an issue making it every hour of the day. It is also very hard for them to stay positive. They are very perfectionistic in nature and they question everything to the point that it scares them.

## 7. Libra (September 23-October 22)

Libra babies tend to hide their problems and pretend that everything is fine because they hate facing them alone. They are in need of a lover so they can invest their time on that person and avoid the putting the focus on themselves. They tend to balance everything and often think that if one thing slips off of their hands, it will ruin everything they ever worked hard for.

## 8. Scorpio (October 23-November 21)

Scorpio babies cannot trust people. Don’t get me wrong, they want people to trust them, but they have a hard time trusting people back, thinking that not everyone will understand what is going on in their lives. They are afraid that someday they will push away people who ever try to come close to them and they will not be able to fix it.

## 9. Sagittarius (November 22-December 21)

All Sagittarius babies want is to love and to be loved. They can get pretty adventurous, but only with the people they love. When they are not with someone, they have a tendency to feel alone and hopeless, like they will never be good enough for anyone at all.

## 10. Capricorn (December 22-January 19)

​Capricorns are strivers of success. They will always fight their way up to the top. They tend not to have deep connections with anyone because they know that they will never settle until they get what they want. For being over-achievers, they are afraid that they will end up alone forever.

## 11. Aquarius (January 20 to February 18)

Aquarius people tend to keep their distance from people and relationships are not their best suit. They will get into a relationship and not feel anything at all and because of this, they feel like there is something wrong with them. They want to feel something, but somehow they can’t and they hate themselves for it.

## 12. Pisces (February 19 to March 20)

​Pisces babies really don’t have any idea on what’s happening to them or in their lives. They often daydream to make themselves feel better. They are lost and confused, but act as if they have everything under control. They just want an escape from reality because they think that the world is terrible and they have a hard time coping with it.
