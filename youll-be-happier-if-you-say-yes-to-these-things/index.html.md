# Youll Be Happier if You Say Yes to These 17 Things in Life ...

It's important to know how to say "no." After all, you don't want to put too much on your plate at once or agree to do something that you're uncomfortable with. However, it's just as important to learn how to say "yes." After all, you'll be happier if you start saying "yes" to these things:

## 1. Exercising

![Exercising](https://img.allw.mn/content/c2/ev/cuk5duc856a8fb792941d400250355_500x223.gif)

The more you exercise, the better you'll feel. After all, working out can help you lose weight, keep your body healthy, and raise your confidence.

## 2. Going out on Dates

![Going out on Dates](https://img.allw.mn/content/r2/ar/b6vkrpzt56a8fb791ef74256222328_500x206.gif)

If a boy asks you out, don't be afraid to take him up on the offer. If you don't like him after the first date, there's no rule that says you need to go on a second date with him. Just enjoy the free meal!

## 3. Traveling

![Traveling](https://img.allw.mn/content/zx/la/yukpdhdh56a8fb797a4ba140506949_430x242.gif)

If you have the opportunity to travel, you should take it. There are so many beautiful sights in the world that you'd be lucky to see.

## 4. Attending Parties

![crowd, rock concert, people, audience, performance,](https://img.allw.mn/content/hm/tv/shedt46l5891b0fa44709912010777_500x227.gif)

Don't turn down every invitation you receive. Even if you think a party is going to be a dud, it could end up being the best night of your life. You can always leave early if you end up miserable.

## 5. Talking to New People

![Talking to New People](https://img.allw.mn/content/xn/dj/i07u637t56a8fb79b3cef454466474_500x250.gif)

If you want to make more friends, then you need to talk to new people. It's not as scary as it sounds.

## 6. Helping Others

![Helping Others](https://img.allw.mn/content/c0/oy/hf2encqn56a8fb79cad25907051186_500x281.gif)

Doing volunteer work can make you happier than ever. After all, it feels good to do good.

## 7. Cooking

![Cooking](https://img.allw.mn/content/yf/bb/m94he41356a8fb7a4f259139494679_500x281.gif)

You don't want to eat junk food and fast food for every meal. That's why it's important for you to learn how to cook in your own kitchen. It'll make your body happier, which will end up making you happier as a whole.

## 8. Taking a Vacation

![Taking a Vacation](https://img.allw.mn/content/v9/s1/hkfpsf6156a8fb7a3fd51734330546_400x211.gif)

You don't have to spend hundreds of dollars on plane tickets and hotel rooms. Just take off from your job for a week so you can relax at home.

## 9. Having a Girls' Night

![Having a Girls' Night](https://img.allw.mn/content/in/ir/d8g1b0sf56a8fb7a5e838531028695_500x275.gif)

Your friendships are just as important as your romantic relationships. That's why you should set aside time to spend with your gal pals.

## 10. Dancing

![Dancing](https://img.allw.mn/content/xn/n2/g17zihid56a8fb7b1631f859493666_500x281.gif)

It doesn't matter if you have the worst dance moves in the world. If you forget about what you look like, you'll have fun dancing around your room or a club.

## 11. Reading

![TRAPPED,](https://img.allw.mn/content/o9/bh/a4yji41e5891b0fa3548f973912645_500x278.gif)

If you don't have enough money to explore the world, then reading is the second best thing. It'll take you to places you've never heard of before.

## 12. Enjoying Some Alone Time

![Enjoying Some Alone Time](https://img.allw.mn/content/s7/xo/kmabxa4a56a8fb7aaad8c377439014_500x213.gif)

Don't make plans for every single hour of the day. It's okay to spend some time alone in your bedroom. In fact, it's healthy.

## 13. Cleaning

![Cleaning](https://img.allw.mn/content/r1/m3/mp9kzgbn56a8fb7b7b6d1268393419_499x214.gif)

Even if you hate tidying up, you'll feel better coming home to a clean house. When your room is decluttered, your mind will feel decluttered and you'll end up being more productive.

## 14. Trying New Foods

![Trying New Foods](https://img.allw.mn/content/lr/nh/afwptn2o56a8fb7b23e84752664754_500x269.gif)

Your taste buds deserve to try new things. So don't order the same meal every time you go out to eat. There are plenty of other items on the menu.

## 15. Hanging out in Person

![Hanging out in Person](https://img.allw.mn/content/ju/u1/cimduumz56a8fb7b875af434739789_500x281.gif)

Instead of texting your friends or playing a game with them over XBox, you should see them in person. There's nothing like an old fashioned, face-to-face conversation.

## 16. Visiting New Places

![play, screenshot,](https://img.allw.mn/content/nj/u8/ngym72jc5891b0fa7e53a139781392_400x225.gif)

Tell your friends to meet you at a new club or tell your boyfriend to take you to a new restaurant. Don't settle for the same old scenery.

## 17. Spending Money

![Spending Money](https://img.allw.mn/content/nk/yb/cjyu52th56a8fb7bda14b244862172_450x241.gif)

No, you don't want to waste all of your cash on stupid things. However, money is meant to be spent. If there's something you're dying to own, don't feel guilty about splurging on it.

If you want to be happy, then you need to make certain changes in your life. What are you going to say "yes" to today?
