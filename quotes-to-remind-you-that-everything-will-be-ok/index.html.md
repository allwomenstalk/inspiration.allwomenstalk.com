# 15 Inspiring Quotes to Remind You That Everything Will Be Okay ...

There are so many **quotes to remind you that everything will be ok**. By hanging some of them around your house, you'll always have a great reminder that things aren't so bad after all. When you need **quotes to remind you that everything will be ok**, these are sure to make you feel better.

## 1. “One Day, You'll Make Peace with Your Demons, and the Chaos in Your Heart Will Settle Flat. and Maybe for the First Time in Your Life, Life Will Smile Right Back at You and Welcome You Home...”

-Robert M. Drake

## 2. “We Will Become Who We Are Meant to Become.”

-Mahatma Gandhi

## 3. “Today You Are You, That is Truer than True. There is No One Alive Who is Youer than You.”

Dr. Seuss

## 4. “in Any Given Moment We Have Two Options: to Step Forward into Growth or to Step Back into Safety.”

-Abraham H. Maslow

## 5. “if You Only Walk on Sunny Days You'll Never Reach Your Destination.”

-Paulo Coelho

## 6. “Only in the Darkness Can You See the Stars.”

-Martin Luther King Jr.

## 7. “Hope is the Thing with Feathers That Perches in the Soul

And sings the tune without the words
And never stops at all.”
-Emily Dickinson

## 8. “They Say a Person Needs Just Three Things to Be Truly Happy in This World: Someone to Love, Something to do, and Something to Hope for.”

-Tom Bodett

## 9. “You Can Cut All the Flowers but You Cannot Keep Spring from Coming.”

-Pablo Neruda

## 10. “It's Really a Wonder That I Haven't Dropped All My Ideals, Because They Seem so Absurd and Impossible to Carry out. Yet I Keep Them, Because in Spite of Everything, I Still Believe That People Are Really Good at Heart.”

-Anne Frank

## 11. “You Cannot Swim for New Horizons until You Have Courage to Lose Sight of the Shore.”

-William Faulkner

## 12. “Try to Be a Rainbow in Someone's Cloud.”

-Maya Angelou

## 13. “Nothing is Impossible, the Word Itself Says 'I'm Possible'!”

-Audrey Hepburn

## 14. “No Act of Kindness, No Matter How Small, is Ever Wasted.”

-Aesop

## 15. “as We Express Our Gratitude, We Must Never Forget That the Highest Appreciation is Not to Utter Words, but to Live by Them.”

-John F. Kennedy
