# 10 Basic Types of Women \- Which One Are You? ...

Now's the time to find out which type of women you are (or what combination) and love yourself for it! Think about which of these traits and qualities you can totally relate to and remember that it's what makes you who you are today! Comment which type of women you are ⬇️ ⬇️ 🌸 ☯️ ❤️

## 1. Affectionate 💋

![person, black and white, kiss, human action, love,](https://img.allw.mn/content/aj/s5/f1qxxmjn5785fe2065e6a522731025_500x210.gif)

Affectionate women are always giving their man cuddles and kisses as soon as he walks in the door! They love to show affection by hugging new people along with a pleasant and polite kiss on the cheek!

## 2. Passionate 🌹

![hair, hairstyle, bangs, costume, Oh!,](https://img.allw.mn/content/sr/fd/iisgabij5785ff9d2eb51713704888_245x245.gif)

Passionate women do what they love and love what they do! They are incredibly passionate about their work as well as hobbies and interests they may have. They put everything they have into each task they complete as they're so driven!

## 3. Caring 🌸

![hair, person, hairstyle, portrait, emotion,](https://img.allw.mn/content/rs/af/uy7xac835785ffbfe7176282047913_500x286.gif)

Caring women are always there for their loved ones, ready to listen to any problems and provide a great shoulder to cry on. They want nothing but the best for the people they care about so deeply and it makes them so content and happy when this happens!

## 4. Daring ✨

![person, human action, thigh, athletics, chest,](https://img.allw.mn/content/kx/fx/s05nw7d55786018083b2a670512605_500x210.gif)

Daring women are always up for a challenge and to be dared by other people! They're not afraid to hold back, be bold and adventurous while being totally confident when taking on new challenges!

## 5. Spontaneous ⚡️

![person, document, for, instanc, started,](https://img.allw.mn/content/s8/b2/a48m3s1h578602580e18c941728037_500x280.gif)

Spontaneous women hate to plan and organise, instead preferring to live life completely in the now and present! They love the thrill of never knowing where life is going to take them or what they're going to do next!

## 6. Challenging 🌷

![person, human action, Bring, it.,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ys/f5/nv17oike5786029e17cbd665133174_500x278.gif)

Challenging women are not afraid to set a challenge for themselves or anyone else, and love to see whether it can be achieved in the end! They like to 'test' people and themselves every now and again to keep life interesting!

## 7. Organised 🌼

![justhappen, have, label, maker,](https://img.allw.mn/content/x5/b7/ynk8p0tn578603299f534993762452_500x300.gif)

Organised women are great and excellent planners who like things to happen exactly as it was arranged. Organising is a great way to keep track of when everything is happening so that they can stay on top of life!

## 8. Relaxed ☮

![hair, hairstyle, human action, arm, muscle,](https://img.allw.mn/content/e3/bn/e4jw5quc5786043ad5c09985315783_500x232.gif)

Relaxed women are pretty casual, laid back and easy-going. They don't live life with too many rules or regulations and don't tend to think too much about anything. They like to take life as it comes and to go with the flow!

## 9. Loving ❤️

![person, facial expression, smile, Love, ya.,](https://img.allw.mn/content/vs/cd/pvd4ns4z578604922ceb3860542013_500x281.gif)

Loving women find it super easy and comfortable to express their love for all of their close ones! They don't hold back and are quick to reassure others of how much they are loved and appreciated.

## 10. Hard Working ✴️

![person, blond, singer, supermodel, singing,](https://img.allw.mn/content/qq/ld/c8ep6w7a578606aa7b9d5534868291_500x208.gif)

Hard working women are incredibly driven to succeed and also super passionate about what they do! They are very self-disciplined and know exactly how to be professional when the time comes for it.
