# 5 Things You Should Never Regret in Life ...

It's important to know that there are some **things you should never regret**. Life may reward you with a lot of good moments but sometimes you let your bad ones cover them up. They are things that have made you who you are and have shaped your life into what it is now. So when you feel bad about something in your past that maybe didn't end the way you wanted it to, consider that it's just part of building your future. Here are some **things you should never regret**.

## 1. Being in Love

![human hair color, nose, eyebrow, girl, lip,](https://img.allw.mn/content/vk/hs/twehwivd59ad94e0e24d2639374421_500x220.gif)

Sometimes you fall perfectly in love, but it doesn't always last. You should be thankful for the pain which is long gone, the pain that you didn't know would make you so strong. Love may not have lasted, but it's definitely one of the **things you should never regret**.

## 2. Leaving an Opportunity behind

![hair, human hair color, blond, hairstyle, girl,](https://img.allw.mn/content/j9/lg/y7ox0urb59ad91f08e8b9821248623_498x239.gif)

Opportunities may come and go, but it's your willingness to leave one behind that matters. Missing out an opportunity could lead to something better ahead. When you see your dreams waiting in front of you, it's hard to walk away, but sometimes it's the best thing to do in the moment.

## 3. Being Inconsistent

![girl, muscle, photo caption,](https://img.allw.mn/content/u5/zd/g9zfjtxc59ad920e39ab3333595366_457x200.gif)

Being able to stay the same is nearly impossible. Struggle, experience, choice, and time changes people. Not staying the same throughout doesn't mean you are walking on the wrong path. It implies that your priorities change as your personality demands what's best for you.

## 4. Dating the Wrong Guy

![eyewear, vision care, sunglasses, black hair, girl,](https://img.allw.mn/content/sr/i5/opesd9he59ad91fb44f49021643364_500x280.gif)

It's funny how you see people's true colors and get mad at yourself for trusting them too much. But maybe this time, give yourself a pat on the back for giving too much without even thinking if you'd get the same in return. That makes you a good person, even if the guy is long gone.

## 5. Failing to Give Your Best

![performance, entertainment, singing, singer, music artist,](https://img.allw.mn/content/y1/qr/ozirxfg659ad952625c1a057642232_499x246.gif)

Sometimes, good enough has to be good enough. If you tried and it didn't work out, it's totally fine to move on to something else that deserves the best of you.

It's never easy to bring out the best in yourself. Sometimes, while strolling leisurely on the path, you come across people and things that inspire you to do things that make a big difference. And there's no regret in that!
