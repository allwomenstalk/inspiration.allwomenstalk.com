# What You Are Most Sensitive about According to Your Zodiac Sign ...

Ever wondered **what you are most sensitive about according to your zodiac sign**? On the whole, we all like to be considered as strong, independent women who can’t be rattled by many things in life, but deep down inside we all have at least one thing that we are especially sensitive about! It might be a big thing, it might be small thing, whatever it is, it has the power to change your mood in an instant. If you like to really put across a powerful and strong front, then you might not even be aware of what your own sensitivity even is! Let’s try to identify it. Here is **what you are most sensitive about according to your zodiac sign**.

## 1. Gemini

You are a very prideful person, so it makes sense that you are most sensitive about the possibility of being embarrassed or humiliated by people.

## 2. Cancer

Your most sensitive tendency is a tendency to dwell on the past, specifically the bad things that have happened in the past. It can prevent you from trying new things and living up to your potential.

## 3. Leo

You are an expert social climber, and one of your biggest sensitivities is the fear of being regarded by those around as someone who doesn’t fit in.

## 4. Virgo

You are someone who tends to bottle up all of your feelings, and this stems from a sensitivity to ever being labelled as emotional or over the top. You don’t want to be singled out like that so you keep everything inside.

## 5. Libra

You are one of the only signs who owns up to your sensitivity. You are a sensitive person and you would rather people know that, so as a result, your main sensitivity stems from being disrespected for being your true self.

## 6. Scorpio

Social injustice is your main sensitivity. You can’t feel great when you see somebody being treated badly, you feel like it is your job to step in.

## 7. Sagittarius

You are most sensitive to being disappointed by the people who you are closest to. To you, family and friends are everything, so when they do something that hurts you, you feel it harder than others.

## 8. Capricorn

Some people see you are touchy, some see you as cold, but it all stems from the same place, a sensitivity to having your feelings hurt by people. You tend to keep them at arms length so that they can’t disappoint you.

## 9. Aquarius

You are loyal and kind and generous, but a side effect of this is that you are very sensitive to the prospect of people taking advantage of you.

## 10. Pisces

You are naturally very caring and giving, so there is always that sense that your generosity might not be returned by those you give it to in a time of need.

## 11. Aries

You can turn into a moaning cry baby pretty quickly, and that is because you are so stubborn. You are sensitive to not getting your own way!

## 12. Taurus

You are sensitive to being seen as stupid or unintelligent, and that can sometimes come across as looking aloof or uninterested.
