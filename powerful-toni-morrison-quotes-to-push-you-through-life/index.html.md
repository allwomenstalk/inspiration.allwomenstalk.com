# 7 Powerful Toni Morrison Quotes to Push You through Life ...

Toni Morrison is a famous novelist, editor, and professor. She's an incredibly intelligent woman who has plenty of things to say. According to [Essence,](https://goo.gl/06KZKw) here are a few of her most powerful quotes:

## 1. Love

![Love](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uz/fr/w021rlru56c71f2d36663078603396_600x456.jpg)

When you finally feel love, it'll change your entire life.

## 2. Passion and Skill

![Passion and Skill](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gf/vf/s28zrqoh56c71f2d674b8669410576_600x456.jpg)

You need both if you want to succeed.

## 3. Falling in Love

![Falling in Love](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vg/yg/gt9xz27856c71f2d99b81374868311_600x456.jpg)

Love should make you a better person, after all.

## 4. Freedom

![Freedom](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yt/be/n07gx8ll56c71f2e0bb9e849868977_600x456.jpg)

Don't you agree?

## 5. Stories

![Stories](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ns/yj/idvffzf956c71f2e211a8642955337_600x456.jpg)

You're in charge of your own story, so make sure it's a good one.

## 6. Definitions

![Definitions](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lt/b9/t2cctfeh56c71f2e6382f883112964_600x456.jpg)

Don't let anyone define you, because you're whoever you want to be.

## 7. Beauty
