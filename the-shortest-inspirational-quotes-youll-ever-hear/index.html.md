# The 7 Shortest Inspirational Quotes Youll Ever Hear ...

You don't have to sit through an hourlong speech to be inspired. There are quotes that are short and sweet that'll motivate you. Here are a few brought to you by [realsimple.com](https://www.realsimple.com/work-life/life-strategies/inspiration-motivation/positive-quotes) that are only 6 words long:

## 1. Be Yourself

![Be Yourself](https://img.allw.mn/content/de/ov/mdkeda9n566aebcdb9719563550342_435x518.gif)

You're the only you that exists. That means you can do what others can't.

## 2. Temporary Circumstances

![Temporary Circumstances](https://img.allw.mn/content/jk/kb/scdq5ji9566aebcdd6537234672463_435x518.gif)

Life changes. It isn't always going to make you feel like it does right now.

## 3. Don't Hesitate

![Don't Hesitate](https://img.allw.mn/content/rk/sv/t1he6i1n566aebce14b96187092125_435x518.gif)

Never hesitate to follow your dreams, no matter how big they are.

## 4. Challenges

![Challenges](https://img.allw.mn/content/pi/yb/obyri4qw566aebceac70f887242074_435x518.gif)

Don't be a big fish in a small pond. Go out there and challenge yourself.

## 5. Fountain

![Fountain](https://img.allw.mn/content/f8/ym/omc70nwj566aebce6a140137685886_435x518.gif)

Doesn't that say it all?

## 6. Laugh, Don't Yell

![Laugh, Don't Yell](https://img.allw.mn/content/p7/zr/dy7iux08566aebceb0360395189158_435x518.gif)

Make it your mission to be happy more often than you're angry.

## 7. Say Yes

![Say Yes](https://img.allw.mn/content/n0/eb/riqktpgs566aebceecec3571359140_435x518.gif)

Don't turn down amazing opportunities. Take advantage of them.

What's your favorite quote of all time?
