# 7 Best Quotes From Narnia Creator C.S. Lewis That Will Change Your Life

It might be well over fifty years since the Chronicles of Narnia began making their way into bookshops and swiftly into the hands of children globally, but the writer's influence remains stronger than ever. Today, I want to share some of the best quotes from Narnia and its creator that are guaranteed to make you pause for thought. These C.S. Lewis famous quotes still carry immense weight.

C.S. Lewis was a respected novelist, poet, essayist, medieval expert, and academic. He was also great friends with J.R.R. Tolkien, another iconic twentieth-century writer. His works have been translated into more than thirty languages, proving his lasting global appeal. As you read through these selections, it becomes obvious why the chronicles of narnia quotes continue to resonate with so many readers today.

## 1. Happiness is within

![Happiness is within](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jv/ln/z6d53yzz_600x450.jpg)

One of the best C.S. Lewis quotes on Narnia and life focuses squarely on happiness. Our joy shouldn't depend on the acquisition of material things. I am just as guilty as the next person of craving a beautiful handbag, assuming it will make me incredibly happy. However, this mindset means that if we lose these possessions or they lose their luster, we will inevitably feel disillusioned. This quote reminds us that true happiness comes from within, ensuring we stay at peace without relying on external influences to determine our inner serenity. Check out the [official C.S. Lewis site](https://www.cslewis.com/) for more on his philosophy.

## 2. Age Ain't Nothing but a Number

![Age Ain't Nothing but a Number](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xz/pq/ms3pwqzl_600x450.jpg)

How many of you have thought that you are simply too old for a new venture? The truth is that [age is nothing but a number](#7), and it is never too late to work towards a goal or dream. As long as you maintain the capacity to dream, you possess the ability to achieve that dream. This perspective echoes throughout many of his writings and reminds us to keep pushing forward.

## 3. Doing Good for the Greater Good

![Doing Good for the Greater Good](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h9/hj/zulmdovk_600x450.jpg)

Being humble is key. Just because you might be doing the washing up in the office or picking up litter in the street does not mean you have stooped to new lows in your career. This famous C.S. Lewis humility quote highlights that humility is about constantly thinking of others. It is about recognizing that there are bigger forces at work in the universe.

- Volunteer in your local community
- Offer a helping hand to a coworker
- Practice daily gratitude

Small acts of kindness build a foundation for genuine humility.

## 4. We're All the Same

![We're All the Same](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z5/jc/kh9xy6kc_600x600.jpg)

I love those moments where we realize that we are so much more alike than we initially imagined. Sometimes the ego likes to convince us that we are entirely unique and that no one else thinks the way we do. However, when we drop our guards, we create strong bonds with others and appreciate how deep down we are incredibly similar. It is one of those [funny Narnia quotes](https://narnia.com/) situations where a simple realization shifts our entire perspective on humanity.

## 5. Self Belief

![Self Belief](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jm/ps/j92c1drd_600x600.jpg)

If we believe we are nothing, we project that negative belief outwards, and others will begin to believe it as well. On the other hand, we are what we believe. If we know we are capable of greatness and success, we radiate that confidence, and people naturally respond to it. Trust in the greatness within yourself, and it will eventually become your reality.

## 6. The Future

![The Future](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qj/e9/k72br7if_600x600.jpg)

Do not obsess over the past. It is gone and you cannot change it no matter how hard you try. Instead, choose to move forward and look ahead because there are always greater things waiting for you. Embracing this mindset allows you to step confidently into new opportunities and leave old regrets behind.

## 7. Life's Journey

![Life's Journey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rx/qw/riwy71rj_600x600.jpg)

This thought-provoking concept really resonates with me. Sometimes the changes we are undergoing do not become immediately obvious, but as time passes, we can look back and acknowledge how completely different things are. Just like the [happiness found within](#1), we must embrace change and accept it. Much like a flower that buds, blooms, withers, and dies, we are all active agents of change in our own lives.

These are just a few of the great quotes by the wonderful writer. Whether you are reflecting on the chronicles of Narnia quote that stuck with you from childhood or discovering a new piece of wisdom today, his words offer timeless guidance. Which of these quotes is your favorite?
