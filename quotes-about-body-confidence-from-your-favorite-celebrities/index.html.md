# 7 Quotes about Body Confidence from Your Favorite Celebrities ...

When you're feeling bad about yourself, there are quotes about body confidence that will make you feel like the gorgeous girl you are. You're a beautiful human being, even if you don't believe it when you look in the mirror or see snapshots of yourself. Everyone is insecure about certain aspects of themselves, whether they're celebrities starring in films or students heading to class. Here are some of the most uplifting quotes about body confidence to make you realize just how amazing you are:

## 1. Taylor Swift

![Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nu/l2/at6e8ib0_600x405.jpg)

Taylor Swift's fans may believe that she's perfect, but she thinks she has flaws just like everybody else. This is one of the quotes about body confidence that proves that everyone feels self-conscious about their traits. It's impossible to be 100% happy with your looks. There will always be something that you're insecure about, but that doesn't make you any less beautiful. Remember that you're gorgeous just the way you are.

## 2. Adele

![Adele](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vp/fb/vlnbc4sw_491x388.jpg)

Even though Adele has a beautiful voice and is beyond talented, people still find ways to criticize her. The most amazing human beings get judged by others, so don't feel like you're the only one. Adele proves that you don't have to get upset over other's opinions. Just be yourself, and stop caring about what everyone else thinks.

## 3. Jennifer Lawrence

![Jennifer Lawrence](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gu/ru/dw9ejncg_600x600.jpg)

Jennifer Lawrence refuses to starve herself for a role. She knows she's gorgeous, and that she doesn't need to alter her looks at all. Women are tricked into believing that they should look a certain way, but the pictures splattered across their magazines are photoshopped. No one looks like celebrities do in real life. It's time to realize that.

## 4. Sofia Vergara

![Sofia Vergara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i4/d1/t7x4o7z9_600x400.jpg)

Sofia, one of the most gorgeous women on television, admits to looking less than fabulous on some days. It's impossible to show your outer beauty every. Sometimes, all you want to do is lounge around in sweatpants without bothering to comb your hair. There's nothing wrong with being comfortable.

## 5. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bk/f3/l74ti35f_500x500.jpg)

When you think of the grand scheme of things, your body issues seem like such minor problems. There's an entire world for you to explore with so many opportunities available. While your health is important, you should enjoy your life, and stop criticizing yourself every single time you pick up a cupcake.

## 6. Tyra Banks

![Tyra Banks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y7/xh/z01a3e0w_567x400.jpg)

The expectations placed on women today by society are higher than ever. Everyone expects us to look a certain way, but there's no reason to listen to their ignorance. Skinny women, heavy women, tall women, and short women are all equal when it comes to beauty. Don't let anyone tell you otherwise. If they do, then they're not worth a second of your precious time.

## 7. Louis CK

![Louis CK](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a7/fa/zmeuniaz_600x334.jpg)

What difference does it make if you have a large waist size? Louis CK is not ashamed to show the entire world what he looks like. He's proud of whom he is, and doesn't care if anyone insults him, because he's comfortable in his skin. We could all learn something from his openness.

You're in charge of how confident you feel, so it's up to you to realize just how beautiful you are. These quotes should help you remember your worth, so you shouldn't have a problem walking tall. What is your favorite thing about your beautiful body?
