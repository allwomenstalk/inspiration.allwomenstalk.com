# Your Zodiac Signs Worst Quality ...

In an ideal world, we would all be perfect human beings, but the very nature of humanity means that this is never going to be the case! Whether we like it or not, we are all flawed individuals in one way or another, and even though we find people who love us just as we are, there is no doubting that sometimes we let out worst selves come out to play! The important thing here is to know what your bad qualities are and work to keep them under as much control as possible. Here is **your worst quality according to your zodiac sign**!

## 1. Aries

You like to have things your own way, which can sometimes turn in to pushiness, dominance and unattractive bossiness.

## 2. Taurus

You are hard working when doing something you are interested in, but when it feels more like a chore, you have a tendency to become a bit lazy.

## 3. Gemini

You have a tendency to be a bit of scatterbrain, which can make making plans with you quite difficult at the best of times.

## 4. Cancer

You can be oversensitive sometimes, which means that your friends and family never quite know if you are going to take offense to something that they say.

## 5. Leo

Like a true Leo, you like to be the centre of attention, which can sometimes translate as you being rather selfish in big groups.

## 6. Virgo

You are a notorious miser. You don’t want to spend any money unless it can be proven that it is going to be 100% worth it which can often jeopardise group plans!

## 7. Libra

You are terrible at making decisions. If you are the person left to decide for a group, you might never end up doing anything at all!

## 8. Scorpio

You like to be in control of all situations, which can sometimes come across as being manipulative.

## 9. Sagittarius

You are a very intelligent person, but in new company, that can sometimes come across as seeming like a bit of a know it all smart Alec.

## 10. Capricorn

You are a total workaholic, which in itself isn’t a bad thing, but it manifests in you as being a constant worrier.

## 11. Aquarius

You can sometimes be very cold and distant without even realising it. This makes other people think that you don’t like them when you actually do.

## 12. Pisces

You are a daydreamer extraordinaire, which can often lead to people thinking you are rude because it takes about three or four attempts for them to get you to listen to them!
