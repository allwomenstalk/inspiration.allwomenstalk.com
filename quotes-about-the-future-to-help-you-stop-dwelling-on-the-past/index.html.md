# 7 Quotes about the Future to Help You Stop Dwelling on the past ...

Sometimes we can get our feet firmly stuck in the mud of the past, but there are some fabulous quotes about the future to help you stop dwelling on the past and look to the future. Looking at your past can of course be helpful when you want to prevent mistakes happening again, but being obsessed with your past is unhealthy. Here are some quotes about the future which will help to remind you how important it is to think about tomorrow rather than worrying about yesterday.

## 1. Lessons Learnt

![Lessons Learnt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h8/kd/njwk82sb_600x600.jpg)

This is one of those great quotes about the future which really helps you to literally say goodbye to the past and hello to the future. It's simple but effective. Imagine you're writing a letter to the past and the future and this is probably what you would say.

## 2. No Regrets

![No Regrets](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iq/k0/r8sf2gep_600x450.jpg)

As well as a plethora of fabulous literary works, Mark Twain has left behind some wonderful words of wisdom, and this quote about embracing the future is no exception. It's true when he says this and it really makes me want to don my hiking boots or book a round the world ticket. Of course, he's not just talking about literal exploration, he's also talking about embracing the future and all it has to offer. Indeed, go forth and conquer your future like an intrepid explorer!

## 3. Masters of Our Destiny

![Masters of Our Destiny](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/so/sr/p3wjp3ee_600x375.jpg)

Only we have the power to create our future. Whilst it is sometimes dependent on many external factors which we often can't control, with some self-determination we can create the future we desire.

## 4. Reflection

![Reflection](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/km/yj/y1r5rncu_600x399.jpg)

Reflecting on our past is important, and although it may be tempting to try and forget much of what has been and gone, we can in fact use the past experiences to help us move forward. There is a thin line between reflection and obsession though.

## 5. Letting Go

![Letting Go](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n9/sc/ws99lmyr_600x389.jpg)

Letting go of the past is essential. as it is unhealthy to harp on about what has been and gone. We can't change the past, no matter how much we wish we could fly around the world like Superman in an attempt to turn back the clock. It's time to seize the day, let go of the past, look to the future and enjoy the present!

## 6. Ignorance is Bliss

![Ignorance is Bliss](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s9/ya/q59vzooo_600x454.jpg)

Whilst many of us wish we could see into the future, I often wonder whether I would really want to know what my future held. Sometimes, we can become just as obsessed with the future as we are with our past, which can be equally as unhealthy. This is why it's so important to live in the present, enjoying and embracing every moment.

## 7. Time Flies

![Time Flies](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qp/k8/rnyyloa7_403x259.jpg)

Remember when you were younger and you wished you were older so that you could go out on your own, drive, drink (not at the same time!) or have a boyfriend? Remember how that time came around sooner than you would have liked, and before you knew it, you were driving your own car? I know it's a cliché but sometimes time literally flies, so don't worry or obsess about the future. Instead, make memories today.

These are just a few of my favorite quotes about the future. Which are yours and do you have any others you would like to share?
