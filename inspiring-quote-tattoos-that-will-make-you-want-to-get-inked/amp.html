Frequently Asked Questions

Why would someone get a quote tattoo?
People get quote tattoos for so many reasons! Sometimes a quote really resonates with them and represents a meaningful moment or belief in their life. It's like carrying a piece of inspiration with you wherever you go!
Are quote tattoos only for people looking for inspiration?
Not at all! While many people get quotes for inspiration or motivation, others might choose quotes that remind them of love, strength, or even something funny. It’s all about what speaks to you personally.
What are some popular themes for quote tattoos?
Strength and resilience are definitely popular themes. People also love quotes about love, struggle, and personal growth. Some go for meaningful sayings that they hold close to their heart.
Do quote tattoos hurt more than other tattoos?
Alright, so pain varies from person to person. However, the pain level usually depends more on the location of the tattoo rather than the design. Getting a quote tattoo will probably feel the same as any other small to medium tattoo in the same spot.
Where's a good place on the body to get a quote tattoo?
That’s totally up to you! Popular spots include the forearm, wrist, ribs, collarbone, and back. You might want to think about how much you want others to see it. Some spots can be easily hidden if needed, like the ribs or back.
