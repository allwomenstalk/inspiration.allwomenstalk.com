# 7 Empowering Quotes from Julia Roberts ...

Julia Roberts has been around for a long time. She's played dozens of roles that we all know and love, so it's hard to pick a favorite. However, [Good Housekeeping](https://www.goodhousekeeping.com/life/entertainment/g2891/julia-roberts-best-movie-quotes/) has compiled a list of her best movie quotes of all time:

## 1. Steel Magnolias

![Steel Magnolias](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ul/gi/ialxyuga56312c7f65590997310279_600x400.jpg)

"I would rather have thirty minutes of wonderful than a lifetime of nothing special."

## 2. Pretty Woman

![Pretty Woman](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eh/xz/u4e4hga056312c72472e5651270931_600x337.jpg)

"I was in here yesterday, you wouldn't wait on me. You people work on commission, right? Big mistake. Big. Huge. I have to go shopping now."

## 3. Hook

![Hook](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/le/w7/zup7z2pw56312c7298754380530373_600x399.jpg)

"You know that place between sleep and awake? That place where you still remember dreaming? That's where I'll always love you."

## 4. My Best Friend's Wedding

![My Best Friend's Wedding](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eb/ab/s39t8g3956312c72b706d415581937_600x300.jpg)

"I realize this comes at a very inopportune time, but I really have this gigantic favor to ask of you. Choose me. Marry me. Let me make you happy. Oh, that sounds like three favors, doesn't it?"

## 5. Notting Hill

![Notting Hill](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y4/f5/lgp963h956312c730eb8c496322191_600x400.jpg)

"I'm just a girl, standing in front of a boy, asking him to love her."

## 6. Erin Brockovich

![Erin Brockovich](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i2/tr/f1rozr8u56312c7385d3f466174304_600x408.jpg)

"For the first time in my life, I got people respecting me. Please, don't ask me to give it up."

## 7. Charlotte's Web

![Charlotte's Web](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/al/ev/q837n8kh56312c7385271558014034_600x478.jpg)

"My webs were no miracle, Wilbur. I was only describing what I saw. The miracle is you."
