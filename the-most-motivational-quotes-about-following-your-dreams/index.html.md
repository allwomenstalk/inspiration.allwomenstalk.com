# The Most Motivational Quotes about following Your Dreams ...

Ever since you were a child, you've been told to follow your dreams. However, the older you get, the harder that is to do, because there are plenty of obstacles bound to get in your way. No matter how many times you fail, you need to try again and again until you're successful. If you need some inspiration to keep you going, here are a few motivational quotes about following your dreams:

## 1. Be the Inspiration, Don't Work for the Inspiration

![text,font,advertising,brand,emotion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p6/rp/u7pfz_text_font_advertising_brand_emotion_500x500.jpg)

Do you want to be the girl that all of your friends are jealous of? Or do you want to spend your whole life jealous of your boss for actually getting to the top of her career? You don't have to settle for a mediocre life. You can follow your dreams in order to be the type of person every woman wishes they were.

## 2. You Owe It to Yourself to Pursue Your Dreams

![text,font,brand,emotion,presentation,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ly/x9/k32bl_text_font_brand_emotion_presentation_480x480.jpg)

You'll feel the most alive when you get a job doing what you love to do. You don't want to end up at a place you dread going to every single morning, do you? That's why you should do yourself a favor by going after your dreams. It's why you were born, after all.

## 3. Adjust Your Take on Reality

![Red de Jovenes,text,font,product,line,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uj/bt/xsmm9_red-de-jovenes_text_font_product_line_500x416.jpg)

It's important to be realistic when it comes to creating goals for yourself. However, don't assume that anything that requires hard work is outside of the realm of possibility. There are plenty of things you could accomplish if you tried hard enough, so don't assume that something is impossible when you're perfectly capable of achieving it.

## 4. We All Have Talent

![text,font,poster,advertising,album cover,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bg/ru/d6680_text_font_poster_advertising_album-cover_480x480.jpg)

Don't fool yourself into believing that you don't have any special skills. You're talented at something, whether it's with numbers or with music. You just have to be courageous enough to pursue whatever it is that you're good at. No matter how difficult it is to get a job in that field, you need to try your best to get one anyway.

## 5. Turn Walls into Doors

![Brewster Home Fashions,Amity University,font,text,line,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zu/ee/vstqt_brewster-home-fashions_amity-university_font_text_line_390x389.jpg)

If you follow your dreams, you'll eventually end up getting presented with opportunities that you wouldn't have gotten if you sat at home being unproductive. Even though you might only see walls right now, eventually you'll end up with open doors. The harder you work at achieving your dreams, the more opportunities you'll be presented with.

## 6. Lead Your Dreams

![font,product,brand,advertising,DONT,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ym/yp/xiaua_font_product_brand_advertising_dont_600x496.jpg)

Your friends and parents don't determine whether or not you succeed. You're completely in charge of achieving your dreams. You don't have to follow them; you have to lead them. You know the direction that you should be going in, so don't hesitate to take that path.

## 7. Choose Your Career First

![Make Up Forever,beauty,font,bride,album cover,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w9/xv/lk160_make-up-forever_beauty_font_bride_album-cover_500x327.jpg)

Lady Gaga isn't saying that you should stay single forever. She's just saying that your first priority should be to follow your career dreams. If a man tries to hold you back from doing what you love to do, he's not the one for you. You can have a career and a husband, so long as your husband supports your career.

No matter what your dreams are, you owe it to yourself to pursue them. If you don't, then you're going to end up regretting it in the future. What's your favorite quote about following your dreams?
