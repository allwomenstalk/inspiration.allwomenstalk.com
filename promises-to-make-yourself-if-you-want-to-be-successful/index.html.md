# 10 Promises  to Make to You if You Want to Be More Successful ...

If you want to be successful, then you're going to have to work hard. Nothing good comes easy. Unfortunately, unless you're willing to do whatever it takes to get where you want in life, you probably won't reach your goals. In order to make sure that you do, here are a few promises that you should make to yourself if you want to be successful:

## 1. I Promise I Won’t Give up

![I Promise I Won’t Give up](https://img.allw.mn/content/gl/ai/hijia7kh56f53c1351fed087651918_500x281.gif)

Failure isn't permanent, unless you let it be. That means that you can't give up after failing once. As long as you try again and again, eventually that failure will turn into success. You just have to be patient and learn from your mistakes. The second time you try to complete a goal, you'll have a higher chance of succeeding than the first time, and so on and so forth.

## 2. I Promise I'll Push Myself

![I Promise I'll Push Myself](https://img.allw.mn/content/bf/sm/pdkfvi0i56f53c2a6f631951644096_500x281.gif)

Never stop moving forward. No matter how talented or intelligent you are, there's always more to learn and accomplish. That means that you shouldn't let yourself get too comfortable with your current position. Push yourself to get a promotion, so that you can make your life even more amazing than it is now. There are always better things up ahead, but only if you aim for them.

## 3. I Promise I Won’t Be Too Hard on Myself

![I Promise I Won’t Be Too Hard on Myself](https://img.allw.mn/content/x9/l3/n424fzip56f53c3e79032411643767_497x304.gif)

You need to push yourself in order to succeed, but that doesn't mean you should torture yourself. Give yourself a break. If you're working hard, then don't get angry when you fail. If you're too tough on yourself, then you're going to hurt yourself instead of help yourself.

## 4. I Promise I’ll Value Myself

![I Promise I’ll Value Myself](https://img.allw.mn/content/w1/nu/s1p6d64x56f53c5ed3e19632247338_500x282.gif)

No matter what you accomplish in life, you need to remember how valuable you are. It doesn't matter how much money you have or how many goals you've actually crossed off of our bucket list. You're still a valuable person.

## 5. I Promise to Learn from My Criticism

![I Promise to Learn from My Criticism](https://img.allw.mn/content/x0/mm/nwyd54dq56f53c862cc2d156923216_500x281.gif)

If someone criticizes your performance at work, don't flip out. Listen to what they have to say, and try to take their criticism into consideration. They might've said something worth listening to. As hurtful as their words might be at first, they could end up shaping you into the person you need to be to succeed.

## 6. I Promise to Stop Doubting Myself

![I Promise to Stop Doubting Myself](https://img.allw.mn/content/ks/hx/c61v18gg56f53c97c9c21566085474_500x281.gif)

You could spend hours listing out all of the things that could go wrong when trying to accomplish your goals, but what good would that do you? Positive thinking can boost your chances of doing what you set out to do, so stay optimistic. There's no reason to doubt yourself when you already know what you're capable of. You just have to wait for the world to see what you're capable of, too.

## 7. I Promise to Grow

![I Promise to Grow](https://img.allw.mn/content/zt/xb/x32ytkt056f53ca730f93265195157_500x353.gif)

You're meant to grow each and every day. If your small town isn't giving you what you need, you have to move away. If your friendships are holding you back, you have to cut some people loose. If you don't, it'll prevent you from changing, and changing is a big part of life. If you want to succeed, you have to give yourself room to grow.

## 8. I PROMISE to Accept My Strengths and My Flaws

![person, speech, musician, profession, Promise,](https://img.allw.mn/content/uc/an/i8aqet5v58c0af86b6ae8976369978_500x281.gif)

Being successful means being aware of what you are and aren't good at. In order to improve your weaknesses you must first recognize them. Same goes for strengths; if you want to master them you need to know them. Lose the low self-esteem and start building yourself up!

## 9. I Will Promise to Keep in Mind There's Things beyond My Control

![man, male, romance, One, Cross,](https://img.allw.mn/content/q7/tz/h7szoc9c58c0af0322abb174994742_500x274.gif)

You will never live a full and happy life if you think you can control everything and everyone. When you begin to realize you only have control of yourself, you will be able to strive to be the greatest YOU can be and hope others recognize that as well.

## 10. I Promise to Forget the Pains of the past but Always Remember Their LessOns

![screenshot, promise,](https://img.allw.mn/content/a6/ue/c4fcm67x58c0af976ae2c249741590_500x199.gif)

With great success comes great failure at some point. Instead of dwelling on those failures, pick yourself up, correct what went wrong, and try again. Rome wasn't built in a day and neither were you dear! You need to eliminate the past frustrations and focus on future achievements in order to be the successful woman you aim to be.

Make these promises to yourself, so that you can create the best life possible. Do you think you can follow through on all of these promises?
