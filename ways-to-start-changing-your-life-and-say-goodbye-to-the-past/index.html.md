# 7 Ways to Start Changing Your Life and Say Goodbye to the past ...

What are the ways to start changing your life? It's common to long for a different way of living, but a lot harder to actually make the necessary changes. We get stuck in a life that we're not happy with, or dwell in the past. That's not healthy or fulfilling. Try these ways to start changing your life, and leave the past behind you …

## 1. Break Old Patterns

One of the most vital ways to start changing your life is to break old patterns of behavior. Do your relationships always end because you're too needy? Or are you too nervous to go for goals that you'd like to achieve? Nothing will change if you don't alter your way of doing or seeing things, so work out what your patterns are and work on changing them.

## 2. Mourn and Move on

Losses of any kind can leave us stuck in the past. We feel that we will never meet anyone as wonderful as our ex, or feel angry about something that we feel was taken from us. Suck it up, and move on. Things happen. There's no sense in focusing on something that has already happened. It won't change. Learn from it, and then focus on the future.

## 3. No Regrets

How often do we beat ourselves up for what we see as our failures? Everyone makes mistakes. We wouldn't be human if we didn't, and anyway, mistakes are often a good way of learning. Regrets are pretty pointless, especially as we can't go back and do the same thing differently.

## 4. Be Courageous

Moving on from the past can require a great deal of courage, especially if we need to make drastic changes. Sometimes you need to make substantial changes in order to break away. But it can often be very worthwhile, and in the long run you'll be glad that you were brave enough to take these steps to a new life. You'll be surprised just how much you are actually capable of.

## 5. Take Action

Your life will not change for the better if you don't take action. Nobody is going to rescue you. Ask yourself what you're not happy with, and what you would like to achieve. Then make a list of the steps you need to take in order to attain those goals. It might take time, but work on getting to where you want to be, one step at a time. Nothing will happen if you don't act, so get going!

## 6. Make Changes

You may also need to make changes to your lifestyle and friendship group in order to move on. Perhaps your friends are the kind of people who don't have any ambitions and so criticise yours. So you end up in the same old place, doing the same things. Spend less time with them and seek out positive people who understand the wish to have a better life.

## 7. Look Forwards

Finally, change your focus from the past, and start concentrating on your future instead. Spend too much time thinking about the past, and you'll carry on being stuck there. That's no way to live; we'll never go back to the past unless they invent time travel. Think about your future instead, and work on getting to where you want to be.

It is definitely possible to change your life, but it does require determination, focus and courage. Above all, you need to stop living in the past. Sadly many of us are stuck in old ways of thinking. Leave the past where it belongs, and start building a happy new life for yourself. What's the biggest change you've ever made to your life?
