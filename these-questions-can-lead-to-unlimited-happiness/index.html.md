# 17 Questions  about Life That Can Bring You Ultimate Happiness ...

If you want to be happy, then you need to figure out what would make you happy. Once you find out that information, then you can start to make the right changes in your life. If you're not sure what you really want yet, here are a few questions that can lead to unlimited happiness:

## 1. Why do You Wake up in the Morning?

![MTV, facial expression, face, hair, black and white,](https://img.allw.mn/content/vn/zk/xk36gohq5895856cc6f19835391835_500x281.gif)

There must be something that pulls you out of bed in the morning. Maybe it's your boyfriend, or your pet, or your job.

## 2. What Type of People Make You Smile?

![What Type of People Make You Smile?](https://img.allw.mn/content/vi/uu/wdxs561x56bcaa1763080511181906_500x281.gif)

You should spend more time around the positive type of people that make you happy. Their attitude will rub off on you.

## 3. What Shows Make You Laugh?

![What Shows Make You Laugh?](https://img.allw.mn/content/ba/rb/lst73dqd56bcaa1938bb2487479612_500x280.gif)

If a television show is the only thing that can make you smile, that's okay. Find more shows like it and watch them in your free time. Even the small things can lead to a better life.

## 4. What’s Your Main Goal in Life?

![What’s Your Main Goal in Life?](https://img.allw.mn/content/t6/cz/lsdrf9hz56bcaa1919ce7126398700_500x300.gif)

Think about what you want to accomplish the most, and then work toward that goal every single day.

## 5. What Are You Most Thankful for?

![What Are You Most Thankful for?](https://img.allw.mn/content/bm/bw/kpk4pn5d56bcaa1bde8d7038854554_500x281.gif)

It's easy to think of the things we hate about life. Instead, try to think of the things you're thankful for. It'll help you realize that your life isn't as bad as you think it is.

## 6. Do You like Kids?

![Do You like Kids?](https://img.allw.mn/content/jn/yt/dpkvbe0b56bcaa191ac1c864629405_500x259.gif)

If you do, then you should think about having children one day. If that's not in your plan, you could always work with children instead.

## 7. What Are You Most Jealous of?

![What Are You Most Jealous of?](https://img.allw.mn/content/p8/qo/exsmbqc556bcaa1906dd1784311444_500x220.gif)

If you're jealous of your neighbor's huge house, then you should aim to make money to buy a big house of your own. If you're jealous of something, then you probably want that thing.

## 8. How Much Money Would Make You Happy?

![How Much Money Would Make You Happy?](https://img.allw.mn/content/rp/p0/lyxp9nn156bcaa1960adb503355673_500x281.gif)

This question will help you see if money is your main priority, or if you care more about the little things in life.

## 9. Are There Any Unhealthy People in Your Life?

![Are There Any Unhealthy People in Your Life?](https://img.allw.mn/content/f4/og/cxpsgqru56bcaa19b491b740315597_500x226.gif)

If there are, then you should kick them out of your life. You don't need them bringing you down.

## 10. Do You like Your Job?

![black and white, person, blond, monochrome, monochrome photography,](https://img.allw.mn/content/dr/dt/e2nuec6m5895856cd8c96569389671_500x247.gif)

If you hate your job, then you need to quit. Just make sure you find a new one that you like better.

## 11. What do You Daydream about?

![facial expression, human action, hair, face, person,](https://img.allw.mn/content/tt/ud/zmig86345895856d21d22650645664_500x242.gif)

If you daydream about marriage, then you should probably focus on dating more often. If your mind always wanders to a certain topic, it's because it's important to you.

## 12. What’s Your Biggest Regret?

![hair, facial expression, face, person, nose,](https://img.allw.mn/content/za/em/gxhetw175895856d796df705956980_500x270.gif)

If your biggest regret is that you never went after your dream job, you still have time to change that.

## 13. How Would You Change Your Life if You Could?

![How Would You Change Your Life if You Could?](https://img.allw.mn/content/oy/v4/gxgny0pc56bcaa1a44ee5579754235_500x282.gif)

If you could change one thing, what would it be? Once you think of it, try to change it.

## 14. What Would You do if You Won the Lottery?

![black and white, person, monochrome photography, monochrome, fashion,](https://img.allw.mn/content/x5/p2/uu5r0i495895856f590dc784769765_500x255.gif)

If you had all the money in the world, what would you do with your time? That can help you figure out where your priorities lie.

## 15. Where do You Want to Live?

![Where do You Want to Live?](https://img.allw.mn/content/a8/tn/vpgq7s7y56bcaa1a0b78c781573599_500x281.gif)

If you want to move to another country, then you should do it. There's nothing keeping you in the place where you grew up.

## 16. Are You Proud of Yourself?

![Are You Proud of Yourself?](https://img.allw.mn/content/ib/wh/iu8mt01y56bcaa1e6d103149481472_500x281.gif)

If you're not proud of yourself yet, think of things you could do to change that.

## 17. What do You Have to Lose?

![hair, image, black hair, photography, lady,](https://img.allw.mn/content/b4/dr/uu3xu2jo5895856f61275268624124_500x264.gif)

If you want to ask out a certain guy or go to a big party, ask yourself what you have to lose. The answer is "nothing," which is why you should go do what you want to do!

The first step to finding happiness is figuring out what changes would enhance your life. Are you able to answer all of these questions?
