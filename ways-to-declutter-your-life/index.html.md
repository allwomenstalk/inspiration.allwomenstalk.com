# 4 Ways to Declutter Your Life for a More Peaceful Existence ...

If you're looking for **ways to declutter your life,** you're in the right place. Every once in awhile you have to get rid of things that make your life messy. Whether it be a bad relationship or toxic friendship. It could also be simple things, like a broken lamp or your old dirty socks. Anyway, if you’re someone who needs a fresh start, keep reading for some great **ways to declutter your life.**

## 1. Remove Yourself from Negative People

There’s nothing worse than having people in your life who do nothing but aggravate you. It could be your boyfriend who always criticizes what you wear or the way you look. How about the friends who talk about you behind your back or take advantage of your kindness? Surround yourself with positive people who value your love in return and people you can trust and depend on. That's one of the best **ways to declutter your life,** so get started today.

## 2. Donate Clothes You Don’t Wear Anymore

We all have clothes we wore only once or twice. Even worse are the clothes that still have the tag on. When you think about it, there are people in this world who are less fortunate and could use those clothes, so pass them along.

## 3. Get Organized

You never know how important staying organized is until you can’t find your keys. Doing a big clean out in your house and car will make life much easier. If you need to, buy extra containers to store stuff you won’t be using anytime soon. Keeping a journal will also help because you can keep track of all the things you need to do or have planned to get done.

## 4. Meditation

This is one of the best ways to get your mind back on track. I would recommend waking up 10 to 15 minutes early to meditate. You will be refreshed and your mind will be re-focused on the things that really matter. You can also take up yoga, which will help eliminate the toxins from your body!

Changing bad habits and the wrong people in your life will make things much simpler and happier. Little by little you can add positivity back into your life! What are some other ways you like to declutter your life?
