# 9 Inspiring Quotes from Lady Gaga to Brighten Your Day ...

Lady Gaga is known for her big heart, so it’s no surprise there is a plethora of inspiring Lady Gaga quotes. For some, it may be hard to look past her larger-than-life hair, the dramatic eyeliner, and over-the-top wardrobe, but behind the glitz and glam, there is an intelligent woman full of some really good advice. So if you’re needing a little something to brighten your day, try reading these inspirational Lady Gaga quotes.

## 1. On Accepting Yourself…

![On Accepting Yourself…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/13201742_3104_400x531.jpg)

Out of all the Lady Gaga quotes, this one is my favorite. This quote is a great reminder that we all have dark spots in our lives. It can be hard to put yourself out there and let yourself shine, because then people are more likely to see the flaws we all desperately try to hide. However, Lady Gaga makes us realize that the fear of our shadows is no reason to avoid the light. Everyone has a past, but it’s important to leave it behind you, just like your shadow.

## 2. On Potential…

![On Potential…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/13201742_3549_536x640.jpg)

I am a firm believer of the phrase “if you can dream it, you can be it.” Gaga expands upon this sentiment in this quote. If you want something badly enough, let it consume you, and often you can make your delusion a reality. Keeping yourself focused on your goals is the best way to achieve them.

## 3. On Not Giving up…

![On Not Giving up…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/13201742_3011_500x662.jpg)

These lyrics from Lady Gaga’s hit “Marry the Night” are inspiration for anyone going through a rough time. If you are feeling down about yourself, life, or a current situation, don’t give up. Just as importantly, be sure you are giving yourself plenty of self-affirmation. We all have a “warrior queen” inside of us, waiting to come to the surface.

## 4. On Insecurities…

![On Insecurities…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/13201742_9804_470x673.jpg)

There are many wonderful and uplifting quotes from Lady Gaga’s The Monster Ball tour, but the one above is my favorite. Unfortunately in life, we will all be told we aren’t good enough to achieve our goals. In this quote, Gaga reminds us of the importance of not listening to those who only want to put us down. Clear the bad out of your life and just focus on the good. And most importantly, remember you ARE a superstar and you WERE born this way!

## 5. On Self-Acceptance…

![On Self-Acceptance…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/13201742_2248_333x500.jpg)

This short and sweet quote is one of my mantras for life. Life is too short to live up to others' expectations. Everyone is an individual, and if you let your uniqueness shine, you are destined to become the best you that you can be. Lady Gaga lives this advice every day. She created her own path in the music industry and has forever changed the game.

## 6. On Being Single…

![On Being Single…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/13201743_0211_600x811.jpg)

This quote is a lyric from Lady Gaga’s song "Gypsy" from her new album ARTPOP. The album is packed full of great quotes, but this one really hit home for me. Being single can be hard, but there is also beauty in it. The song is about living a gypsy life and holding out for that special someone.

## 7. On Feminism…

![On Feminism…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/13201743_2300_600x1011.jpg)

“If you’re a strong female, you don’t need permission” is a quote from Lady Gaga’s song "Scheiße." The song is all about female empowerment! This is something I think we can all get behind. You don’t need to answer to any male in your life. If your boyfriend makes you ask him permission before you do something, it is not a healthy relationship. Stand up for yourself and be empowered – just like Lady Gaga.

## 8. On Love and Hate…

![On Love and Hate…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/13201743_0441_500x700.jpg)

This Lady Gaga quote is one of my all time favorites. I like to think about this quote whenever I’m making decisions. It’s always important to act out of love and what’s good as opposed to doing things out of hate. Practicing this sentiment will make everyone feel better, including yourself.

## 9. On Independence…

![On Independence…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/13201743_7034_400x567.jpg)

Sometimes it feels like everyone has abandoned you and you are on your own. This quote from Lady Gaga helps us remember that that’s okay because you will always have your biggest supporter – yourself. Only you can make your dreams come true so it’s important to build up confidence and independence.

I hope these Lady Gaga quotes have inspired you as much as they inspire me. Gaga is always encouraging her Little Monsters, so these 9 quotes are just the beginning of the inspirational Lady Gaga quotes out there. Do you have a favorite piece of advice from Mother Monster? If so, tell us what it is in the comment section.
