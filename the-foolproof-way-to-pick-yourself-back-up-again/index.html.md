# The Foolproof Way to Pick Yourself up  after a Sad Period ...

Picking yourself back up after a bit of depression or even just a bad day is hard. It takes a lot of mental stimulation, good vibes and you focusing on it. Below, I've got some of the best tips that always help pick me right up!

## 1. Stop Being so Freakin' Hard on Yourself

![Stop Being so Freakin' Hard on Yourself](https://img.allw.mn/content/j6/lh/mceypzsq573692d52dbd3266523733_500x360.gif)

Seriously, it doesn't do anything but hurt you.

## 2. Move It, Move It

![Move It, Move It](https://img.allw.mn/content/eg/lv/kv6w3isa573692d52d389946240368_500x281.gif)

Exercise is huge!

## 3. Find Something to Laugh at

![Find Something to Laugh at](https://img.allw.mn/content/ts/wg/onzi1bbp573692d5df96f896426915_500x281.gif)

There is joy all around you. Promise.

## 4. Treat Yourself to Your Favorites

![Treat Yourself to Your Favorites](https://img.allw.mn/content/fc/ur/zhwjalcn573692d6cd955836999604_500x271.gif)

Ice cream your fave? Treat yo' self!

## 5. Surround Yourself with Animals

![Surround Yourself with Animals](https://img.allw.mn/content/lp/qq/lmj68qy1573692d634788481480006_500x244.gif)

This is what I do. My puppies always bring me joy.

## 6. Do Something Exciting

![Do Something Exciting](https://img.allw.mn/content/rt/d1/gu677bfk573692d5dac05847485181_500x250.gif)

It doesn't have to be big. Go for a bike ride and let go when you go down the hill. Excite yourself!

## 7. Do Something Spontaneous

![Do Something Spontaneous](https://img.allw.mn/content/fm/xr/ipjfqmm7573692d6daf9a425266022_500x302.gif)

Also, make sure you are spontaneous at some point.

## 8. Find a Way to Inspire Yourself

![Find a Way to Inspire Yourself](https://img.allw.mn/content/ne/us/luiat58p573692d6bef25494645144_500x278.gif)

Inspiration is everywhere!

## 9. Focus on the Positive

![Focus on the Positive](https://img.allw.mn/content/dy/hn/bwzz04ye573692d6eb1e9028510021_500x281.gif)

Instead of the negative.

## 10. Surround Yourself with Love

![Surround Yourself with Love](https://img.allw.mn/content/tr/kn/jcbbveqp573692d744d75214345956_500x245.gif)

This is huge. Instead of negative people, surround yourself with love.

## 11. Make a List of Everything You're Grateful for

![Make a List of Everything You're Grateful for](https://img.allw.mn/content/mx/mm/oiqynpc6573692d7cb30b627199636_500x307.gif)

And refer back to the list when you are down.

## 12. Be Honest with Yourself

![Be Honest with Yourself](https://img.allw.mn/content/r1/vz/h75157t8573692d8741dc629453473_500x260.gif)

This is sometimes hard but so necessary.

## 13. Move past the Resistance

![Move past the Resistance](https://img.allw.mn/content/bq/sj/mcyy62h6573692d8512a3002737646_500x281.gif)

Is something holding you back? Push through it!

## 14. Celebrate Your Victories, No Matter How Tiny

![Celebrate Your Victories, No Matter How Tiny](https://img.allw.mn/content/v8/ah/rt1g37q1573692d80cb89254857083_500x281.gif)

Seriously, celebrate any and all victories.

## 15. Disconnect with Electronics

![Disconnect with Electronics](https://img.allw.mn/content/ai/pg/ca1hhdap573692d8a4702040836646_500x283.gif)

This will help remove a lot of the negativity.

## 16. Connect with Nature More

![geological phenomenon, fire, atmosphere of earth, screenshot,](https://img.allw.mn/content/qg/fc/mq4ok3hg58f745f915dae502581604_500x281.gif)

Connect a lot more with all types of nature.

## 17. Channel Your Inner Child

![Channel Your Inner Child](https://img.allw.mn/content/ky/h3/phhbwuc9573692d8bfd56713455603_500x282.gif)

Become a child again!
