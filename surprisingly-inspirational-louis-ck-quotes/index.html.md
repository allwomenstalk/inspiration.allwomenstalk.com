# 7 Surprisingly Inspirational Louis CK Quotes ...

Even though he's a comedian that specializes in dirty, inappropriate humor, there are some pretty inspirational Louis CK quotes. He's a man who tells it like it is, so you know what he's saying is the truth. Plus, he's pretty passionate about everything that he talks about, so you know that he's really thought over his opinions. Whether or not you're a fan of his comedy, we all can agree that the following Louis CK quotes are utterly inspirational:

## 1. Grass Isn't Greener

![Grass Isn't Greener](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ju/u2/xxlk6z0h_500x368.jpg)

People are constantly competing with one another. They want to wear the best clothes, eat the tastiest meals, and date the sexiest men. This is one of the Louis CK quotes that should remind you that your life is pretty darn special. You don't have have to compare it to others in order to feel good about yourself. Life isn't a competition, so stop making it one.

## 2. Learn to Love Life

![Learn to Love Life](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hh/kb/dy5dq8ok_600x399.jpg)

You should never forget how lucky you are to be alive. It's easy to complain about every little thing that happens to you, but start trying to pay more attention to the good stuff. Life is amazing, so stop taking it for granted and learn to appreciate it all.

## 3. The Fun of Failure

![The Fun of Failure](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hj/r7/atow2ysr_600x400.jpg)

Don't be upset when you look back at your old writing or photos and realize how ridiculous you sounded or looked. As you age, you're going to get better and better at your craft. You don't start out by being perfect at anything. If you think that everything you've done is perfect, then you need to rethink your way of thinking.

## 4. Tame Your Texting

![Tame Your Texting](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g9/d8/v7yb6lvn_600x385.jpg)

There's nothing wrong with snapping photos that you can keep for the rest of your life. However, the memories contained in your mind are equally as valuable. Life isn't as fun when you're constantly viewing it through a lens, instead of soaking it all in and ignoring your technology. So calm it with the social media sites.

## 5. Bye Bye Boredom

![Bye Bye Boredom](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j1/i7/jqqp5f67_475x317.jpg)

You're surfing the web, but think of how many millions of different things you could be doing right now if you wanted to. The next time that you're bored, you should get off of the couch and do something exciting. No, you don't have to take a hike up a mountain, but you can start a new craft, learn a new exercise, or whip up a new recipe.

## 6. Silver Linings

![Silver Linings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/od/oq/hoc4a2vc_600x407.jpg)

No one hears about a divorce and is happy about it, but it's not always a sad occasion. Yes, it's sad that the relationship didn't work out, but wouldn't you rather have your friend be happy and single than in a destructive relationship? Happiness is the most important thing, after all.

## 7. No Negativity

![No Negativity](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l2/yj/df640wcf_600x300.jpg)

If someone says nasty things about you, ignore them. There's no reason to pay attention to the rude people that people spit out of their mouths and rant about on Twitter. The world can be harsh, but you need to stay positive. Delete all of the negativity from your life.

Louis CK can be offensive, dirty, and inappropriate, but he's quite intelligent, so you shouldn't judge him before you know how awesome he can be. Are you a fan of Louis CK's standup or his TV show? What's your favorite joke of his?
