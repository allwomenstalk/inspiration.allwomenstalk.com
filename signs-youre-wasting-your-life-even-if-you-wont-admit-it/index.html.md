# 7 Signs You're Wasting Your Life Even if You Won't Admit It ...

If you think that no matter what you do, you still don’t live the life of your dreams, here are a few signs you’re wasting your life, even if you won’t admit it. We all dream big when we are little, but somehow we manage to sabotage ourselves and our happiness. We forget about our plans and we focus our energy on things that don’t even deserve our attention. Most people don’t realize that these harmless routines are the ones that are making them unhappy and preventing them from enjoying a meaningful and fulfilled life. Time is one of the most precious commodities and one of the most important resources anyone possesses. That’s why it’s extremely important to spend it wisely, since you can’t buy it back. Just read on and pay attention to the next warning signs you’re wasting your life, so you will not miss out on life as it’s happening:

## 1. You do Things You Shouldn’t Be Doing

One of the most obvious signs you’re wasting your life is the fact that you’re spending too much time doing things you shouldn’t be doing. How much time do you spend on social networking sites every day, or how much time do you spend playing video games? How are you spending the majority of your life? Does it help you fulfill your dreams and reach all your goals, or do you need to make some changes in your daily routine?

## 2. You Don’t Feed Your Mind

Do those things you do on a daily basis feed your mind? Do they help you grow and become better at what you do? Always focus on learning new things and improving yourself. After all, just like Carol Morgan, professor at Wright State University, said, “Positive challenges in your life will expand your mind, not send it backwards.”

## 3. You Think You Have No Passions

Do you often think that you have no passions? Is there nothing that excites you? Do you often feel uninspired? If that’s the case, then maybe you spend too much time doing the wrong things, since everyone has interests and things they are passionate about. You just need to rediscover yours and then simply do more of that.

## 4. You Have a Lot of Negative Self-Talk

If you often doubt your abilities, if you always tell yourself that you’re not good or capable enough to reach your objectives, then you might be right, because you are the one that stands in the way of fulfilling your dreams. Just like Henry Ford said, “Whether you think you can, or think you can’t… either way, you’re right.”

## 5. You Complain Too Much

Does everyone tell you that you complain too much? Do you often complain about your boss, your partner, your money, your health, your life in general? If your answer is yes, then all you do is exude negative energy, which only makes things worse, since negativity will not help you solve your problems. It just keeps you stuck and prevents you from enjoying the wonderful things around you and living your life to the fullest.

## 6. You Don’t Make Plans

Just like Milton Erickson, the famous hypnotherapist, said, “A goal without a date is just a dream.” While it’s extremely important to live in the present and enjoy your life, you shouldn’t forget to make plans for the future. After all, as Carol Morgan said, “Sometimes you need to look ahead to see where you want to go.”

## 7. You Spend Too Much Time with the Wrong People

Time is precious, so you shouldn’t waste it on people who don’t deserve your attention, who are not making you feel like a better person and who are preventing you from growing. Don’t let them pull you down with them! Surround yourself with positive people who will inspire and encourage you, and who will help you become better at what you do.

Even if those points seem very familiar to you, don’t despair. You can make a lot of changes in your life to reach your goals and fulfill all your dreams. Do you know any other signs you’re wasting your life, even if you often do it without realizing that you are sabotaging yourself? Please share your thoughts with us in the comments section!
