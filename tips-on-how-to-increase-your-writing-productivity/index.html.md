# 7 Tips on How to Increase Your Writing Productivity ...

If you want to learn how to increase your writing productivity then you’ve come to the right place. It’s very hard to be productive and creative when you feel exhausted all the time and when you have a lot of other tasks you need to take care of on a daily basis. In order to increase your productivity, you should learn how to best utilize your allotted writing time. You should know that our body is capable of doing much more than it’s already doing and you can always learn how to make more effective use of it under the right circumstances. Here are a few tips on how to increase your writing productivity that I hope you will find very helpful:

## 1. Time Management

One of the best tips on how to increase your writing productivity I could give you is to tell you to work on improving your time management skills. Just try to create a weekly time-table and make sure you are completely honest about how much time you can dedicate to your writing, without being distracted by all the wonderful things around you. Stick to this time-table as rigorously as you can and you will surely increase your writing productivity.

## 2. Set Deadlines

In order to increase your productivity, try to set some strict and realistic deadlines for yourself, just to be sure that you will finish your work on time. For example, if you are working on a bigger project, you could set the deadline for the day after you anticipate that you will finish it.

## 3. Plan

Before you sit down and start writing, make sure you have a basic idea of what you want to write. Do your research before you start working on that project and plan every little step you will take to finish it. You can plan what are you going to write when you are doing all kinds of other things, like take a shower for example, or go grocery shopping.

## 4. Fast Drafts

If you are feeling inspired but you don’t have the time right now to start working on your writing projects, just take a little piece of paper and write down the most important ideas that came to your mind. Just write a fast draft that you will edit later when you have the time to add some polish to it.

## 5. Write Often

In order to increase your writing productivity, try to write as often as you can and as much as you can. Try to write at least one page every day and think of it as training. This way, your typing speed will increase and your finger muscles will be able to learn all your keys, since most keyboards are different.

## 6. Get Organized

Get organized and keep all your writing stuff in one place. This way, when you need some information, you will know where you can find it without spending too much time and effort looking for it. Just get a notebook or create a new folder and keep all your thoughts in one place.

## 7. Don’t Multitask

I know that you may think that multitasking is what allows you to take care of a lot of things at the same time, but I must tell you that you are wrong. That's because this activity will not only affect your productivity but it will also make you feel even more exhausted. You are more productive when you focus on one task at a time, so working on all kinds of different things at the same time is a total waste of time and effort.

As a writer, it’s very important to be as productive as you can be, since your paycheck depends on this. Do you know any other tips on how someone can increase their writing productivity? Please share your thoughts with us in the comments section!
