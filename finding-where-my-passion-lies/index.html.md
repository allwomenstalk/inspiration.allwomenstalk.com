# Finding Where My Passion Lies ...

**Finding Where My Passion Lies**

This world is filled with options,
So much to do so much to be,
Yet everything has something,
Everyone but me,
I'm always last picked for the team,
Not good at anything,
For the rules to be reversed,
To have just one thing in this world,
Where I find my passions lie,
Sometimes it seems so out of reach,
That I'd rather not just try,
I know it's out there somewhere,
That my talent does exist,
And I'm only getting closer,
With each new cross upon my list.
-e.h

Have you ever thought that somehow or sometime in your life that you are this poem? I have. When you think hard and you try your best to think what would the best thing that you want to do, that you love to do, and wanted to do all your life, that something that your heart would love to do, you too are this poem. As the saying goes, "do what you have to do, until you can do want you want to do." It takes a lot of patience, perseverance and hope and the hardest thing is, this something that you've been longing for so long, is still hard to figure out. Sometimes you don't know if your passion is really for you, or you just have to focus on something else. I guess it's all part of the process, It's probably the road you have to take and go through before you arrive. The hardest thing is, there is no guarantee, no way of knowing, no way when, and you don't know if you passed it already or it's still to come. Just hang in there, if you've missed the train, there is always another trip.
