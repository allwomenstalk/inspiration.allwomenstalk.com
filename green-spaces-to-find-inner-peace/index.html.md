# 7 Green Places to Visit if You Want to Find Inner Peace in Life ...

Looking for some **green spaces to find inner peace**? You are in the right place. Anytime you feel down or sad, a visit to a place that makes you feel better is definitely in order. A garden is the perfect choice because it encourages contemplation and reflection. Here are the best **green spaces to find inner peace.**

## 1. A Botanical Garden

![plant, greenhouse, flora, arecales, botanical garden,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dy/k2/sh9rlgfe59e0e42fec049816101483_600x600.jpg)

A botanical garden is packed with plants that help you feel calm and happy. It's one of the best **green spaces to find inner peace.**

## 2. An Arboretum

![garden, botanical garden, pumpkin, cucurbita, plant,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lj/j2/ux69h86s59e0e399f229a213370667_600x600.jpg)

This is a place where trees and plants are grown, in order to be studied. So you know there's tons of nature here.

## 3. Zoological Gardens

![water, nature, green, waterway, plant,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cs/i1/unn88odz59e0eb09e2dd5076853466_600x600.jpg)

These are found in the very near a zoo and are a nice respite from the smell and noise of the animals.

## 4. Sculpture Gardens

![tree, tourist attraction, outdoor structure, garden, historic site,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n4/v0/ukuxhq0c59e0e4b1d0dbf689253272_600x600.jpg)

When a museum displays pieces of sculpture in an open-air setting, it's usually near plants, flowers or trees. The result is a relaxing place to sit and think.

## 5. College and University Campuses

![nature, reflection, water, tree, sky,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q2/pq/n62bpjna59e0e648d0b62479502785_600x600.jpg)

Colleges and universities like to make their campuses appealing and many have gardens or open spaces that are perfect for reflection.

## 6. Historic Homes or Places of Worship

![home, house, property, cottage, leaf,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cu/mw/l018aw8359e0e68d81f40065614859_600x600.jpg)

During preservation of an historic home, an effort is usually made to recreate the landscaping in the yard. Sometimes these buildings are open to the public and makes them a great place to commune with nature.

## 7. County, State and Federal Parks

![tree, photograph, woody plant, plant, woodland,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zb/fd/eh9513yj59e0e6d2d6b3b155336010_600x600.jpg)

The government controls many open spaces around the country and they are a great place that is natural and undisturbed. You'll love it there!

You might also want to create a community garden to share with others. Even sitting in your own backyard has calming benefits.
