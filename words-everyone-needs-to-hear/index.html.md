# Words Everyone Needs to Hear ...

This may sound odd but there are some **words everyone needs to hear**.

I care about you. I stay up some nights wondering if there are people out there, that need someone to look out for them or someone who needs what I have more than I do. I hope that one day I will be able to do something about that.

I don’t know you, but I hope that you’re doing okay. Those are **words everyone needs to hear**.

I know there are people out there who just need someone to care about them. Surround yourself with people that will.

It’s okay to ask for help. It doesn’t make you any less of a person. It doesn’t make you weak. It makes you human.

It’s okay to want to be loved. You deserve to be. You don’t deserve to be tossed aside or ignored.

If today was bad, I hope that tomorrow will be better.

I hope that you will be able to smile brighter and laugh without any worry every day, even if you haven’t been able to before.

If you are going through a rough patch, remember that it is a patch. It may seem neverending but you will get out of it. In the grand scheme of things, a patch is not that long. Also, remember that having multiple rough patches is normal.

Sometimes in order for things to get better, they have to get a lot worse. Look for the bright moments to help you through.

And most of all, remember that this is a place to grow, share, and inspire. Even if you don’t feel like anyone around you is there for you, this community will be.
