# 7 Ways You Can Change Your Life Right This Second ...

You don't have to wait until you're older to make big life changes. You can make them right here and now, without hesitating. All it takes is a little bit of bravery. Here are a few ways you can change your life right this second:

## 1. Text Your Crush

If you have your crush's number, you can text him right now to ask him out on a date. If you don't have his number, you can search for him on Facebook and add him as a friend. Once he accepts you, then you can chat on the social media site. The Internet makes it easy to access everyone you know, so there's no excuse for putting off a conversation with your crush.

## 2. Send out Job Applications

You can call up your boss right now and quit your job. If you're not ready to do something that extreme, then you can start by sending out job applications. Everything is available online nowadays, which means that you can apply to any job you'd like with a few clicks of your mouse.

## 3. Throw out Your Junk Food

It's time to break your bad habits. If you've become way too obsessed with chocolate, open up your fridge and toss out all of the candy bars that you have (or at least put them in a basket to give to your friends). If you smoke, then it's time to toss out your pack of cigarettes. Force yourself to destroy your bad habits right now.

## 4. Join a Gym

It's time to get a gym membership, so you can work out as often as you'd like. Some gyms allow you to apply for a membership online, so you won't even have to leave your coach to do it. Others will require you to go down to the physical location, but there's nothing wrong with a little car ride. Either way, it's something you can do right now, so don't put it off for any longer.

## 5. Make New Friends

Go to the mall and talk to people you come across in the food court. Add strangers on Facebook and ask them about their hobbies. As long as you play it safe, and remember the possibilities of stranger danger, then there's nothing wrong with trying to make new friends. One of them could change your entire life.

## 6. Admit a Secret

Secrets lead to guilt and frustration. If you've been hiding something that you know you need to admit eventually, then now is the time to come clean. It's hard to be honest, but at the end of the day, you'll feel better about yourself, because you'll know that you did the right thing.

## 7. Buy Something Special

Amazon will help you buy absolutely anything you'd like, so your options are endless. If you've always wanted to learn how to play an instrument, splurge on a guitar. If you've always wanted to learn how to cook, treat yourself to a cookbook. You'll have to wait for your product to arrive, but as soon as you put it in your shopping cart, you'll already feel like your life is changing.

It doesn't take much time change your life. As long as you're brave enough to go for what you want, the world is your oyster. What are you going to do to change your life today?
