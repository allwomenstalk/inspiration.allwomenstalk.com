# Whats Your Chinese Zodiac Animal?

As we enter into the year of the pig, perhaps your own interest in the ancient zodiacs and horoscopes of the Chinese culture has been sparked a little bit? It doesn’t matter what culture or background you come from, it is always fun to know where you might fit in in other cultures too! If you don't know, each year is allocated an animal based on a twelve-year cycle. The Chinese zodiac animals are rat, ox, tiger, rabbit, dragon, snake, horse, sheep, monkey, rooster, dog, and pig. If you want to find **what's your Chinese zodiac animal**, read on.

## 1. Rat

Birth years: 1912, 1924, 1936, 1948, 1960, 1972, 1984, 1996, 2008, 2020

If you are a rat, then you are witty, charming, and clever, extremely good with money and always curious and excited to take on new challenges!

## 2. Ox

Birth years: 1913, 1925, 1937, 1949, 1961, 1973, 1985, 1997, 2009, 2021

You are a true leader of the pack, very detail oriented and hard-working, but someone who your friends and family look to for guidance and authority.

## 3. Tiger

Birth years: 1914, 1926, 1938, 1950, 1962, 1974, 1986, 1998, 2010, 2022

You are charming, ambitious, and warm-hearted. Your special talent is making people feel special and included; you are a true people person.

## 4. Rabbit

Birth years: 1915, 1927, 1939, 1951, 1963, 1975, 1987, 1999, 2011, 2023

You are about as likeable as a person can get! You avoid conflict at all costs and never have a problem compromising for the greater good. You are a dream best friend.

## 5. Dragon

Birth years: 1916, 1928, 1940, 1952, 1964, 1976, 1988, 2000, 2012, 2024

You are absolutely bursting with energy. You live life at a million miles a minute and only those who can keep up with you are the ones who get to stay in your life!

## 6. Snake

Birth years: 1917, 1929, 1941, 1953, 1965, 1977, 1989, 2001, 2013, 2015

You are an incredibly generous person. You will do anything for anyone and never expect something in return. Your main goal in life is to make people happy.

## 7. Horse

Birth years: 1918, 1930, 1942, 1954, 1966, 1978, 1990, 2002, 2014, 2026

You are someone who can be relied and depended on no matter what the situation is. You always seem to know what to do at any given time, and that is an invaluable skill.

## 8. Goat

Birth years: 1919, 1931, 1943, 1955, 1967, 1979, 1991, 2003, 2015, 2027

You tend to let your creative side and imagination run wild, but that is what makes you so lovable. People never know what they are going to get when they spend time with you.

## 9. Monkey

Birth years: 1920, 1932, 1944, 1956, 1968, 1980, 1992, 2004, 2016, 2028

You are someone who is always seeking stimulation, both mental and physical! Being bored is the worst thing you could imagine, so you are always on the move looking to do something.

## 10. Rooster

Birth years: 1921, 1933, 1945, 1957, 1969, 1981, 1993, 2005, 2017, 2029

You are the most practical of all the animals, extremely observant and happy to blend into the background so you can keep tabs on everything else that is going on!

## 11. Dog

Birth years: 1922, 1934, 1946, 1958, 1970, 1982, 1994, 2006, 2018, 2030

You can be a little bit prone to mood swings, but thankfully, the majority of these moods are an upswing rather than a downswing!

## 12. Pig

Birth years: 1923, 1935, 1946, 1959, 1971, 1983, 1995, 2007, 2019, 2031

You are gentle and well mannered, someone who is always seeking to learn new things and add new arrows to their bow.
