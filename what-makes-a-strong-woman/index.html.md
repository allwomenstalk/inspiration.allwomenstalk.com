# What Makes a Strong Woman ?

**What makes a strong woman**?

A strong woman is one who feels deeply and loves fiercely.
Her tears flow just abundantly as her laughter. She is not afraid to be afraid.

A strong woman is both soft and powerful. She takes compliments to heart and treats herself like the queen that she is.

A strong woman is equally visionary and decisive. She is able to hope when things look hopeless. She is both practical and spiritual. A strong woman in her essence is a gift to all the world. So **what makes a strong woman**?

To all the strong women in the world, who strive and stand for themselves, who don't care for what other would say or do, the world needs you. A woman who doesn't need anybody's validation is a fierce one. Struggles and challenges may hit her but she would hit it back with a mighty punch. Being a strong woman doesn't mean you don't have problems, it is you strengthen yourself with faith, do the right things, do your best, be your best, and be your own superhero. Being strong doesn't come easy, sometimes you get knocked down, hit rock bottom, are broken-hearted, and struggle like never before, but you get back up, stand tall, kick harder than ever. Your scars prove that you are stronger than the battle you've been fighting and come back stronger. You tackle responsibility with strength and fire in your soul. A strong woman doesn't say it, she gets things done. She turns her mistakes into lessons, and learns well from them, she knows not to take the same path again, even if sometimes it feels inviting for her. And when you're that strong, people can tell, not the other way around.
