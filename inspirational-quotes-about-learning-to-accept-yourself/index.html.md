# 7 Inspirational Quotes about Learning to Accept Yourself ...

We all have insecurities that we let ruin our lives, which is why it's healthy to read quotes about learning to accept yourself. You're never going to be perfect, but you are always going to be a human that has value. It's impossible to look flawless, but it's possible to feel like you're beautiful inside and out. Here are a few quotes about learning to accept yourself that you should reread when you're feeling down:

## 1. There's Beauty in Being Yourself

![There's Beauty in Being Yourself](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wc/je/cewn5xp4_600x600.jpg)

You shouldn't strive to make other people love you, because everyone has their own, personal opinion of what beauty is. Since everyone is so different, it's impossible to please them all. Instead, you should aim to please yourself. This is one of the most helpful quotes about learning to accept yourself, because it pushes you to focus on your own needs.

## 2. Accept All Your Insecurities

![Accept All Your Insecurities](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l1/nm/uchbdqkm_600x462.jpg)

If you're trying to make peace with yourself, you have to accept all of your flaws, and not just your positive traits. Even if you hate certain aspects about yourself, you have to learn to accept having them. You can't change them, so then there's no point in stressing over them. Accept that they're a part of who you are.

## 3. Become Comfortable

![Become Comfortable](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/om/uz/wi2vuggl_567x472.jpg)

If you're happy with who you are, then you'll stop caring about what other people think of you. If they say you're too skinny or heavy, you won't bat an eye, because you think you're perfect just the way you are. It doesn't matter what they think, because you know they're wrong.

## 4. Embrace Everything You Have

![Embrace Everything You Have](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pm/m8/shq5cbrw_458x480.jpg)

Your body belongs to you, and it isn't going anywhere. You're stuck with it, so you might as well appreciate it for all of the amazing things it does for you. Even if you don't like what you look like, it's incredible that your body keeps your blood pumping and your heart beating. Be thankful for it.

## 5. Invincibility Comes with Acceptance

![Invincibility Comes with Acceptance](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/io/a5/anbsvv9t_570x570.jpg)

You can't get hurt by others when you are as confident as can be. Before that can happen, you have to figure out who you are. It sounds silly, but you might not know yourself as well as you think you do. Take some time to really think about your likes, passions, and dreams. Then you can learn to accept yourself.

## 6. Others Love You for You

![Others Love You for You](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ns/jq/o8g2ddqo_403x403.jpg)

Believe it or not, you have friends and family who love you unconditionally. That's why you have to treat yourself kindly. If you don't, then you'll end up miserable, which can negatively impact those around you. You're an amazing person that can achieve amazing things, so don't forget it.

## 7. Be Fearless of Flaws

![Be Fearless of Flaws](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pq/rs/dlwahdyx_567x481.jpg)

You won't cringe when someone comments on a flaw of yours if you've already learned to accept it. You've thought about it more than they have, so there's nothing they can say that will hurt you. Once you accept yourself for who you are, the world will seem a much more beautiful place.

Accepting yourself is an important step to achieving peace. If you're always criticizing yourself and worrying about your flaws, you're going to forget about how amazing you are. It's important to remember that you're a special gal, no matter what your mirror tells you. What's your favorite thing about yourself?
