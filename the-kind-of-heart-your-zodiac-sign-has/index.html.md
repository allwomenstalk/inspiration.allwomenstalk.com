# The Kind of Heart Your Zodiac Sign Has ...

Something that I find pretty incredible about the human race in general is that, whilst we might all have the same biological hearts, it is amazing how differently our hearts can react to the world in an emotional sense! Sure, they all beat and pump blood around our bodies, but when it comes to the psychological nature of the heart of the feelings that it evokes for you personally, even identical twins can be worlds apart! Here is **the kind of heart that you have according to your zodiac sign**.

## 1. Aries

You have a hardened heart. You have been treated badly in the past and you are doing everything that you can in order to not feel that way again.

## 2. Taurus

You have a stubborn heart. Once someone makes their way into it, you are as loving as they come, but people have to jump through hoops to make it first!

## 3. Gemini

You have a heavy heart. You are extremely sensitive to everything that happens around you and in the wider world, which often leads you to feeling too deeply and caring too much!

## 4. Cancer

You have a soft heart, you can’t be mean to someone for real and you will always find time to help people out when they need it!

## 5. Leo

You have a guarded heart. You know that relationships can fall apart at the drop of a hat, so you are always unsure about whether to progress with a connection or not.

## 6. Virgo

You have a cautious heart. You know all too well what happens when people break it, and you are very careful about putting yourself in situations where that might happen.

## 7. Libra

You have an old-fashioned heart. You like to be spoiled and treated like a princess and your idea of happiness is living happily ever after.

## 8. Scorpio

You have a warm heart, a heart that is open to absolutely everyone. It is impossible for you to make any enemies.

## 9. Sagittarius

You have a damaged heart. It might feel really bad right now but just know that there is always a light at the end of the tunnel!

## 10. Capricorn

You have an icy heart, but that doesn’t mean that it can’t be melted by the people that mean a lot to you!

## 11. Aquarius

You have a huge heart, the biggest of anyone you know, there isn’t a favour too small or too big to do for someone as far as you are concerned. Your capacity for love in both a platonic and romantic sense is infinite.

## 12. Pisces

You have a strong heart. You aren’t afraid to throw caution to the wind and take risks with love because you know that you can through anything if it goes south.
