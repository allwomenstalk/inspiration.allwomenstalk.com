# 5 Best Inspirational Quotes of All Time ...

Are you looking for the **best inspirational quotes of all time**?
Words are something that we live by. Words can be quite powerful and also inspirational as they have the capability to uplift our mood and remove us from negativity. There are so many insightful quotes that people turn to at difficult times in order to shed some wisdom, light, and inspiration into their lives. When hard times come, we tend to place negativity on them but that’s not always the case. For example, failure. Failure can be seen as a way to challenge oneself and also be followed by the notion, “if you’ve never failed you’ve never tried.” So here are five of the **best inspirational quotes of all time** to turn to and live by that will help brighten up your day.

## 1. “without Music Life Would Be a Mistake.”- Fredrich Nietzche

Music is such a powerful tool and escape. Its functionality is limitless as it is used for so many things such as easy listening, therapy, yoga, anxiety, background music, entertainment, to sleep etc. This quote speaks for itself, because if music weren’t in our lives then life wouldn’t be the same. Music is one of those things that people depend on. For example, music maintains a strong relationship with exercise. It makes it more enjoyable. At the end of the day, music maintains a strong linkage with many things that makes it a necessity. This is one of my favorite **best inspirational quotes of all time**.

## 2. “Life Can Only Be Understood Backwards but It Must Be Lived Forwards”- Soren Kierkegaard

One of the many struggles that people face is focusing on their past. It is hard to move on from our past, but in order to live a happy life, it is the only way to move forward. We must take things day-by-day and live in the present moment. If we live in the past, then essentially we can’t enjoy the present. When something major happens in our lives and it affects us deeply, of course, it can be hard to move on. But it can be achieved. It just takes time and a little concrete brain reconfiguration. Meaning putting yourself in the right mindset.

## 3. “if You Can’t Fly then Run. if You Can’t Run then Walk. if You Can’t Walk then Crawl. but Whatever You do, You Have to Keep Moving Forward”- Martin Luther King Jr

This quote is quite powerful as it is motivational and invigorating. It is saying that you ought to never give up even when life becomes a struggle. There is always a solution to a problem and that despite the circumstances, continue to live life to the fullest. Those who live the best lives are those who pull through in hard times and those who smile even when it’s hard to. It’s always crucial to keep moving forward and not look at every downfall as a mishap.

## 4. “the More Real You Get, the More Unreal the World Gets.”- John Lennon

Each day when you continue to be your true self and love yourself then the world becomes a better place. When you are feeling good, loving your life and living out your dreams then the world becomes a utopia. When you bring meaning to your life then life becomes more meaningful and worth living. When you live each day by setting out what you want to achieve, then the world becomes your platform and it feels like a dream, yet it is more real than you ever expected.

## 5. “ the Solution to Sadness is Not to Look at It as a Negative. the Solution to Sadness is Understanding It. the Solution to Sadness is Listening to It and Walking It out of Your Heart the Same Way That It Walked in. You Can’t Expect It to Overtake You by Walking in, and to Leave by Disappearing. It Took Steps in. It Needs to Take Steps out.” – Najwa Zebian

For anyone who has ever suffered depression or anxiety, this quote will be very inspiring and helpful. Listening to your thoughts and feelings without judging them and understanding them is important. Sadness is widespread. We cope with it every day with loved ones and friends surrounding us. At times, it’s hidden and sometimes, it’s evident. What we ought to do is adjust what “learning” means, and this way we can progress from a set state of mind to a developed mindset. Nobody naturally chooses to be in a state of sadness. Everyone’s goal in life is to be happy. It is never accurate to suppose that someone wants to dwell on his or her sadness. But just know that sadness is not permanent.
